Armature Works Private Events

4.9★ (111 reviews)

Located in the heart of Tampa's vibrant Armature Works district, this versatile event space offers a unique and engaging team building experience. With its industrial-chic aesthetic and flexible layout, Armature Works Private Events provides the perfect backdrop for groups to collaborate, bond, and push their limits in a memorable and fun way.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of team-focused activities, from scavenger hunts that encourage exploration and problem-solving to high-energy games that get everyone moving and interacting. The attentive staff is on hand to facilitate the experience and ensure everything runs smoothly, allowing your team to focus on the task at hand and build lasting connections.

Who It's For

Armature Works is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to break out of the traditional meeting room setting and try something more dynamic and immersive. The variety of activities and open, loft-like atmosphere make it a great fit for both corporate retreats and social gatherings where colleagues can let loose and challenge themselves together.
