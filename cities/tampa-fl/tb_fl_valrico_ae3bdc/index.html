The Pottery Patch

4.6★ (144 reviews)

Located in the charming city of Valrico, Florida, The Pottery Patch offers a truly unique and engaging team building experience. Dive into the world of pottery-making, where creativity and collaboration come together to craft one-of-a-kind masterpieces.

What to Expect

During a visit to The Pottery Patch, your team will be guided through the entire pottery-making process, from shaping the clay to glazing the finished pieces. Participants will work together to bring their creative visions to life, fostering a sense of camaraderie and problem-solving skills.

Who It's For

The Pottery Patch is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking for a hands-on, artistic team building activity. The shared experience of creating pottery helps to break down barriers and encourages team members to connect in a fun and relaxed setting.
