Zero Latency VR Tampa

5.0★ (1502 reviews)

Zero Latency VR Tampa transforms team building through free-roam virtual reality experiences that get groups moving together in digital worlds. Teams strap on wireless VR headsets and work collaboratively through zombie apocalypses, space missions, and other immersive scenarios that demand communication and coordination. The Tampa location delivers cutting-edge VR technology that creates shared adventures impossible in traditional meeting rooms.

What to Expect

Your team enters a warehouse-style arena equipped with wireless VR gear and backpack computers, allowing complete freedom of movement within the virtual space. Groups of up to 8 players navigate missions together, seeing and interacting with teammates as avatars while tackling objectives that require strategy, teamwork, and quick thinking. Sessions typically run 30-45 minutes with briefings before and debriefing discussions after the virtual experience.

Who It's For

Ideal for tech-forward teams of 6-16 people who want an energizing break from conventional team activities. The physically engaging, problem-solving nature works especially well for younger workforces and groups comfortable with gaming technology.
