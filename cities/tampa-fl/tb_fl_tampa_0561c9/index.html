The Vault

4.6★ (217 reviews)

The Vault in Tampa, FL is a one-of-a-kind event space that doubles as a premier team building destination. Housed in a historic 1920s bank building, this unique venue blends old-world charm with modern amenities, creating an unforgettable backdrop for group activities and corporate gatherings.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of interactive team building experiences at The Vault, from high-energy scavenger hunts that send participants racing through the building, to collaborative escape room challenges that require critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Professional facilitators are on hand to ensure a smooth, engaging process from start to finish.

Who It's For

The Vault's versatile space and tailored team building programs make it an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40) looking to foster camaraderie, improve communication, and ignite a little healthy competition. The shared experience of conquering a challenge together is a surefire way to break down silos and bring colleagues closer.
