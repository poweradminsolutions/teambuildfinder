ESC of Tampa Bay

4.4★ (8 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Tampa, ESC of Tampa Bay is a premier destination for team building experiences. Their facilitated workshops and immersive retreats provide a dynamic and engaging environment where coworkers can bond, communicate, and push themselves to new heights.

What to Expect

When you visit ESC, your team will dive into thoughtfully designed activities that encourage collaboration, problem-solving, and friendly competition. From thrilling escape rooms to outdoor team challenges, the experienced facilitators ensure a seamless and memorable experience for all participants.

Who It's For

ESC's team building offerings are perfect for mid-size to large groups (15-50 people) looking to strengthen workplace relationships and improve overall team dynamics. Whether you're planning a company off-site, a department retreat, or a leadership development session, the versatile event spaces and customizable programs make ESC an ideal choice.
