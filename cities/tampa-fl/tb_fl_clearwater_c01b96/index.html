MCS Clay Studios

4.9★ (200 reviews)

MCS Clay Studios in Clearwater, FL provides a hands-on creative experience perfect for team building. Groups can explore their artistic side by designing and crafting their own pottery pieces under the guidance of experienced instructors.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be welcomed into the light-filled art studio and provided with all the necessary materials and tools. After a brief introduction to pottery techniques, everyone will get to work shaping, sculpting, and decorating their own ceramic creations. The collaborative process encourages communication, problem-solving, and a healthy sense of friendly competition.

Who It's For

These pottery workshops are ideal for mid-sized teams (10-25 people) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The relaxed, supportive environment helps colleagues from different departments connect in a memorable way, making it a great choice for company offsites or employee appreciation events.
