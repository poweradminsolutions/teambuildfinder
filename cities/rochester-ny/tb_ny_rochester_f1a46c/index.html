ARTISANworks

4.7★ (818 reviews)

ARTISANworks transforms team building into an immersive creative experience within a converted factory space filled with multidisciplinary art installations. This Rochester venue combines hands-on art workshops with the inspiring backdrop of a working art gallery, allowing teams to create together while surrounded by rotating exhibitions. Groups can choose from guided art-making sessions or explore custom creative challenges designed to spark collaboration.

What to Expect

Teams participate in structured art workshops where they work on collaborative pieces or individual projects under professional artist guidance. The experience typically includes a brief tour of the gallery spaces to inspire creativity, followed by hands-on art making using various media depending on the chosen workshop. Sessions conclude with a group reflection where teams discuss their creative process and share their finished works.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and groups seeking an alternative to traditional team building activities. The artistic environment naturally encourages open-minded collaboration and works especially well for teams wanting to tap into different communication styles beyond verbal discussion.
