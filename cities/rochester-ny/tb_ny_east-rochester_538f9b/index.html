Total Sports Experience

4.6★ (158 reviews)

Located in the heart of East Rochester, New York, Total Sports Experience is a premier destination for team building activities. With a diverse range of sports facilities and specialized programming, this dynamic complex offers an unparalleled opportunity for groups to bond, challenge themselves, and cultivate camaraderie.

What to Expect

When your team visits Total Sports Experience, they'll be immersed in a high-energy, interactive environment. From competitive sports tournaments to facilitated workshops, the experienced staff will guide your group through engaging activities tailored to your objectives. Whether your team takes the field for a heated soccer match or tackles a collaborative escape room challenge, the shared experiences will foster deeper connections and stronger teamwork.

Who It's For

Total Sports Experience is ideal for mid-size teams (15-40 participants) seeking a physical, competitive team building experience. The variety of sports and team-focused challenges make it a great choice for companies looking to build morale, improve communication, and encourage cross-departmental collaboration.
