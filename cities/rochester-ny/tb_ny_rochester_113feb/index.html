Nut House Escape Rooms

4.9★ (254 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Rochester, NY, Nut House Escape Rooms offers a thrilling team building experience like no other. Their expertly-designed escape rooms challenge groups to put their problem-solving skills to the test as they race against the clock to uncover clues and solve mind-bending puzzles.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be immersed in a high-intensity, story-driven scenario where collaboration and critical thinking are key. With 60 minutes on the clock, you'll need to work together to search for hidden items, decipher codes, and ultimately make your daring escape before time runs out.

Who It's For

Nut House Escape Rooms is perfect for mid-sized teams (10-30 people) seeking a unique, adrenaline-fueled way to bond and build trust. The shared challenge of outsmarting the room creates an unforgettable experience that's sure to bring your group closer together.
