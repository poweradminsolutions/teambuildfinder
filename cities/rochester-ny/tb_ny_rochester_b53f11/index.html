BKLYN enRoc Venue & Party rental

Located in the creative hub of Rochester, NY, BKLYN enRoc Venue & Party Rental offers an array of immersive team building experiences. What to Expect

When you book an event at BKLYN enRoc, your team will dive headfirst into a hands-on activity tailored to your goals. Whether it's sculpting clay, weaving textiles, or engineering unique inventions, the experienced facilitators guide participants through the creative process from start to finish, fostering collaboration and problem-solving along the way.

Who It's For

BKLYN enRoc's team building activities are perfect for mid-size groups (15-40 people) looking to strengthen interpersonal connections and ignite their innovative spirit. The shared creative challenge allows coworkers to break down barriers and get to know each other on a deeper level, setting the stage for more effective collaboration back in the office.
