Roc Artists Open Market at Innovation Square

4.9★ (8 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Rochester, New York, the Roc Artists Open Market at Innovation Square is a vibrant hub where creativity and community converge. This unique team-building destination offers a one-of-a-kind experience, blending art workshops, craft workshops, and live music performances into an engaging and immersive environment.

What to Expect

When you arrive, you'll be greeted by a bustling marketplace showcasing the work of local artists and artisans. Your group will have the opportunity to participate in hands-on workshops, where you'll learn new skills and techniques, whether it's painting, pottery, or even drumming. The lively atmosphere and interactive nature of the activities encourage collaboration, communication, and a shared sense of accomplishment.

Who It's For

The Roc Artists Open Market is an ideal team-building destination for groups of 15 to 40 people seeking a dynamic and creative experience. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, a team-building event, or simply a day of exploration and bonding, this market offers a unique and memorable way to bring your group together.
