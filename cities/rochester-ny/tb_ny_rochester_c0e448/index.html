Rochester Pedal Tours

4.8★ (381 reviews)

Rochester Pedal Tours transforms team building into an active adventure through Rochester's scenic waterfront and historic neighborhoods on multi-person pedal-powered vehicles. These guided tours blend light physical activity with local sightseeing, creating a relaxed environment where colleagues can chat and collaborate while exploring the city together.

What to Expect

Teams climb aboard large pedal bikes that accommodate multiple riders working together to power the vehicle while a guide handles steering and shares local insights. The pace is leisurely and conversation-friendly, with stops at Rochester landmarks and photo opportunities along scenic routes that typically last 90 minutes to two hours.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-16 people who want something active but not intensely competitive. The shared pedaling effort naturally encourages teamwork while the casual pace keeps things inclusive for all fitness levels.
