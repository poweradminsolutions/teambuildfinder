Serendipity Labs - Rochester Innovation Square

5.0★ (57 reviews)

Serendipity Labs in Rochester, New York is a dynamic coworking space that also specializes in curated team building activities. Their facilitated workshops are designed to foster collaboration, problem-solving, and camaraderie among groups of all sizes.

What to Expect

When you book a team building session at Serendipity Labs, you can expect a structured, interactive experience led by skilled facilitators. Activities might include escape room-style challenges, design thinking exercises, or outdoor teamwork challenges - all aimed at strengthening communication and leadership within your group.

Who It's For

Serendipity Labs' team building offerings are well-suited for mid-sized groups (15-40 people) looking to break out of the conference room and try something more engaging. The varied activities cater to both competitive and creative teams, making it a versatile choice for corporate off-sites, department retreats, or any occasion that calls for shared problem-solving and fun.
