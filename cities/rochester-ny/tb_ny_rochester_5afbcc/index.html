Clubhouse Fun Center

4.5★ (1489 reviews)

Clubhouse Fun Center transforms traditional team outings with its multi-activity approach that gets groups moving between 36 holes of themed miniature golf, competitive go-kart racing, and arcade challenges. This Rochester venue excels at creating natural conversation starters as colleagues navigate putting obstacles together, then shift gears to high-energy kart racing that reveals competitive sides and builds camaraderie through shared thrills.

What to Expect

Groups typically rotate through stations, spending 30-45 minutes on mini-golf where teams can split into foursomes and tackle the elaborate course featuring tree house themes and creative obstacles. The go-kart experience puts individuals behind the wheel for timed races, creating friendly competition and plenty of post-race storytelling. Arcade games provide a casual wind-down option where teammates can pair up or compete in smaller clusters.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 12-30 people who want variety and movement rather than sitting around a conference table. The mix of individual and small-group activities works especially well for departments that don't interact daily, as the informal setting and shared experiences naturally break down workplace hierarchies.
