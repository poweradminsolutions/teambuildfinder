Arbor Midtown

4.7★ (45 reviews)

Situated in the heart of Rochester, New York, Arbor Midtown offers a unique and inspiring setting for team building activities. From dynamic art workshops to energetic music and drumming sessions, this versatile venue empowers groups to tap into their creativity and foster deeper connections.

What to Expect

At Arbor Midtown, teams dive into hands-on experiences that challenge them to work collaboratively. Art workshops guide participants through painting, sculpting, or printmaking projects, while the music and drumming activities get everyone moving and rhythmically engaged. The expert instructors ensure the activities are structured for maximum impact, with ample opportunities for teamwork, communication, and personal expression.

Who It's For

Arbor Midtown's team building offerings are perfect for mid-size groups (15-40 participants) looking to break out of the traditional corporate mold. Whether planning a company retreat, offsite, or just a fun team outing, the artistic and musical experiences here cultivate a lively, energetic atmosphere that brings colleagues closer together.
