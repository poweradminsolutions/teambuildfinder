The Lodge at Shadow Hill

4.7★ (43 reviews)

The Lodge at Shadow Hill in Ontario, New York provides a serene and scenic setting for impactful team building experiences. With facilitated workshops and customized retreats, groups can step away from the office and dive into meaningful activities that foster connection, communication, and collaboration.

What to Expect

At the Lodge, team building agendas are crafted to meet the unique needs of each group. Facilitators guide participants through a blend of interactive exercises, outdoor challenges, and reflective discussions designed to strengthen interpersonal bonds and problem-solving skills.

Who It's For

The Lodge's team building programs work well for mid-size to large groups (20-50 people) seeking an immersive, off-site experience. The beautiful natural surroundings and carefully curated activities make this an ideal choice for companies, nonprofits, or other organizations looking to level up their team's cohesion and performance.
