TreeTrails Adventures Trevose

4.8★ (206 reviews)

TreeTrails Adventures in Trevose, PA offers an exhilarating team building experience that will have your group working together to conquer a high-ropes obstacle course set amid the treetops. With a variety of challenge levels, this hands-on activity is sure to push your team's limits and build camaraderie in the great outdoors.

What to Expect

When you arrive at TreeTrails, your group will be outfitted with safety gear and guided through a training session before embarking on the aerial course. Working together, team members will navigate a series of suspended bridges, zip lines, and other obstacles high above the ground, building trust and communication along the way.

Who It's For

This team building activity is well-suited for mid-sized groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds and boost morale through a shared physical challenge. The competitive yet supportive environment encourages participants to step outside their comfort zones and celebrate small victories as a team.
