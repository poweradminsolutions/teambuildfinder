The East Allentown Fire Co. Social Hall in Allentown, PA is a versatile event space that doubles as an excellent venue for team building activities. What to Expect

Groups can expect a tailored, hands-on itinerary at the East Allentown Fire Co. Social Hall. The variety of programming and flexible layout make it suitable for a wide range of corporate events, from departmental retreats to all-hands meetings that require a little extra team spirit.
