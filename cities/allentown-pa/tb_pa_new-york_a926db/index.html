Throwback Fitness

5.0★ (362 reviews)

Throwback Fitness in New York, NY offers a delightful twist on traditional team building activities. Their classes tap into the nostalgic fun of classic playground games, infusing friendly competition and high-energy collaboration into a dynamic fitness experience.

What to Expect

When your group visits Throwback Fitness, you'll find yourself transported back to the carefree days of recess. Instructors lead the team through a series of playful games and challenges, from dodgeball and relay races to cooperative obstacle courses. The activities are designed to get hearts pumping and bodies moving in a supportive, inclusive environment.

Who It's For

Throwback Fitness is perfect for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to bond over shared laughter and a little friendly rivalry. The upbeat, lighthearted atmosphere is ideal for breaking down barriers and building trust between colleagues from different departments.
