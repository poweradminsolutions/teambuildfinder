Da Vinci Science Center

4.4★ (1563 reviews)

Da Vinci Science Center transforms team dynamics through hands-on scientific exploration in their modern Allentown facility. Groups engage with interactive exhibits and participate in guided experiments that require collaboration and creative problem-solving. The two-story space provides an engaging backdrop where colleagues discover new perspectives while tackling science challenges together.

What to Expect

Your team will rotate through facilitated workshop stations where they conduct actual experiments and work with innovative exhibits. Activities might include engineering challenges, physics demonstrations, or collaborative problem-solving exercises using the center's interactive displays. Each session is guided by science educators who tailor the experience to your group's size and objectives.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 20-50 who want something more intellectually stimulating than typical team building fare. The hands-on science approach works particularly well for groups that include analytical thinkers, engineers, or anyone who enjoys discovering how things work.
