Urban Air Go-Kart

2.9★ (13 reviews)

Trexlertown's Urban Air Go-Kart offers a thrilling team building experience on an indoor racing track. Drivers compete head-to-head in high-speed electric karts, putting their skills and strategy to the test in a heart-pounding challenge that builds camaraderie and competitive spirit.

What to Expect

Groups start with a brief safety orientation before taking to the track for 10-15 minutes of intense racing. Teammates can work together to devise passing maneuvers and block rivals, or battle it out for the fastest lap times. The activity culminates in an awards ceremony to celebrate the top finishers.

Who It's For

Urban Air is perfect for mid-sized teams (15-30 people) looking to bond over an adrenaline-fueled activity. The combination of speed, strategy, and shared triumph creates an experience that breaks down barriers and gets colleagues working in sync like never before.
