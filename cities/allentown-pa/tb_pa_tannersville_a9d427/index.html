Camelback Ziplining

3.5★ (15 reviews)

Camelback Ziplining in Tannersville, PA provides an exhilarating aerial adventure perfect for team building. Soar through the scenic Pocono Mountains on a series of high-speed ziplines, challenging your group to work together as they conquer this shared adrenaline-fueled experience.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will receive a comprehensive safety briefing before being outfitted with top-of-the-line gear. You'll then ascend to the first launch platform and take turns zipping through the treetops, cheering each other on as you fly past breathtaking vistas. The course features multiple lines of varying lengths and difficulties, ensuring an engaging experience for adventurers of all skill levels.

Who It's For

Camelback Ziplining is a fantastic choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking a unique, high-energy team building activity. The shared thrill of gliding through the open air helps break down barriers and foster camaraderie in a way that traditional indoor team exercises simply can't match.
