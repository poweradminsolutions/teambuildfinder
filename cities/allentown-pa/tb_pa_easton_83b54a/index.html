Crayola Experience

4.2★ (9423 reviews)

Crayola Experience transforms team building into a hands-on art adventure where colleagues collaborate on creative projects using interactive stations and colorful materials. This Easton attraction goes beyond typical corporate activities by engaging teams in activities like creating custom crayons, designing digital coloring pages, and working together on large-scale art installations. The warehouse setting provides ample space for groups to spread out while still fostering collaboration through shared creative challenges.

What to Expect

Teams rotate through interactive art stations where they might design and wrap their own crayons, collaborate on digital art projects, or work together to complete colorful murals and sculptures. Groups typically spend 2-3 hours exploring different creative activities, with facilitators available to guide team challenges that require cooperation and communication. The experience culminates with teams presenting their collaborative creations to each other.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and departments looking to break out of traditional meeting rooms with something genuinely different. Works particularly well for groups who want to tap into their artistic side while building stronger working relationships through collaborative projects.
