Bellina Alimentari

4.6★ (1901 reviews)

Bellina Alimentari transforms team bonding into hands-on culinary adventures through private cooking classes and wine tastings in their airy Atlanta space. Groups learn to craft authentic Italian dishes from scratch while exploring curated wine pairings. The market setting adds authenticity—teams can browse imported ingredients and artisanal products before diving into their cooking experience.

What to Expect

Your group gathers in the teaching kitchen where an instructor guides everyone through preparing traditional Italian dishes like handmade pasta or regional specialties. Teams work together at stations, learning techniques while sampling wines that complement each course. The session concludes with everyone sharing the meal they've created together.

Who It's For

Ideal for food-loving teams of 12-25 people who want something more engaging than typical corporate events. The collaborative cooking format naturally encourages conversation and teamwork without forced icebreakers.
