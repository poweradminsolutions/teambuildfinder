Just Kiln' Time

4.4★ (136 reviews)

Just Kiln' Time in Marietta, GA, is a one-of-a-kind destination for creative team building experiences. Their pottery workshops and instructor-led activities provide a hands-on, interactive way for groups to bond, learn new skills, and unleash their inner artists.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by friendly staff who will guide you through the studio and explain the day's creative challenges. Depending on the package, you might try your hand at throwing pottery on the wheel, decorating pre-made pieces, or collaborating on a group art project. The structured, yet relaxed format encourages teamwork and artistic expression.

Who It's For

Just Kiln' Time is ideal for teams of 10-30 seeking an engaging, out-of-the-box team building experience. The pottery-making activities work well for corporate events, birthday celebrations, and other group outings where colleagues can get to know each other in a fun, low-pressure setting.
