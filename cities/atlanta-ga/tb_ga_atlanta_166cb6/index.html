Peters Street Station

4.8★ (84 reviews) 2-4 hours

Nestled in the heart of Atlanta, Peters Street Station offers a premier team building experience that combines a historic industrial setting with expertly facilitated workshops and retreats. This unique venue provides the perfect canvas for groups to come together, challenge themselves, and forge stronger bonds.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a seamless and engaging experience at Peters Street Station. The team will work closely with you to design a customized program, whether it's a high-energy escape room challenge, a collaborative problem-solving workshop, or a reflective offsite retreat. Participants will be immersed in a dynamic environment that encourages creativity, communication, and camaraderie.

Who It's For

Peters Street Station is an ideal choice for mid-to-large sized teams (15-50 people) looking to strengthen their interpersonal dynamics and foster a greater sense of cohesion. The versatile space and carefully curated activities make it a standout option for corporate offsites, departmental retreats, and team-building days that leave a lasting impact.
