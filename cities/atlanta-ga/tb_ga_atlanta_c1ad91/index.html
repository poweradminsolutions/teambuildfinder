City Winery Atlanta

4.2★ (2897 reviews)

City Winery Atlanta transforms corporate gatherings into sophisticated experiences that blend wine education with interactive entertainment. Located in the trendy Ponce City Market, this intimate venue combines guided tastings of their on-site wine production with engaging trivia competitions and live music performances. The upscale yet relaxed atmosphere encourages natural conversation while teams explore different wine varietals and participate in knowledge-based challenges.

What to Expect

Your group will start with a guided tasting session featuring wines produced on-site, learning about varietals, production methods, and flavor profiles from knowledgeable staff. Teams then participate in wine-themed trivia or interactive games that test both wine knowledge and general topics, often accompanied by live acoustic performances that create an energetic backdrop. The experience includes light bites from their New American menu to complement the tastings.

Who It's For

Ideal for professional teams of 12-30 people who appreciate a more refined team building approach. The sophisticated setting works particularly well for client entertainment, executive retreats, or celebrating team milestones where you want to impress while building connections.
