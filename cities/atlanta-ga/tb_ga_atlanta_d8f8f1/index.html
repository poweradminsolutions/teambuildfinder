Callanwolde Fine Arts Center

4.6★ (660 reviews)

Tucked away in a historic Atlanta mansion, Callanwolde Fine Arts Center offers a truly unique team building experience. From guided art workshops to facilitated group activities, this vibrant community arts hub provides an inspiring backdrop to bring your team together.

What to Expect

Groups can dive into hands-on projects like painting, pottery, or printmaking, with expert instructors on hand to provide guidance and encourage creative collaboration. Alternatively, Callanwolde's beautiful grounds set the stage for team-oriented challenges that get everyone engaged and communicating.

Who It's For

Callanwolde's diverse offerings cater to teams of all sizes and interests, whether you're looking to foster camaraderie through a shared creative endeavor or strengthen problem-solving skills through interactive games and activities. The center's casual, supportive atmosphere makes it an excellent choice for corporate offsites, nonprofit retreats, or any group seeking to bond over a unique shared experience.
