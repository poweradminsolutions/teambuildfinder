Chattahoochee Nature Center

4.7★ (2003 reviews)

Teams can explore 127 acres of protected wilderness in Roswell while participating in guided nature walks or hands-on environmental restoration projects. The center combines outdoor education with meaningful conservation work, letting groups bond over trail maintenance, habitat restoration, or wildlife observation activities that leave a lasting positive impact.

What to Expect

Groups typically start with a brief orientation at the discovery center before heading onto the trail system for guided activities. Environmental cleanup sessions involve removing invasive species, maintaining boardwalks, or restoring native plant areas under expert supervision. Nature-focused programs include wildlife tracking, habitat exploration, and team challenges that incorporate natural elements found throughout the preserve.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-25 who want meaningful outdoor work that feels more purposeful than typical team building. Environmental stewardship projects appeal especially to mission-driven organizations and groups seeking activities that align with sustainability values.
