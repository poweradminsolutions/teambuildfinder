Onward Theatre

4.8★ (20 reviews)

Onward Theatre in Atlanta, GA is a premier improv and comedy club that offers unique team building experiences. Their interactive workshops and high-energy shows are a fantastic way for groups to bond, improve communication, and unleash their inner comedic talents.

What to Expect

Groups can participate in hands-on improv classes led by Onward's skilled instructors. These sessions focus on building trust, embracing spontaneity, and thinking on your feet. After the workshop, teams can enjoy a side-splitting performance by Onward's resident improv troupes, who will have the whole group in stitches.

Who It's For

Onward Theatre's team building activities are ideal for mid-sized groups of 15-40 looking to foster camaraderie and boost morale. The collaborative, lighthearted nature of improv makes it a great choice for company offsites, holiday parties, or any occasion when a group needs to connect and have fun together.
