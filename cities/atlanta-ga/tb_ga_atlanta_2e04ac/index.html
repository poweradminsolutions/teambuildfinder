AdVenture Games Team Building Atlanta

5.0★ (8 reviews) 90–120 minutes" for "Office Escape

Located in the heart of downtown Atlanta, AdVenture Games Team Building offers an immersive and challenging escape room experience that's perfect for building camaraderie and problem-solving skills among colleagues. With a variety of themed rooms to choose from, your team will work together under the pressure of a ticking clock to uncover clues, solve puzzles, and make their daring escape.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be briefed on the rules and mechanics of your chosen escape room. You'll then be locked inside, tasked with working as a team to find hidden items, decipher cryptic messages, and ultimately unlock the door before time runs out. The experience requires clear communication, creative thinking, and a healthy dose of friendly competition.

Who It's For

AdVenture Games Team Building is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking to break out of the conference room and engage in a truly unique team-building activity. The shared sense of accomplishment - or frustration, if you don't make it out in time! - tends to forge bonds between coworkers across departments.
