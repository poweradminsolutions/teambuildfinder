Art & Soul Studio

4.5★ (77 reviews)

Located in the heart of Sandy Springs, Georgia, Art & Soul Studio offers a unique and engaging team building experience through their art and craft workshops. With a focus on creativity and collaboration, these sessions encourage groups to unleash their artistic sides and bond over shared projects.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through the process of selecting a pottery or painting project, then provided with all the necessary materials and step-by-step instruction from the studio's talented artists. Together, you'll work towards completing your creations, fostering communication, problem-solving, and a sense of shared accomplishment.

Who It's For

Art & Soul Studio's team building workshops are ideal for mid-sized groups of 15 to 40 looking to mix things up and tap into their creative energies. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, a department outing, or a fun team building activity, these hands-on experiences are sure to bring your colleagues closer together.
