Let's Try This Improv

5.0★ (7 reviews)

Let's Try This Improv in Atlanta offers a fresh approach to team building with their dynamic improv comedy workshops. Dive into the world of improvisational acting and watch your group come alive with laughter, camaraderie, and a newfound sense of collaboration.

What to Expect

Your group will be guided through a series of interactive improv exercises and games that build communication skills, quick-thinking, and trust. No prior experience is necessary - the talented instructors will have everyone participating and creating hilarious scenes from the very first minute.

Who It's For

This activity works exceptionally well for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to inject some playful energy into their team building. The shared experience of stepping out of comfort zones and supporting one another on stage cultivates a stronger bond that carries back to the workplace.
