David L. Lawrence Convention Center

4.5★ (4218 reviews)

The David L. Lawrence Convention Center transforms its expansive facilities into customized retreat and workshop spaces for corporate teams visiting Pittsburgh. Professional facilitators work with groups to design structured learning experiences that range from leadership development sessions to collaborative problem-solving workshops. The venue's flexible meeting rooms and breakout spaces allow for seamless transitions between presentations, small group activities, and larger team exercises.

What to Expect

Your team will participate in structured workshop sessions led by experienced facilitators who tailor activities to your specific goals and challenges. Expect a mix of interactive presentations, breakout discussions, and hands-on exercises that move between different meeting spaces throughout the day. Most programs include dedicated time for reflection and action planning to ensure teams leave with concrete next steps.

Who It's For

Ideal for medium to large corporate teams (20-100+ people) planning multi-day retreats or intensive skill-building sessions. The professional atmosphere and comprehensive facilities work particularly well for leadership development programs and strategic planning initiatives.
