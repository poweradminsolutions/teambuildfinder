Heinz Hall for the Performing Arts

4.8★ (3144 reviews)

Heinz Hall transforms team building through collaborative music experiences led by professional musicians in one of Pittsburgh's most prestigious venues. Groups participate in interactive drumming circles and rhythm workshops that build communication skills while surrounded by the grandeur of this 1927 concert hall. The acoustics alone make every beat resonate with unusual power and clarity.

What to Expect

Teams gather in performance spaces within the hall for structured drumming sessions where everyone receives percussion instruments and learns basic rhythms together. Professional facilitators guide groups through call-and-response exercises, group compositions, and synchronized performances that require listening, timing, and coordination. Sessions typically build from individual beats to complex ensemble pieces that showcase collective achievement.

Who It's For

Ideal for corporate groups of 20-60 people seeking an upscale team building experience with cultural flair. The combination of musical collaboration and historic elegance appeals especially to leadership teams and client entertainment events where you want to make a lasting impression.
