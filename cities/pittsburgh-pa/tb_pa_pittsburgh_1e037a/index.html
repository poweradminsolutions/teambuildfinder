Little House, Big Art

4.9★ (41 reviews)

Located in the heart of Pittsburgh, Little House, Big Art offers a unique team building experience through immersive art workshops. With a focus on fostering creativity and collaboration, groups can explore a variety of mediums, from pottery-making to canvas painting, in a welcoming and inspiring studio environment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be greeted by the studio's talented instructors who will guide you through the chosen art activity. Participants will have the opportunity to work together to conceptualize and create their own masterpieces, fostering a sense of camaraderie and shared accomplishment.

Who It's For

Little House, Big Art's art workshops are perfect for teams of all sizes and experience levels, whether you're looking for a fun afternoon activity or a more in-depth creative retreat. The hands-on, collaborative nature of the experience makes it ideal for team bonding, problem-solving, and celebrating shared successes.
