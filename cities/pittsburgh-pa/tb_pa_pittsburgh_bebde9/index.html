Scene on the Blvd

4.8★ (50 reviews) 5 hours

Scene on the Blvd in Pittsburgh offers a lively team building experience with their trivia and game night packages. Groups can expect a high-energy, social atmosphere as they put their collective knowledge and problem-solving skills to the test through a variety of engaging activities.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided to a dedicated event space with ample room to mingle. The evening kicks off with a lively round of trivia, testing everyone's pop culture and current events savvy. Teams then move on to interactive challenges that foster collaboration and healthy competition, from solving escape room-style puzzles to virtual scavenger hunts.

Who It's For

These team building events at Scene on the Blvd work particularly well for medium-sized groups of 15-40 looking to shake up the typical happy hour or off-site meeting. The mix of games and activities creates an energetic atmosphere that helps break down barriers between departments and build camaraderie.
