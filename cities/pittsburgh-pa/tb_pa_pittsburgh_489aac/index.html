CityLine Event Center

4.2★ (19 reviews)

Located in the heart of Pittsburgh, CityLine Event Center offers a wide range of team building activities perfect for groups looking to break out of the boardroom. From competitive trivia nights to innovative hybrid experiences, this versatile venue has something to energize and engage teams of all kinds.

What to Expect

CityLine's team building packages include fun, fast-paced trivia games where groups race against the clock to showcase their knowledge. For a more immersive experience, the venue's hybrid activities blend digital and physical elements, challenging teams to collaborate and problem-solve their way through unique challenges.

Who It's For

CityLine's team building activities work well for mid-size groups (15-40 people) seeking a dynamic, interactive experience. Whether you're looking to foster interdepartmental collaboration or simply want to inject some friendly competition into your next offsite, this Pittsburgh venue has the versatility to deliver an unforgettable team building event.
