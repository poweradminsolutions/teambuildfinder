Pittsburgh Botanic Garden

4.6★ (1670 reviews)

Pittsburgh Botanic Garden transforms team building into a nature-immersive experience across 460 acres of themed gardens and woodlands in Oakdale. Groups can participate in guided botanical scavenger hunts, collaborative garden mapping exercises, or team challenges that incorporate the garden's diverse ecosystems and seasonal plantings.

What to Expect

Teams arrive for structured activities that blend education with collaboration, moving through different garden zones to complete nature-based challenges or learn about plant identification and ecosystem relationships. Most programs include both walking trail exploration and hands-on activities in designated garden areas, with facilitators providing botanical context throughout the experience.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams seeking a refreshing alternative to indoor venues, particularly groups of 12-30 who appreciate slower-paced, contemplative activities. The natural setting works especially well for organizations wanting to emphasize mindfulness, environmental awareness, or simply give their team fresh air and beautiful surroundings.
