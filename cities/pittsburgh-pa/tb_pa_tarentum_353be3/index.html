Gundel's Fun Zone

5.0★ (25 reviews)

Gundel's Fun Zone in Tarentum, PA is a local favorite for corporate teams seeking an engaging escape room experience. With both traditional and virtual rooms on offer, they provide a unique team-based challenge that requires cooperation, critical thinking, and a bit of friendly competition to solve the puzzles and beat the clock.

What to Expect

When you visit Gundel's, your group will be assigned to a themed room and given a specific scenario to work through. You'll have a set amount of time, typically 60-90 minutes, to search for clues, solve riddles, and ultimately escape before time runs out. The virtual rooms add an extra layer of immersion with 360-degree environments displayed on large screens.

Who It's For

Gundel's Fun Zone is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to foster better communication and collaboration through a shared problem-solving experience. The mix of teamwork and friendly competition makes these activities great for onboarding new hires, bringing departments together, or simply boosting morale.
