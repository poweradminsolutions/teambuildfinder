The Johnston House

4.8★ (255 reviews)

Nestled in the quaint town of Mars, Pennsylvania, The Johnston House offers a unique and memorable team building experience. This historic tea house has been meticulously restored, providing a charming and picturesque setting for group retreats and facilitated workshops.

What to Expect

When you arrive, your team will be greeted by the knowledgeable staff and guided through a variety of interactive exercises designed to foster collaboration, communication, and problem-solving skills. Activities can range from scavenger hunts and escape room-style challenges to guided meditation sessions and group cooking classes.

Who It's For

The Johnston House is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to step away from the office and engage in meaningful team building. The carefully curated activities and serene, distraction-free environment make it an ideal setting for groups seeking to strengthen relationships, build trust, and unlock their collective potential.
