Landmark Theatre

4.5★ (2292 reviews)

Landmark Theatre transforms team building through private group performances and behind-the-scenes theatrical experiences in Syracuse's historic performing arts district. Your team can participate in improv workshops led by professional actors, explore backstage areas during guided tours, or attend exclusive group performances followed by cast meet-and-greets.

What to Expect

Groups typically start with a welcome reception in the theatre lobby before diving into interactive workshops focusing on communication, creativity, and collaboration through theatrical exercises. Professional facilitators guide teams through improv games, storytelling activities, and performance challenges that encourage participants to step outside their comfort zones in a supportive environment.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and organizations wanting to boost communication skills and break down workplace hierarchies. The theatrical setting naturally encourages even the most reserved team members to participate and connect.
