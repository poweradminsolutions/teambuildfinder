Delavan Studios

4.8★ (69 reviews)

Delavan Studios in Syracuse, New York offers a dynamic, hands-on team building experience through their art workshops. Groups have the opportunity to unleash their creativity and work collaboratively on unique projects, fostering camaraderie and problem-solving skills in a fun, supportive environment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through a thoughtful selection of art mediums, from painting and pottery to printmaking and mixed media. Skilled instructors provide step-by-step instruction and encouragement, helping participants of all skill levels discover their inner artist. The activity flow is designed to promote teamwork, communication, and a shared sense of accomplishment.

Who It's For

Delavan Studios' art workshops are perfect for mid-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to bond over a creative challenge. The shared experience of crafting something new together tends to break down barriers and build trust, making it an excellent choice for corporate retreats, onboarding events, or simply treating your hard-working staff to a memorable team-building activity.
