The Oncenter

4.5★ (336 reviews)

Situated in the heart of Syracuse, New York, The Oncenter is a premier destination for team building activities. Boasting a diverse range of flexible event spaces, this convention center offers a unique opportunity for groups to engage in immersive retreats and facilitated workshops that foster camaraderie and collaboration.

What to Expect

When your team visits The Oncenter, they can expect a seamless, professionally-guided experience. Facilitators lead groups through interactive exercises and challenges designed to promote problem-solving, communication, and leadership skills. Whether it's an escape room challenge or a competitive team-building workshop, the focus is on building connections and strengthening bonds.

Who It's For

The Oncenter's team building offerings are perfect for mid-sized groups of 15-40 looking to step away from the daily grind and invest in their collective growth. The shared experiences and team-oriented activities create an environment that breaks down barriers, allowing colleagues to forge stronger, more cohesive relationships.
