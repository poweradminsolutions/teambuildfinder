Far Shot Syracuse (Axe Throwing, Archery, and Knife)

5.0★ (1143 reviews)

Far Shot Syracuse transforms typical team outings with hands-on axe throwing and archery experiences that get everyone engaged, regardless of skill level. This Syracuse venue combines the thrill of learning new skills with friendly competition, creating memorable bonding moments as colleagues cheer each other on and celebrate unexpected victories.

What to Expect

Groups rotate through guided sessions where trained instructors teach proper throwing techniques and safety protocols before teams compete in various challenges. The format typically includes individual skill-building followed by team-versus-team competitions, with scoring systems that keep everyone invested in the outcome.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-25 people who want something more adventurous than typical corporate activities. The physical nature and friendly competition work especially well for groups looking to build camaraderie and see colleagues in a completely different light.
