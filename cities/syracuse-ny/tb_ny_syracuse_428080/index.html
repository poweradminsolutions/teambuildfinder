WonderWorks Destiny

4.1★ (838 reviews)

Located in the heart of Syracuse, New York, WonderWorks Destiny offers an unparalleled team building experience that combines hands-on science exhibits with adrenaline-pumping activities. From navigating the ropes course to engaging in high-stakes laser tag battles, this multi-faceted attraction provides the perfect setting for groups to bond, challenge themselves, and foster camaraderie.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by a dedicated staff who will guide you through the various team building options. Start by conquering the ropes course, where you'll navigate obstacles and work together to reach new heights. Then, gear up for a laser tag showdown in the state-of-the-art arena, where friendly competition will bring out the best in your colleagues. Between activities, explore the 100+ interactive science exhibits that will spark creativity and provoke thought-provoking discussions.

Who It's For

WonderWorks Destiny is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to infuse their team building experience with a healthy dose of adventure and intellectual stimulation. The shared challenges of the ropes course and laser tag will break down barriers between departments, while the educational exhibits provide a unique opportunity for team members to bond over their shared curiosity and sense of wonder.
