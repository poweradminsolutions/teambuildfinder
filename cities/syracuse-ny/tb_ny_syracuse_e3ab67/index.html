Amazing Mirror Maze

4.0★ (49 reviews)

The Amazing Mirror Maze in Syracuse, New York offers a truly unique and thrilling team building experience. Navigating the labyrinth of reflective surfaces challenges groups to communicate, strategize, and work together under pressure to find their way out. This dynamic activity is perfect for fostering collaboration and problem-solving skills.

What to Expect

When your group arrives, you'll be immersed in a disorienting maze of mirrors and forced to rely on your teamwork to make your way through. You'll need to put your heads together to identify visual cues, avoid dead ends, and uncover the exit as quickly as possible. The competitive element adds an extra layer of excitement to the challenge.

Who It's For

The Amazing Mirror Maze is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams of 15-40 seeking a physical, high-energy team building activity. The shared sense of accomplishment when your group solves the maze helps to break down barriers and build camaraderie across departments.
