CREATE Pottery Shop

4.5★ (107 reviews)

Located in the charming city of Midlothian, Virginia, CREATE Pottery Shop offers a truly unique team building experience. Groups can roll up their sleeves and tap into their creative sides by designing and crafting their own pottery pieces under the guidance of expert instructors.

What to Expect

Participants will start by learning basic pottery techniques like wedging, centering, and shaping clay on the pottery wheel. Then they'll move on to decorating their creations with colorful glazes. The final step is watching their handiwork come to life in the kiln before taking their masterpieces home.

Who It's For

This hands-on workshop is perfect for teams of 10-30 looking to foster collaboration and camaraderie through a shared creative challenge. The interactive, focused nature of the experience makes it ideal for corporate gatherings, office outings, or any group seeking to bond over a fun, memorable activity.
