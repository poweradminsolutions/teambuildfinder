2810: a community gathering place

5.0★ (21 reviews)

2810 is a versatile community gathering place in the heart of Richmond, VA that offers unique team building experiences. Their two onsite escape rooms, including a virtual option, challenge groups to work together under pressure and put their problem-solving skills to the test.

What to Expect

Teams of 2-10 players are immersed in themed escape room environments and tasked with using their collective wits to find clues, solve puzzles, and ultimately 'escape' the room within the 60-minute time limit. The virtual escape room option allows remote teams to tackle the challenge together from anywhere.

Who It's For

The escape room experiences at 2810 are a great fit for mid-size teams (10-40 players) seeking a memorable team building activity that encourages collaboration, communication, and friendly competition. The shared sense of accomplishment tends to bring coworkers closer together.
