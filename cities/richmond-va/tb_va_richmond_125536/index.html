Gather Arts District Downtown

4.7★ (84 reviews)

Gather Arts District Downtown in Richmond, VA offers a dynamic, creativity-fueled setting for team building. With a flexible event space, breakout rooms, and expert facilitation, groups can dive into hands-on workshops, design challenges, or immersive retreats tailored to their goals.

What to Expect

When your team arrives, the Gather staff will greet you and provide an orientation to the versatile 8,000 sq ft facility. From there, you might kick things off with an icebreaker activity before transitioning into a facilitated session focused on ideation, problem-solving, or team bonding.

Who It's For

Gather's team building experiences work well for mid-size groups (15-40 participants) seeking an energizing, out-of-the-office day. The collaborative, innovative atmosphere makes it an excellent choice for cross-functional teams looking to spark new ideas or strengthen working relationships.
