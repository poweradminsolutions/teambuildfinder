ROOM3C

4.6★ (23 reviews)

ROOM3C in Richmond, VA offers immersive, facilitated workshops and retreats that challenge teams to work together in dynamic, hands-on environments. Their experienced staff curates engaging experiences tailored to each group's goals, from intensive problem-solving exercises to creative, collaborative projects.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a thoughtfully structured agenda that blends interactive activities, discussion, and time for reflection. Facilitators keep the energy high as teams tackle challenges that require open communication, strategic thinking, and a spirit of friendly competition.

Who It's For

ROOM3C's programs are well-suited for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a transformative team building experience. The shared triumphs and obstacles tend to rapidly build trust and camaraderie across departments, making it an ideal choice for organizations looking to boost morale and collaboration.
