Common House Richmond

4.6★ (177 reviews)

Located in the heart of Richmond, Virginia, Common House offers a unique team-building experience with its weekly trivia and game nights. This social club provides a fun and engaging atmosphere for groups looking to bond over friendly competition and a shared love of games.

What to Expect

When your team visits Common House, you'll be greeted with a lively, welcoming vibe. The staff will help you get set up in one of the club's comfortable lounges or private event spaces, equipped with a variety of board games, trivia materials, and other interactive activities. Your group will work together to tackle themed trivia rounds or compete in classic party games, all while enjoying craft cocktails and shareable bites.

Who It's For

Common House's trivia and game nights are perfect for mid-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to mix a bit of friendly rivalry with camaraderie. Whether you're planning a company outing, a holiday party, or just want to get your colleagues out of the office, this venue provides an ideal setting to strengthen interpersonal connections and build team unity.
