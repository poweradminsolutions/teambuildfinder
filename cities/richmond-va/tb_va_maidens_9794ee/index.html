The Estate At River Run

4.9★ (214 reviews)

Nestled in the picturesque countryside of Maidens, Virginia, The Estate at River Run offers a serene and inspiring setting for team building retreats. With its stunning natural surroundings and elegant event spaces, this venue provides the perfect backdrop for groups to connect, collaborate, and challenge themselves.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, groups are immersed in a variety of team-focused activities, from outdoor adventures like hiking and kayaking to indoor problem-solving exercises and creative workshops. The experienced staff guides participants through engaging, customized programs that foster communication, trust, and camaraderie.

Who It's For

The Estate at River Run is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (20-40 people) seeking a transformative team building experience. The combination of natural beauty, interactive activities, and attentive service makes this venue a favorite for corporate offsites, nonprofit retreats, and groups looking to strengthen their bonds.
