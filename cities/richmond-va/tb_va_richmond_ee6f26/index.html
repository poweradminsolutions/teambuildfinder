Gallery5

4.7★ (324 reviews)

Housed in a historic 1867 firehouse, Gallery5 in Richmond, VA offers a unique and engaging team building experience unlike anything else in the city. This dynamic arts venue specializes in hosting immersive workshops and improv sessions that challenge groups to unleash their creativity and collaboration skills.

What to Expect

A team building session at Gallery5 might involve an interactive art class where colleagues work together to design and paint a mural. Or an improv comedy workshop that gets everyone out of their comfort zones and thinking on their feet. The flexible event spaces allow for a range of customizable activities to suit any group's needs.

Who It's For

Gallery5's team building offerings are ideal for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to bond through shared creative challenges. The hands-on, high-energy activities tend to break down barriers between departments and build trust faster than a standard corporate retreat.
