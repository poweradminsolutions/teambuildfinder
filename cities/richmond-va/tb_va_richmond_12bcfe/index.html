RVA Paddlesports

4.9★ (491 reviews) $$$$ 1-4 hours

RVA Paddlesports transforms Richmond's scenic waterways into team building opportunities through guided rafting trips and kayak excursions. Teams navigate the James River together, tackling rapids and enjoying calmer stretches while building communication skills naturally. Their experienced guides handle the logistics while your group focuses on working together on the water.

What to Expect

Groups meet at their launch point for a safety briefing and paddle instruction before heading out on the James River. Depending on your chosen route, teams spend 2-4 hours on the water, with guides providing direction and ensuring everyone stays together. The experience includes all necessary equipment and ends with groups sharing stories about their river adventure.

Who It's For

Ideal for active teams of 8-25 people who want to step away from conference rooms and try something adventurous together. The shared experience of navigating rapids creates natural bonding moments that translate back to workplace collaboration.
