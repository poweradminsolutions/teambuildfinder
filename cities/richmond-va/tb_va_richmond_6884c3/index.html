Lily Black

5.0★ (6 reviews)

Lily Black in Richmond, VA offers a unique team building experience that immerses groups in the art of facilitated workshops and thought-provoking retreats. Led by seasoned facilitators, these engaging sessions challenge teams to step outside their comfort zones, fostering collaboration, problem-solving, and meaningful connections.

What to Expect

At Lily Black, groups can expect a carefully curated agenda that blends interactive exercises, group discussions, and creative activities. The flexible format allows for customization to suit the needs and goals of each team, whether they're seeking a half-day workshop or a multi-day retreat.

Who It's For

Lily Black's team building programs are particularly well-suited for mid-sized teams (15-40 participants) looking to strengthen interpersonal dynamics and cultivate a cohesive, high-performing culture. The tailored experiences cater to a wide range of industries and occasions, from onboarding new hires to strategic planning sessions.
