Treetop Zoofari Zipline and Adventure Park

4.8★ (439 reviews) $$ 45-60 minutes" for Junior Explorer, "1 to 2 hours

Treetop Zoofari combines high-flying zipline courses with challenging rope obstacles through the forest canopy in Moseley, Virginia. Teams navigate elevated platforms and zip between trees while working together to overcome height fears and physical challenges. The multi-level course design creates natural opportunities for peer encouragement and shared achievement.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety briefings and harness fitting before tackling progressively challenging obstacle sections connected by ziplines. Teams move through the course together, with members naturally forming support networks as they navigate rope bridges, cargo nets, and aerial platforms. The experience typically involves 2-3 hours of active participation with built-in rest points between course sections.

Who It's For

Ideal for physically active teams of 8-20 people who want an authentic challenge that reveals character under pressure. The shared vulnerability of height and physical obstacles creates genuine bonding moments that translate directly back to workplace trust and communication.
