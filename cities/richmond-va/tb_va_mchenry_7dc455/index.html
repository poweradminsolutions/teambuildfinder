Twisted Limits Axe Throwing Lounge

4.8★ (73 reviews)

Twisted Limits Axe Throwing Lounge in McHenry, Illinois offers a truly unique team building experience. Groups of all sizes can try their hand at the ancient sport of axe throwing, honing their accuracy and coordination in a safe, supervised setting. The lounge's expert staff provide thorough training and guidance, ensuring even first-time throwers feel comfortable and confident.

What to Expect

When your team arrives, you'll receive a brief lesson on proper throwing technique. Then it's time to take aim at the targets and see who has the best axe-slinging skills. The competition is friendly yet spirited, with plenty of cheering and camaraderie as colleagues challenge each other. Rounds are structured to encourage teamwork and strategy.

Who It's For

Twisted Limits is ideal for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking a memorable, physically-engaging team building activity. The shared challenge of mastering axe throwing helps break down barriers and foster new connections between employees, making it a great choice for company offsites, department outings, or even bachelor/bachelorette parties.
