Black Iris- Social Club, Art Gallery, Event Space

4.9★ (119 reviews)

Richmond's Black Iris Social Club is a unique hybrid space that blends an art gallery, event venue, and social club into one dynamic destination. Teams looking to tap into their creative side will thrive in the hands-on art workshops and improv comedy sessions hosted here.

What to Expect

Art workshops led by local artists guide participants through painting, sketching, or sculpting projects, bringing out their inner artist in a fun, judgement-free environment. The improv classes, meanwhile, have teams working together to devise clever, on-the-spot comedy routines, building communication and collaboration skills.

Who It's For

Black Iris is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a playful, out-of-the-box team building experience. The interactive activities encourage cross-functional bonding and are suitable for all skill levels, from the art aficionado to the comedy novice.
