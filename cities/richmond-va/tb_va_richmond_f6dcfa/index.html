Mission Laser RVA

4.9★ (1003 reviews)

Mission Laser RVA transforms team building into an adrenaline-fueled battle across multi-level arenas filled with fog, neon lighting, and strategic obstacles. This Richmond laser tag facility goes beyond basic point-and-shoot gaming with mission-based scenarios that demand communication and tactical planning. Teams navigate darkened mazes while coordinating attacks and defending objectives in an immersive sci-fi environment.

What to Expect

Groups gear up with lightweight laser equipment and receive mission briefings before entering themed arenas for 15-20 minute games. Multiple rounds allow teams to adjust strategies, form alliances, or settle scores while real-time scoring tracks individual and team performance. The facility can accommodate large groups by running simultaneous games or tournament-style competitions.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-50 people who want high-energy competition without the mess and pain of paintball. The fast-paced action and immediate do-overs make it excellent for groups with mixed fitness levels or anyone seeking an activity that builds camaraderie through shared victories and defeats.
