Stumpy's Hatchet House of Hershey

4.9★ (1120 reviews) $$ 1-2 hours

Stumpy's Hatchet House brings competitive axe throwing to corporate teams in Hershey, combining the primal satisfaction of hurling steel with structured team competition. Groups rotate through throwing lanes with professional coaching, creating an unexpectedly effective bonding experience that gets everyone cheering for colleagues they barely knew before.

What to Expect

Teams receive safety briefings and throwing technique instruction before rotating through private lanes with trained axe masters. Sessions typically include individual practice time followed by team tournaments with scoring systems that keep everyone engaged regardless of skill level.

Who It's For

Ideal for groups of 8-30 people who want something memorably different from typical team events. The novelty factor and shared learning curve create natural conversation starters and lasting memories.
