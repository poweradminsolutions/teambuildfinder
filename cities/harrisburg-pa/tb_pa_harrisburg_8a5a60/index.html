Pennsylvania Farm Show Complex & Expo Center

4.5★ (8177 reviews)

The Pennsylvania Farm Show Complex transforms its massive exhibition halls into versatile team building venues throughout the year in Harrisburg. Groups can organize large-scale volunteer initiatives like community service projects or charity drives that leverage the facility's enormous capacity. The complex also hosts facilitated workshops and corporate retreats that benefit from the unique agricultural setting and spacious meeting areas.

What to Expect

Your team will work in one of several large exhibition halls or meeting spaces, depending on your chosen activity format. Volunteer events might involve assembling care packages or organizing donation drives, while workshops typically feature breakout sessions followed by large group presentations. The agricultural atmosphere and industrial-scale spaces create an unexpectedly engaging backdrop for team collaboration.

Who It's For

Ideal for large organizations (50+ people) planning major volunteer initiatives or multi-day retreats. The unique venue works especially well for companies in agriculture, manufacturing, or nonprofits that want something memorable beyond the typical hotel conference room.
