Mud Queen Pottery

4.8★ (38 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, Mud Queen Pottery offers an immersive team building experience that taps into the creative potential of your group. With expert guidance, your team will learn the art of wheel-throwing and hand-building, crafting unique pottery pieces that showcase their collaborative spirit.

What to Expect

During a Mud Queen Pottery team building session, your group will start by learning the fundamentals of working with clay. Participants will have the opportunity to try their hand at the pottery wheel, guided by the studio's skilled instructors. The hands-on experience culminates in each person designing and creating their own one-of-a-kind piece to take home as a memento of your team's shared experience.

Who It's For

Mud Queen Pottery's team building activities are an excellent choice for groups of 10-25 looking to foster creativity, communication, and camaraderie. The shared challenge of mastering the pottery techniques encourages colleagues to work together, while the personal nature of the final products helps to break down barriers between team members.
