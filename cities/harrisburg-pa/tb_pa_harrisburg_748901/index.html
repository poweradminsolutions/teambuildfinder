The Building

4.4★ (9 reviews)

The Building in Harrisburg, PA provides an exceptional team building experience with their facilitated workshops and retreats. Groups are immersed in creative problem-solving exercises and hands-on challenges that foster collaboration and build trust among team members.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be welcomed by The Building's experienced facilitators who guide you through a tailored agenda of interactive activities. Expect to engage in a mix of indoor and outdoor tasks that encourage creative thinking, communication, and friendly competition.

Who It's For

The Building's team building offerings are well-suited for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds and break down barriers. The shared experiences tend to be both productive and fun, making them ideal for company offsites, leadership retreats, and other professional gatherings.
