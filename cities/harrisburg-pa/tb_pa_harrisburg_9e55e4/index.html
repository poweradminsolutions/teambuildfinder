Harrisburg Jewish Community Center

4.7★ (138 reviews)

The Harrisburg Jewish Community Center is a vibrant hub for team building activities in central Pennsylvania. Their improv and comedy workshops are a unique way to foster collaboration, communication, and creativity among your group.

What to Expect

Led by experienced facilitators, these 2-3 hour sessions get teams working together through a series of engaging improv games and exercises. Participants will learn the fundamentals of improvisational comedy while building trust, listening skills, and the ability to think on their feet.

Who It's For

The Harrisburg JCC's improv workshops are perfect for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking to break out of their usual routines and bond over a shared, playful challenge. The shared laughter and 'yes, and' mentality of improv helps break down barriers and brings coworkers closer together.
