The Country Club of Harrisburg

4.7★ (174 reviews) $$$ 2 hours

The Country Club of Harrisburg, located in the heart of Pennsylvania's capital, offers a unique team building experience that combines friendly competition with scenic views. With its expansive golf course and range of sports facilities, the club provides the perfect backdrop for groups to bond over shared challenges and camaraderie.

What to Expect

Teams can take part in a variety of sports tournaments, from golf scrambles to tennis or basketball competitions. The club's staff will work with you to customize the experience, ensuring a seamless flow and ample opportunities for your group to put their skills to the test in a fun, supportive environment.

Who It's For

The Country Club of Harrisburg is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a memorable team building activity. The combination of physical activity and teamwork encourages collaboration and communication, making it an ideal option for corporate groups, sports teams, or any organization looking to foster stronger connections between colleagues.
