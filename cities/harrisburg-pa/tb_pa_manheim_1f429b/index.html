ClayHouse Arts

5.0★ (13 reviews)

Located in the charming town of Manheim, Pennsylvania, ClayHouse Arts offers a unique and creative team building experience through their hands-on art workshops. With expert instruction, groups can explore their artistic sides by crafting personalized pottery pieces or delving into other artistic mediums.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be guided through a 2-3 hour workshop tailored to your team's interests and skill levels. Whether it's throwing clay on the pottery wheel, sculpting intricate designs, or experimenting with painting techniques, the collaborative process encourages communication, problem-solving, and a shared sense of accomplishment.

Who It's For

ClayHouse Arts is an excellent choice for teams of 10-30 looking to step away from the office and bond over a rewarding creative activity. The relaxed, supportive environment is perfect for team members at all artistic levels, making it a great option for both regular team outings and special events.
