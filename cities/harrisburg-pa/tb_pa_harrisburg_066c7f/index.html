John Harris-Simon Cameron

4.8★ (80 reviews)

Situated in the heart of Harrisburg, this historic mansion offers a unique and engaging team building experience. Explore the captivating Civil War exhibits and immerse yourselves in the rich history of the region, then participate in facilitated workshops and retreats that foster collaboration and problem-solving among your group.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by knowledgeable guides who will lead you on a private tour of the mansion and museum. Afterwards, you'll gather in the elegant banquet hall for a facilitated workshop or team-building activity tailored to your group's needs. These sessions encourage communication, creativity, and camaraderie through interactive exercises and challenges.

Who It's For

This venue is ideal for mid-sized teams (15-40 members) seeking a blend of cultural exploration and team development. The combination of hands-on activities and historical context creates an experience that brings colleagues closer together while broadening their understanding of the local community.
