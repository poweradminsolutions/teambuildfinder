Hidden Hillside Events

4.2★ (18 reviews)

Nestled in the rolling hills of Camp Hill, Pennsylvania, Hidden Hillside Events offers a unique and dynamic team building experience. This picturesque venue combines the great outdoors with a variety of engaging group activities that challenge teams to work together, think creatively, and strengthen their bonds.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Hidden Hillside, your group will be greeted by the friendly staff and guided through a range of team-focused games and challenges. From navigating a high-ropes course to working together to solve an outdoor escape room, each activity is designed to get people out of their comfort zones and collaborating in new ways.

Who It's For

Hidden Hillside's team building programs are ideal for mid-sized groups of 15-40 looking to foster better communication, boost morale, and cultivate a stronger sense of camaraderie. The combination of physical, mental, and problem-solving tasks makes this venue a great choice for corporate retreats, leadership development workshops, or simply a fun day away from the office.
