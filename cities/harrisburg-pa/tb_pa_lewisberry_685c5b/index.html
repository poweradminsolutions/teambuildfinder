Roundtop Ropes Course

4.3★ (12 reviews)

Roundtop Ropes Course in Lewisberry, Pennsylvania offers an exhilarating team building experience like no other. Suspended high above the ground, groups navigate a thrilling network of cables, platforms, and obstacles, fostering camaraderie and pushing individual limits in a beautiful outdoor setting.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive a comprehensive safety briefing before suiting up in provided harnesses and helmets. Then it's time to take on the course, with team members encouraging and supporting one another as they tackle each challenging element. The shared experience of conquering the ropes and obstacles is a powerful bonding activity.

Who It's For

Roundtop Ropes Course is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 participants) seeking a high-energy, adventurous team building activity. The physical and mental challenges help break down barriers, improve communication, and build trust between coworkers in a fun, memorable way.
