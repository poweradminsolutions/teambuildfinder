Avondale Brewing Company

4.5★ (832 reviews) 4 hour rental

Avondale Brewing Company transforms team building into a multi-sensory experience that goes far beyond typical corporate outings. This Birmingham hotspot combines craft beer education with interactive workshops, live music elements, and even indoor golf challenges, creating memorable team experiences that blend learning with genuine fun.

What to Expect

Groups typically start with guided beer tastings led by knowledgeable staff who explain the brewing process and flavor profiles of signature beers. Teams can participate in facilitated workshops that might include music-based activities or competitive indoor golf tournaments, with the flexibility to customize the experience based on your group's interests and energy level.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 10-30 who want something more sophisticated than a standard happy hour but less formal than a conference room workshop. The combination of craft beer education and interactive elements works especially well for celebrating project completions or welcoming new team members.
