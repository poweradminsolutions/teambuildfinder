The Venue at CJP

4.6★ (12 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Birmingham, Alabama, The Venue at CJP offers a dynamic and immersive team building experience. With its state-of-the-art conference facilities and unique activity options, this venue is the perfect setting to bring your team together for a memorable and productive day.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of team-focused activities, including scavenger hunts, escape rooms, and interactive workshops. The experienced staff will guide your team through the chosen experience, fostering collaboration, communication, and problem-solving skills in a fun and engaging environment.

Who It's For

The Venue at CJP is ideal for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to strengthen their camaraderie and boost morale. Whether you're planning a company retreat, a team-building day, or a corporate event, this versatile space and its diverse activity offerings can accommodate your group's needs.
