Story

4.9★ (104 reviews)

Story in Louisville, KY is a coworking space that offers a range of engaging team building activities. From competitive escape rooms that challenge groups to work together under pressure, to creative workshops that spark collaboration, Story provides experiences tailored to bring teams closer.

What to Expect

When your group visits Story, you'll be greeted by a helpful staff who will guide you through the chosen activity. Depending on your selection, you might find yourselves racing the clock to solve mind-bending puzzles, or tapping into your artistic side to create something new as a team.

Who It's For

Story's team building offerings are perfect for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds and break down barriers. Whether celebrating a company milestone or just wanting to inject some friendly competition into the workday, Story's versatile space and activities cater to a variety of team-focused occasions.
