Belle of Louisville Riverboats

4.5★ (3251 reviews) $$ 1-2 hours

The Belle of Louisville combines authentic steamboat history with engaging team experiences on the Ohio River. Groups can book private charters for trivia competitions and game nights aboard this National Historic Landmark, creating memorable Louisville team outings with stunning river views and nostalgic charm.

What to Expect

Teams board the vintage paddlewheeler for organized activities like interactive trivia contests or customized game tournaments while cruising the Ohio River. The historic vessel provides a unique backdrop as groups work together on challenges, with the gentle movement of the boat and scenic waterfront creating a relaxed but focused atmosphere.

Who It's For

Ideal for established teams of 20-60 people who want something distinctly different from typical conference room activities. The combination of history, water, and structured games works especially well for groups celebrating milestones or looking to strengthen relationships in a memorable setting.
