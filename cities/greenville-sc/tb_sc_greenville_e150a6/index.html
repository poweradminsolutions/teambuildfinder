Roper Mountain Science Center

4.7★ (498 reviews)

The Roper Mountain Science Center in Greenville, South Carolina offers a diverse range of hands-on workshops and facilitated activities perfect for team building. From guided tours of the impressive planetarium and observatory to immersive living history experiences on the farm, there's something to engage every member of your group.

What to Expect

Groups can participate in a wide variety of interactive STEM-focused workshops led by knowledgeable staff. Popular options include building and launching model rockets, exploring the wonders of the night sky in the state-of-the-art planetarium, or diving deep into the scientific processes that sustain the on-site greenhouse and arboretum.

Who It's For

The Roper Mountain Science Center is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to strengthen bonds through shared learning and discovery. The mix of educational and recreational activities creates an atmosphere of friendly competition that brings colleagues together in a unique and memorable way.
