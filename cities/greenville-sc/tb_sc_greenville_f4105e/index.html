Escape Ballroom

4.2★ (104 reviews)

Located in the heart of Greenville, South Carolina, Escape Ballroom offers an immersive escape room experience that's perfect for team building. Groups work together to solve a series of puzzles and challenges, putting their problem-solving skills and communication to the test in a fun, high-energy setting.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be escorted to a themed escape room where you'll have 60 minutes to work together and escape. You'll need to search for clues, crack codes, and think creatively to progress through the experience. No prior escape room experience is necessary - the friendly staff will provide a thorough briefing to get you started.

Who It's For

Escape Ballroom works well for mid-size teams (10-30 participants) looking for a unique team building activity that encourages collaboration and camaraderie. The shared challenge of escaping the room in time is an excellent way to break down barriers and foster team bonding.
