Saluda Outdoor River Company

4.8★ (220 reviews) 2-3 hours

Saluda Outdoor River Company in Greenville, SC offers a thrilling team building experience on the pristine Saluda River. Groups can challenge themselves with whitewater rafting, kayaking, or stand-up paddleboarding through Class I-III rapids, all while bonding over the shared adventure.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive comprehensive safety and technique training from experienced guides. Then it's time to hit the water! Teams will navigate the river's winding turns and churning rapids, working together to power through the obstacles. Laughter and adrenaline are guaranteed as you get soaked and have the ride of your life.

Who It's For

Saluda Outdoor River Company is ideal for mid-size teams (12-30 people) seeking an active, adventurous way to build camaraderie. The physical challenge of conquering the river brings colleagues closer together and provides plenty of talking points back at the office.
