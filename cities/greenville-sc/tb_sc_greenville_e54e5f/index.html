Avenue

4.8★ (108 reviews)

Avenue in Greenville, SC is a flexible event venue that excels at hosting engaging team building experiences. From interactive scavenger hunts that get groups exploring the city, to creative workshops that spark collaboration, the dedicated staff curates activities to bring teams closer together.

What to Expect

When your group arrives at Avenue, they'll be greeted by a helpful events coordinator who will provide an overview and set the tone for the experience. Activities are designed to get people moving, thinking, and interacting in new ways - whether that's piecing together a puzzle against the clock or participating in a friendly (but fierce) game tournament.

Who It's For

Avenue's team building offerings are ideal for mid-size groups of 20-50 looking to strengthen bonds, improve communication, and encourage creative problem-solving. The varied options cater to both competitive and collaborative teams, making it a versatile choice for companies, nonprofits, and other organizations.
