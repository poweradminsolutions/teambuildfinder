Timber Ridge Axe Throwing

5.0★ (167 reviews)

Timber Ridge Axe Throwing in Seneca, SC provides a truly unique and thrilling team building experience. Groups learn the art of axe throwing from experienced instructors, then put their new skills to the test in a friendly competition to see who can hit the bullseye most consistently.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive a safety briefing and hands-on instruction from the Timber Ridge team. You'll then have 90 minutes to practice your technique and compete against coworkers, friends, or family members. The lively, social atmosphere makes for an unforgettable shared challenge.

Who It's For

This activity works well for medium-sized teams (15-30 people) looking to bond over a shared physical activity. The competitive element and skill-building nature of axe throwing tends to break down barriers between participants faster than a typical team building exercise.
