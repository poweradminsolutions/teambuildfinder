Urban Air Trampoline and Adventure Park

4.4★ (2341 reviews)

Urban Air transforms corporate team building into an adrenaline-pumped playground experience across their sprawling Miami trampoline complex. Teams tackle everything from competitive dodgeball tournaments to challenging obstacle courses and high ropes adventures. The multi-zone layout lets groups rotate through different activities, keeping energy high and engagement consistent throughout the session.

What to Expect

Your group splits into teams for structured activities like trampoline dodgeball, relay races through foam pits, and collaborative challenges on the ropes course. Sessions typically run 90 minutes to 2 hours with a dedicated party host coordinating rotations between zones. The format emphasizes friendly competition while requiring genuine teamwork to navigate obstacle courses and climbing walls successfully.

Who It's For

Ideal for energetic teams of 12-30 people who want something more active than traditional team building. The physical challenges naturally level the playing field between all personality types and job levels, making it especially effective for mixed departments or newly formed teams.
