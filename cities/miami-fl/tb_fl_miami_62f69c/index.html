Unarthodox

4.9★ (38 reviews)

Unarthodox in Miami, FL offers a one-of-a-kind team building experience through their innovative art workshops and improv classes. Groups can tap into their creative side while bonding over shared challenges and laughter.

What to Expect

Participants can expect a highly interactive, immersive experience led by talented local artists and comedians. The activities are designed to encourage collaboration, communication, and out-of-the-box thinking in a fun, low-pressure environment.

Who It's For

Unarthodox's team building activities work well for mid-sized groups (15-40 people) looking to break out of the typical offsite. The shared creative journey helps teams connect on a deeper level and bring new energy back to the workplace.
