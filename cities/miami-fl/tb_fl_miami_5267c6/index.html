Ayesha's Kitchen

5.0★ (107 reviews)

Ayesha's Kitchen in Miami, FL offers an immersive culinary experience that brings teams together through the joy of cooking. With their expert instructors, groups of all skill levels dive into hands-on lessons that teach classic techniques and global flavors.

What to Expect

Participants will work collaboratively in small teams to prepare a multi-course menu, practicing essential skills like knife work, seasoning, and plating. The session culminates in a shared meal where everyone enjoys the fruits of their labor.

Who It's For

Ayesha's Kitchen's team building classes are perfect for mid-sized groups (12-30 people) looking to bond over a shared challenge. The interactive format encourages communication, problem-solving, and a healthy dose of friendly competition.
