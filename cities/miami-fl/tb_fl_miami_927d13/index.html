Briza on the Bay

4.5★ (227 reviews)

Briza on the Bay is a premier event venue in vibrant Miami, Florida that specializes in transforming corporate retreats and team building activities into unforgettable experiences. With its stunning waterfront setting and expertly-facilitated workshops, Briza provides the perfect backdrop for groups to bond, collaborate, and push their boundaries in a supportive environment.

What to Expect

At Briza, team building goes beyond the typical icebreakers and trust falls. Groups can participate in engaging, purpose-driven activities like wilderness survival challenges, charitable team volunteer projects, or high-energy indoor/outdoor games that encourage problem-solving and friendly competition. The experienced on-site facilitators ensure each experience is tailored to the group's needs and goals.

Who It's For

Briza's versatile spaces and programming make it an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 participants) seeking a memorable team building retreat. Whether celebrating a company milestone, strengthening cross-departmental bonds, or simply looking to boost morale, Briza's unique activities and stunning bayfront setting provide the perfect backdrop for groups to connect, communicate, and cultivate camaraderie.
