Pottery studio 1 - Pottery classes and events

4.9★ (594 reviews) $$$ 2 hours

Tucked away in the heart of vibrant Miami, Pottery Studio 1 offers a unique and engaging team building experience like no other. With their expertly-led pottery classes and workshops, groups can unleash their creativity, bond over a shared challenge, and walk away with handcrafted works of art to commemorate the occasion.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by the studio's friendly instructors who will provide a quick tutorial on pottery-making techniques. From there, everyone will dive in and get their hands dirty, shaping, sculpting, and decorating their very own ceramic pieces. The collaborative nature of the class encourages teamwork and communication as participants support one another through the creative process.

Who It's For

Pottery Studio 1's team building activities are perfect for groups of 10-30 looking to step away from the office and tap into their artistic sides. Whether you're planning a company off-site, a client appreciation event, or just a fun team outing, this hands-on experience is sure to foster camaraderie and lasting memories.
