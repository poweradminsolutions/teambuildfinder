Treetop Trekking Miami

4.1★ (373 reviews) $$ 30 min - 2h30

Treetop Trekking Miami offers an exhilarating adventure in the heart of the Magic City. With challenging ropes courses and obstacle challenges suspended high above the ground, this is a team-building activity that will push your group to new heights, both literally and figuratively.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Treetop Trekking, you'll gear up with safety harnesses and receive thorough training from the experienced guides. Then it's time to take on a series of suspended platforms, bridges, and zip lines that wind through the treetops. The courses are designed to get your heart racing as you and your teammates work together to navigate the obstacles.

Who It's For

Treetop Trekking is perfect for medium-sized teams (15-40 people) looking for a unique and active team-building experience. The shared sense of accomplishment as you conquer the courses will bring your group closer together and break down barriers more effectively than a standard office happy hour.
