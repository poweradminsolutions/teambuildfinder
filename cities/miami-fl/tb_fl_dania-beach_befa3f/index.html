Gasper Arts Center

4.8★ (65 reviews)

Dania Beach's Gasper Arts Center is a dynamic hub where teams can immerse themselves in creativity and collaboration. Their facilitated art workshops tap into the power of artistic expression to foster team bonding and problem-solving skills.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a hands-on, guided experience in mediums like painting, ceramics, or printmaking. Expert instructors lead the group through fun, skill-building activities that encourage teamwork and innovation. No prior art experience is necessary - just an open mind and a willingness to get a little messy.

Who It's For

Gasper Arts Center's team building workshops are ideal for mid-sized groups (10-30 people) looking to break out of the office and explore their artistic sides together. The shared creative process tends to break down barriers and build camaraderie, making it a great choice for corporate retreats, department offsites, or any team that needs to strengthen their bonds.
