KuuPottery

5.0★ (19 reviews)

Located in the heart of Hialeah, KuuPottery provides a unique and engaging team building experience through hands-on pottery workshops. Their expert instructors guide groups through the entire process, from selecting clay to shaping and glazing their own one-of-a-kind creations.

What to Expect

When your team visits KuuPottery, they'll dive right into the pottery-making process. After a brief introduction, everyone will select their clay and begin sculpting their pieces under the watchful eye of the skilled staff. Once the initial shaping is complete, the group will learn glazing techniques before their works are fired in the kiln.

Who It's For

KuuPottery's pottery workshops are perfect for teams of all sizes looking to tap into their creative sides. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, birthday party, or just want to bring your department closer together, this tactile, collaborative activity will foster camaraderie and team spirit.
