Miami Culinary Institute

4.4★ (37 reviews)

The Miami Culinary Institute in downtown Miami offers team-building experiences that bring groups together through the shared joy of cooking. Aspiring chefs will guide your team through hands-on lessons in whipping up delectable Latin-inspired dishes, from savory empanadas to sweet churros. This interactive, results-oriented activity is a surefire way to foster camaraderie and communication.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be outfitted with aprons and chef's hats before diving into a lively cooking competition. Guided by experienced instructors, you'll work together to prepare a multi-course meal, putting your culinary skills and teamwork to the test. The activity culminates in a shared feast where you can enjoy the fruits of your labor.

Who It's For

The Miami Culinary Institute's team building classes are ideal for mid-size groups (10-30 people) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, a holiday party, or simply want to treat your employees to a unique team outing, this hands-on experience is sure to bring your group closer together.
