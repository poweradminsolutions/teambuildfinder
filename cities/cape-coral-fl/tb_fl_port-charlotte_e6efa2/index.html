Bisque IT Studio

4.8★ (262 reviews)

Bisque IT Studio in Port Charlotte, FL offers a unique team building experience with their hands-on art and craft workshops. Unleash your group's creativity through guided projects like pottery painting, glass fusing, and canvas painting that encourage collaboration and problem-solving skills.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by the studio's friendly staff who will provide a brief orientation. They'll then guide you through the chosen creative activity, offering step-by-step instructions and assistance as needed. The structured format allows everyone to participate actively and see tangible results by the end of the session.

Who It's For

Bisque IT Studio's team building activities work well for groups of 10-30 looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The relaxed, judgment-free environment is ideal for companies wanting to foster more connection and camaraderie between colleagues.
