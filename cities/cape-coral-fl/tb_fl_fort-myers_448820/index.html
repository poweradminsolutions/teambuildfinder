PEYKO VENUE

4.4★ (47 reviews) $$$ 2 hours

Nestled in the heart of Fort Myers, Florida, PEYKO VENUE offers a unique and immersive retreat experience for team building. This former movie studio has been transformed into a versatile event space, providing an inspiring backdrop for groups to come together, bond, and push their boundaries.

What to Expect

At PEYKO VENUE, teams can embark on a collaborative, team-focused retreat filled with a variety of engaging activities. From scavenger hunts that encourage problem-solving and communication to guided outdoor adventures that foster trust and camaraderie, the experienced staff ensures that every group leaves feeling more connected and motivated.

Who It's For

PEYKO VENUE is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 members) looking to step away from the office and engage in a dynamic, energy-filled team building experience. The diverse range of offerings caters to both active and reflective groups, making it a great fit for a wide variety of organizations and occasions.
