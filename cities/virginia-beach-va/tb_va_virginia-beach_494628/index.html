The Bunker Brewpub

4.6★ (670 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Virginia Beach, The Bunker Brewpub is a local favorite that offers a fantastic team building experience. With its extensive selection of craft beers and delectable American-style fare, this brewpub creates a lively and engaging atmosphere perfect for groups looking to bond over shared interests and healthy competition.

What to Expect

When you visit The Bunker, your team can participate in a variety of activities, including wine, beer, and cocktail tastings led by knowledgeable staff. The pub also hosts regular trivia nights and other game-based events that encourage collaboration and friendly rivalry among colleagues.

Who It's For

The Bunker Brewpub is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 members) seeking a memorable team building experience. The casual, laid-back vibe and interactive activities make it ideal for fostering camaraderie and breaking down barriers between departments.
