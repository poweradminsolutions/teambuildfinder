Adventureworks Zipline Virginia Beach

5.0★ (2896 reviews) $$ 90 minutes

Adventureworks Zipline brings the adrenaline rush of soaring through the treetops to Virginia Beach, combining their signature zipline courses with challenging high ropes obstacles. Teams navigate elevated platforms and aerial challenges together, creating natural opportunities for trust-building and mutual encouragement. The outdoor adventure format pushes groups beyond their comfort zones while fostering genuine camaraderie.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety briefings and equipment before tackling a series of zipline runs and ropes course elements designed to progressively build confidence. Participants work through obstacles at varying heights, with team members naturally becoming spotters and cheerleaders for each other. The experience typically flows from ground-level trust exercises to increasingly challenging aerial elements.

Who It's For

Ideal for active teams of 8-25 who want to bond through shared physical challenges rather than typical indoor activities. The format works especially well for groups with mixed fitness levels, as participants can choose their comfort level while still supporting teammates.
