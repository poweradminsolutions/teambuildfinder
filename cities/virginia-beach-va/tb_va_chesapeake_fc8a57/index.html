Glazenfyre

4.4★ (117 reviews)

Glazenfyre in Chesapeake, VA offers a unique and creative team building experience with their pottery workshops. Gather your group and let their expert instructors guide you through the process of designing and crafting your own handmade pottery pieces, fostering collaboration and camaraderie in a relaxed, artistic setting.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, you'll be welcomed into Glazenfyre's inviting studio space and provided with all the necessary materials and tools. The instructor will walk your team through the basics of pottery-making, from shaping the clay to decorating your creations. Everyone will have the chance to get their hands dirty and unleash their inner artist, working together to bring their designs to life.

Who It's For

Glazenfyre's pottery workshops are perfect for mid-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The hands-on, collaborative nature of the activity encourages communication, problem-solving, and a healthy dose of friendly competition as colleagues work together to craft their masterpieces.
