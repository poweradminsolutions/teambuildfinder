Now You're Cooking Culinary Studio

4.7★ (65 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Chesapeake, VA, Now You're Cooking Culinary Studio offers an unparalleled team building experience through the art of collaborative cooking. This dynamic venue invites groups to don their aprons and work together to prepare a delectable multi-course meal, fostering camaraderie and problem-solving skills in the process.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through a hands-on cooking class led by the studio's expert instructors. Working together at fully equipped stations, you'll chop, sauté, and plate your way to a gourmet feast, learning essential culinary techniques along the way. The interactive format encourages communication, creativity, and a healthy dose of friendly competition.

Who It's For

Now You're Cooking is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a unique, engaging team building activity. The shared experience of preparing a meal from scratch promotes bonding, as colleagues discover new talents and connect over a mutual love of food.
