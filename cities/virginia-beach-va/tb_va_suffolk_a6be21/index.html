Hub 757

4.8★ (317 reviews)

Located in the heart of Suffolk, Virginia, Hub 757 is a versatile event venue that has become a go-to destination for team building activities. With an open, industrial-chic space and a variety of interactive experiences, this venue offers groups the perfect setting to foster camaraderie, problem-solving, and friendly competition.

What to Expect

At Hub 757, team building groups can participate in a range of activities, from fast-paced scavenger hunts that have them dashing around the venue, to collaborative building challenges that put their creativity and communication skills to the test. The experienced staff is adept at tailoring the experience to the group's needs and goals, ensuring an engaging and productive outing.

Who It's For

Hub 757's team building offerings are well-suited for mid-sized groups of 15 to 40 participants. Whether you're looking to build cross-departmental bonds, boost morale, or simply have some fun together, the diverse activity options and flexible space make this venue an excellent choice for a wide range of team building occasions.
