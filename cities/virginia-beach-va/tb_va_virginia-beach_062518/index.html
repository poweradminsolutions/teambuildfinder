The Attic at Waterman's

4.8★ (52 reviews)

The Attic at Waterman's in Virginia Beach offers an array of team-building experiences that immerse groups in the great outdoors. From facilitated workshops to environmental cleanup projects, this versatile event venue empowers teams to collaborate, problem-solve, and make a tangible impact on the local community.

What to Expect

Groups can expect hands-on activities that get everyone moving, communicating, and working together toward a common goal. The Attic's experienced facilitators guide teams through carefully curated exercises, whether it's strategizing in a high-stakes escape room or rolling up their sleeves for a beach cleanup along the Virginia coast.

Who It's For

The Attic at Waterman's is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 participants) looking to strengthen bonds, boost morale, and foster a spirit of camaraderie. The venue's flexible, nature-inspired setting lends itself well to teambuilding retreats, volunteer projects, and other collaborative experiences that challenge groups in a refreshing, memorable way.
