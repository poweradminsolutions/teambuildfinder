Grand Affairs

4.4★ (444 reviews)

Located in vibrant Virginia Beach, Grand Affairs offers a diverse array of team building experiences that bring groups closer through shared challenges and meaningful collaboration. From facilitated workshops that develop leadership and communication skills to hands-on volunteer projects that give back to the community, the versatile event spaces at Grand Affairs provide the perfect backdrop for teams to bond and grow together.

What to Expect

When you book a team building event at Grand Affairs, your group will dive into carefully curated activities designed to foster teamwork and camaraderie. Whether it's solving escape room puzzles under the pressure of the clock or rolling up your sleeves for an environmental cleanup along the beach, the dedicated staff will guide your team through an engaging, high-energy experience tailored to your goals.

Who It's For

Grand Affairs is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 participants) seeking a memorable team building adventure. The versatile event spaces and diverse activity options make it easy to find the right fit, whether you're looking to ignite some friendly competition, cultivate leadership skills, or give back to the local community.
