Holiday Inn Express & Suites VA Beach Oceanfront by IHG

4.1★ (1571 reviews)

This oceanfront hotel in Virginia Beach transforms its conference facilities into versatile team building spaces with stunning Atlantic views. Teams can book dedicated meeting rooms for workshops, strategy sessions, and collaborative activities while enjoying the relaxed coastal atmosphere. The combination of professional amenities and beachside location creates an ideal escape from the typical office environment.

What to Expect

Your group will work in well-equipped conference rooms with modern audiovisual capabilities and plenty of natural light from ocean-facing windows. Sessions typically run in flexible blocks, allowing teams to break onto the nearby beach or oceanfront deck between activities. The hotel's event coordinators help arrange room setups, catering, and timing to match your team's specific objectives.

Who It's For

Ideal for corporate teams seeking a change of scenery that combines productivity with relaxation. The oceanfront setting works particularly well for leadership retreats, annual planning sessions, or groups celebrating major milestones who want to mix business with a mini-getaway atmosphere.
