Adventure Calls Outfitters, Inc.

4.7★ (176 reviews)

Adventure Calls Outfitters in Castile, NY is the premier destination for teams seeking an exhilarating white-water rafting experience. With expert guides leading the way, your group will navigate the rushing waters of the Genesee River, working together to overcome the challenges of Class III and IV rapids. This is a true test of teamwork and communication that will have your colleagues high-fiving by the end of the trip.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, you'll receive a comprehensive safety briefing and all the necessary gear, including helmets and lifejackets. Then it's time to hop in the raft and put your paddling skills to the test. Your guide will lead you through the most thrilling sections of the river, calling out instructions and encouraging collaboration to navigate the rapids successfully. Prepare to get soaked and have the time of your life.

Who It's For

Adventure Calls is perfect for medium-sized teams of 15-30 looking to bond over a shared adrenaline-fueled experience. The teamwork required to conquer the river will break down barriers between departments and leave your colleagues with a profound sense of accomplishment.
