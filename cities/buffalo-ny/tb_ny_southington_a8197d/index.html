Montana Nights Axe Throwing - Southington

4.8★ (121 reviews) 75 min

Escape the city and head to Montana Nights Axe Throwing in Southington, where your team can channel their inner lumberjacks and compete in a thrilling axe throwing tournament. This high-energy activity is perfect for breaking the ice and fostering camaraderie through a shared challenge.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, you'll receive a safety briefing and basic instruction on proper axe throwing technique. Then it's time to step up to the targets and put your aim to the test. Teams will go head-to-head in a series of rounds, cheering each other on as they work to land bullseyes and rack up points.

Who It's For

Montana Nights is ideal for medium-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to inject some friendly competition and physical activity into their team building agenda. The shared experience of learning a new skill together creates an opportunity for colleagues to bond in a unique way.
