Lock & Key Escape Room

4.8★ (365 reviews) $$ 60 Minute

Located in the heart of Buffalo, New York, Lock & Key Escape Room offers teams an unforgettable challenge that brings colleagues together through a shared experience of problem-solving and critical thinking. This unique escape room experience tests groups' ability to collaborate, communicate, and think creatively under time pressure.

What to Expect

When your team arrives, you'll be ushered into a themed room and given 60 minutes to work together, search for clues, solve puzzles, and ultimately escape before time runs out. The immersive, interactive environment encourages teamwork and teambuilding, as groups put their skills to the test.

Who It's For

Lock & Key Escape Room works well for medium-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to build camaraderie and trust through a shared challenge. The high-energy, competitive nature of the activity tends to break down barriers between departments and foster stronger cross-functional collaboration.
