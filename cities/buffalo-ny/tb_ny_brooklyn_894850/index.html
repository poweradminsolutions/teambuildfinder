Major R. Owens Health & Wellness Community Center

4.5★ (367 reviews)

Located in the heart of Brooklyn, the Major R. Owens Health & Wellness Community Center provides an engaging space for teams to come together and give back. Through their diverse volunteer and charity-focused programs, groups can roll up their sleeves and make a tangible difference in the local community.

What to Expect

Volunteer projects at the center run the gamut from packaging and distributing food for local food banks, to revitalizing community gardens, to hosting arts and crafts sessions for underprivileged youth. The staff curates meaningful, hands-on activities that encourage teamwork, communication, and a sense of shared purpose.

Who It's For

The Major R. Owens Center is an excellent choice for mid-size to large teams (15-50 people) looking to bond through charitable work. The collaborative nature of the volunteer projects helps break down silos between departments and build empathy across an organization.
