Bury the Hatchet Axe Throwing Brooklyn

5.0★ (8014 reviews)

Transform your team's competitive spirit into something memorable with guided axe throwing sessions in Brooklyn. Professional coaches teach proper technique before groups rotate through lanes, creating an unexpectedly social atmosphere where precision matters more than strength. The combination of skill-building and friendly competition makes for genuinely engaging team dynamics.

What to Expect

Teams receive safety briefings and throwing instruction before splitting into smaller groups at individual lanes. Sessions typically run 90 minutes with coached practice time followed by tournament-style competitions between colleagues. The venue handles scoring and provides different game formats to keep energy high throughout the experience.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-30 people who want something more active than typical corporate outings. The accessible learning curve and competitive element work particularly well for groups mixing different departments or seniority levels.
