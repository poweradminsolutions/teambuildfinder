Buffalo School of Culinary Arts & Hospitality Mgmnt

4.8★ (45 reviews) 1-2 hours

Located in the heart of Buffalo, New York, the Buffalo School of Culinary Arts & Hospitality Management offers a unique team-building experience that brings groups together through the shared joy of cooking. Participants will learn hands-on culinary skills from expert instructors while also strengthening communication and collaboration within their team.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be divided into smaller teams and guided through the preparation of a multi-course meal. Working together to chop, sauté, and plate, your team will put their culinary skills to the test as they race against the clock to deliver a delicious final product. The interactive, high-energy atmosphere encourages teamwork and creative problem-solving.

Who It's For

This activity works exceptionally well for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to bond over a shared challenge. The cooking class format allows colleagues to connect on a personal level, all while developing new skills they can apply back in the office.
