Eataly

4.2★ (6267 reviews)

Eataly transforms team building into an immersive Italian culinary adventure where groups learn authentic cooking techniques from professional chefs. This iconic New York marketplace combines hands-on pasta making, bread crafting, and regional cooking workshops with the energy of a bustling Italian food hall. Teams work together to create complete meals while exploring the nuances of Italian cuisine in a vibrant, market-style setting.

What to Expect

Your group will gather in Eataly's dedicated cooking spaces where chef instructors guide you through preparing traditional Italian dishes from scratch. Teams typically work in small stations, collaborating on everything from rolling fresh pasta to shaping artisanal bread, then sit together to enjoy the meal you've created. The experience flows seamlessly between active cooking, learning about ingredients sourced from the adjacent market, and communal dining.

Who It's For

Ideal for food-loving teams of 12-25 people who want a sophisticated yet interactive experience. The collaborative cooking format naturally encourages communication and teamwork while the shared meal creates lasting memories beyond typical corporate activities.
