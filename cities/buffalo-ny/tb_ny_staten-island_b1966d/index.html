Silk Road Ceramics Studio

5.0★ (98 reviews)

Nestled on Staten Island, Silk Road Ceramics Studio offers a hands-on art experience perfect for team building. Groups can learn the art of pottery making, crafting personalized pieces under the guidance of experienced instructors. With a focus on creative collaboration, this activity encourages teamwork, communication, and a shared sense of accomplishment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, teams will dive right into the creative process. After a brief introduction to ceramic techniques, everyone will select their clay and tools, then work together to design and construct their own pottery. The instructors provide step-by-step coaching throughout, ensuring participants of all skill levels can fully participate.

Who It's For

Silk Road Ceramics Studio is an ideal team building option for mid-sized groups of 10-25 looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The collaborative nature of the pottery workshops helps break down barriers between co-workers, while the tangible end product leaves teams with a lasting memento of their team building experience.
