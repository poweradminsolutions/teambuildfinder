The Caz

4.5★ (157 reviews)

The Caz in Buffalo, New York is a one-of-a-kind entertainment destination that offers a wide range of team building activities perfect for corporate groups. From high-energy improv shows to friendly trivia competitions, this versatile venue has something to engage and energize any team.

What to Expect

When you book a team event at The Caz, your group will enjoy a unique and immersive experience. For example, the venue's virtual escape rooms challenge teams to work together, think creatively, and solve puzzles under time pressure. Or get the whole crew laughing with an interactive comedy show led by talented local improvisers.

Who It's For

The Caz is an ideal choice for mid-size to large teams (15-50 people) looking to bond over shared experiences. The diverse activity options make it easy to find the right fit, whether you're planning an offsite, a holiday party, or just a fun team outing.
