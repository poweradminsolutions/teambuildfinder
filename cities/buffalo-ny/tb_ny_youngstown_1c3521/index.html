Niagara Outfitters

5.0★ (139 reviews) $$ 1-2 hours

Niagara Outfitters in Youngstown, NY offers an exhilarating array of team building activities on the water and in the great outdoors. From guided kayak tours through the scenic Niagara River Gorge to full-day whitewater rafting adventures, groups can challenge themselves physically and mentally while bonding over shared experiences.

What to Expect

When you book a team outing with Niagara Outfitters, your group will receive expert instruction and equipment before setting out on the water or trails. Depending on the package, you might paddle tandem kayaks, navigate Class III rapids, or embark on a scenic bike ride through the region's rolling hills and forests.

Who It's For

Niagara Outfitters' team building activities work well for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to push their limits in a fun, supportive environment. The camaraderie and problem-solving required to complete these challenges together can quickly build trust and communication between coworkers from different departments.
