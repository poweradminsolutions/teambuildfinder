Fulcrum Adventures

4.3★ (19 reviews)

Fulcrum Adventures in Culver City, California offers an exhilarating high ropes course and team-building activities that challenge groups to work together in problem-solving and trust-building exercises. The experienced guides are experts at designing customized programs that foster communication, leadership, and camaraderie.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Fulcrum Adventures, you'll start with a safety briefing before gearing up and taking on the high ropes challenge course. Teams must navigate obstacles and traverse bridges suspended 30 feet in the air, all while supporting and encouraging one another to conquer their fears and reach new heights.

Who It's For

This is an ideal team-building activity for medium-sized groups (15-40 people) looking to push their boundaries in a fun, high-energy setting. The shared triumph of completing the course together tends to break down barriers and build trust faster than a typical office happy hour.
