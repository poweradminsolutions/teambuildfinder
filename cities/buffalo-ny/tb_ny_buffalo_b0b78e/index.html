Tappo at the Museum

4.8★ (32 reviews)

Located in the heart of downtown Buffalo, Tappo at the Museum offers a truly unique team building experience. With its stunning event space inside the renowned Albright-Knox Art Gallery, this venue provides an inspiring backdrop for groups to collaborate, bond, and unleash their creativity.

What to Expect

Tappo's team building packages include catered dining experiences, interactive art workshops, and immersive scavenger hunts that encourage teams to explore the museum's world-class collection. The hands-on activities are facilitated by expert staff, ensuring everyone stays engaged and has fun while strengthening interpersonal connections.

Who It's For

Tappo's team building offerings are perfect for mid-size to large groups (25-100 people) looking to infuse their next offsite or retreat with a dose of culture and artistry. The shared experience of creating art or solving challenges together tends to break down barriers between departments and build trust in a way that typical team bonding exercises cannot.
