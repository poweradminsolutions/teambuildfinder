Labyrinth Workshop

5.0★ (23 reviews) $$ 2 hours

Nestled in the heart of Buffalo, New York, Labyrinth Workshop offers a truly unique team-building experience. Their art and craft workshops challenge groups to collaborate, think creatively, and bond over shared challenges in a relaxed, inspiring setting.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through a hands-on project, whether it's crafting ceramic tiles, weaving colorful wall hangings, or experimenting with abstract painting techniques. The structured yet open-ended format encourages communication, problem-solving, and a playful sense of exploration.

Who It's For

Labyrinth Workshop's workshops work particularly well for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to step away from the office and engage in something imaginative and team-oriented. The shared creative journey tends to break down barriers between departments in a refreshing way.
