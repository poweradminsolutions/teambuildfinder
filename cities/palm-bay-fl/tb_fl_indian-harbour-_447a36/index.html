Harbour House Oceanfront Venue

4.9★ (259 reviews)

Situated right along the Florida coastline, Harbour House Oceanfront Venue in Indian Harbour Beach provides the perfect setting for team-building retreats. With its stunning oceanfront views, serene natural environment, and versatile indoor/outdoor spaces, this venue offers an inspiring backdrop for groups to reconnect, strategize, and bond.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of team-building activities, from guided nature walks and kayaking excursions to collaborative art projects and professional development workshops. The on-site event staff works closely with each group to curate a customized experience that aligns with their specific goals and needs.

Who It's For

Harbour House is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a rejuvenating coastal retreat. The combination of outdoor adventures and structured teambuilding exercises creates an opportunity for colleagues to step away from the office, build trust, and return to work feeling reenergized.
