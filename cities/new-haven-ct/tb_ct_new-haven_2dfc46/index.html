Yale Peabody Museum

4.8★ (2645 reviews)

The Yale Peabody Museum transforms its world-class natural history collections into engaging art workshop experiences for corporate groups visiting New Haven. Teams work alongside museum educators to create nature-inspired artwork using specimens, fossils, and scientific illustrations as their muse. The combination of Yale's academic prestige and hands-on creativity makes for memorable team building that feels both educational and genuinely fun.

What to Expect

Your group will be guided through the museum's galleries before settling into a dedicated workshop space with art supplies and natural specimens. Teams typically work in small groups of 4-6 people, creating collaborative pieces inspired by dinosaur fossils, mineral collections, or evolutionary dioramas. The session concludes with teams presenting their artwork and discussing what they learned about both creativity and collaboration.

Who It's For

Ideal for professional teams of 12-30 people who want something more intellectually stimulating than typical team building activities. The museum setting appeals especially to groups in healthcare, education, or research fields who appreciate the scientific backdrop.
