Annex Y.M.A. Club

4.3★ (121 reviews)

Tucked away in the heart of New Haven, the Annex Y.M.A. Club offers a versatile and dynamic venue for team building activities. From spirited sports tournaments to lively trivia nights, this community hub provides a unique opportunity for groups to bond over shared challenges and friendly competition.

What to Expect

At the Annex Y.M.A. Club, teams can participate in a range of interactive experiences, from organizing a bingo tournament to coordinating a high-energy scavenger hunt. The flexible event spaces allow for seamless transitions between activities, ensuring a smooth and engaging team building session from start to finish.

Who It's For

The Annex Y.M.A. Club is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 participants) looking to break out of the traditional office setting and embrace a more energetic, social approach to team building. Whether planning a corporate retreat or a department-wide bonding event, the club's diverse offerings cater to groups seeking a memorable and collaborative experience.
