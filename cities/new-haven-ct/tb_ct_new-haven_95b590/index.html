Escape New Haven

4.8★ (577 reviews) 60 minutes

Escape New Haven in downtown New Haven, CT offers an exciting and immersive escape room experience perfect for team building. Groups of 2-12 must work together under pressure to solve a series of challenging puzzles and clues in order to 'escape' the room before time runs out.

What to Expect

When you arrive, your team will be locked in a themed room and given just 60 minutes to find the way out. You'll need to search for hidden items, crack codes, and think creatively to progress through the experience. Clear communication and collaboration are key to success.

Who It's For

Escape New Haven is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (8-40 players) looking to build camaraderie and problem-solving skills. The shared challenge of escaping the room fosters teamwork, critical thinking, and healthy competition in a fun, contained environment.
