Family Fun Center

4.5★ (2206 reviews)

Family Fun Center transforms team building into a multi-activity adventure across its expansive Lakeland facility. Groups can tackle laser tag battles, compete in miniature golf tournaments, and challenge each other in arcade competitions, all under one roof with casual dining to fuel the fun.

What to Expect

Teams rotate through different activity stations, typically starting with laser tag matches that divide groups into competing squads navigating the arena's obstacles and barriers. The experience flows into mini-golf challenges and arcade tournaments, with pizza and refreshments available between activities to keep energy levels high.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 12-30 who want variety and friendly competition without intense physical demands. The mix of individual skill games and team-based laser tag creates natural conversation starters and lets different personality types shine.
