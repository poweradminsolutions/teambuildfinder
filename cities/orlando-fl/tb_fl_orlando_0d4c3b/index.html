Grand Bohemian Orlando, Autograph Collection

4.4★ (3176 reviews)

The Grand Bohemian Orlando transforms team retreats into sophisticated experiences combining luxury accommodations with professional workshop spaces. This upscale hotel specializes in multi-day corporate retreats where teams can work intensively during the day, then unwind at the rooftop pool or explore the on-site art gallery together.

What to Expect

Your group will have access to dedicated meeting spaces for facilitated workshops, with the flexibility to break into smaller discussion areas throughout the hotel's artistic common spaces. Sessions typically blend structured activities with informal networking time, taking advantage of the property's restaurant and spa amenities for team bonding between workshop segments.

Who It's For

Ideal for executive teams and leadership groups seeking an elevated retreat experience that balances serious professional development with luxury amenities. The sophisticated atmosphere works particularly well for companies wanting to reward high performers while investing in strategic planning sessions.
