Pottery U Paint

4.8★ (602 reviews)

Pottery U Paint in Longwood, FL is a unique team building destination where groups get hands-on with clay and paint. With friendly instructors guiding the way, your team will create their own custom pottery pieces from start to finish in a laidback, creative atmosphere.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be seated at pottery wheels or tables stocked with clay, glazes, and tools. Everyone will get to try their hand at shaping their own pottery creations, from mugs and bowls to decorative pieces. After the clay is formed, you'll move on to painting and decorating your pieces with vibrant glazes.

Who It's For

This experience works well for teams of 10-40 looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The pottery-making process encourages collaboration and brings out each person's artistic side, making it a great option for team building events, company parties, or staff retreats.
