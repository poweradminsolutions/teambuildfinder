The Kitchen House

5.0★ (49 reviews)

The Kitchen House in Orlando, FL offers a unique and engaging team building experience through their hands-on cooking classes. With a focus on collaborative meal preparation, groups work together to plan, prepare, and enjoy a delicious multi-course feast in a beautiful garden setting.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through a menu planning session to select the dishes they'll prepare. Then it's off to the kitchen, where you'll work together to chop, sauté, and plate each course. The final result is a family-style meal that you get to enjoy together, along with a sense of accomplishment from conquering the culinary challenge as a group.

Who It's For

The Kitchen House's cooking classes are perfect for mid-size teams (12-30 people) looking to bond over a shared creative activity. The collaborative nature of the experience helps break down barriers and build camaraderie, making it an excellent choice for company retreats, department outings, or any occasion where you want your group to work together in a fun, relaxed environment.
