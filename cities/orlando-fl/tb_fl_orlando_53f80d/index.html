Social Link Room Events Venue

5.0★ (14 reviews) 5 hours, plus 2 additional free

The Social Link Room in downtown Orlando, Florida offers a unique team building experience with their facilitated workshops and retreats. Groups can expect an engaging, high-energy atmosphere as they collaborate to solve challenges and puzzles in a stylish, modern event space.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by the Social Link Room's experienced facilitators who will guide you through a custom team building activity. You might find yourselves racing against the clock to escape a themed room, working together to construct a Rube Goldberg machine, or using creative problem-solving to complete a series of interactive tasks.

Who It's For

The Social Link Room's team building activities are ideal for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds, boost morale, and have fun in the process. Whether celebrating a company milestone or needing to get colleagues from different departments collaborating, this venue delivers an unforgettable shared experience.
