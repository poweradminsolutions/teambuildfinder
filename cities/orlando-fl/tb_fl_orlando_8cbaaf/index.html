Wave Of Wellness

5.0★ (19 reviews)

Situated in the heart of Orlando, Wave of Wellness offers a unique team-building experience that combines the thrill of water sports with an opportunity to give back. Their guided kayak and canoe tours on the picturesque lakes and rivers of Central Florida provide a refreshing way for groups to bond, challenge themselves, and make a positive environmental impact.

What to Expect

Groups start their day with a brief safety orientation before embarking on a guided tour of the local waterways. Along the way, they'll have the chance to paddle in sync, navigate through obstacle courses, and participate in a guided shoreline cleanup, collecting trash and debris to help preserve the natural beauty of the area.

Who It's For

This activity works well for medium-sized teams (15-30 people) looking to step out of the office and try something physically engaging. The shared challenge of navigating the water and working together to clean up the environment encourages collaboration, communication, and a sense of camaraderie that will last long after the outing.
