Truffles & Trifles

3.9★ (75 reviews)

Truffles & Trifles in Orlando, FL offers a delicious twist on team building. Groups of all sizes can roll up their sleeves and learn the secrets to crafting mouthwatering pastries and desserts from the school's expert instructors. It's a fun, hands-on way to bond over a shared love of baking.

What to Expect

During a Truffles & Trifles team building class, your group will be guided through the process of preparing and decorating multiple sweet treats. Whether it's truffles, trifles, or something else entirely, you'll work together to master techniques like tempering chocolate, piping buttercream, and assembling elegant desserts.

Who It's For

Truffles & Trifles is a great option for teams of 10-30 who want to get out of the office and try something new. The structured, collaborative format is perfect for bringing coworkers closer, and the indulgent end products make for a tasty team-building experience.
