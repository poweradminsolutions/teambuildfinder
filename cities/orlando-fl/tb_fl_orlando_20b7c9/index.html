The Brass Axe

4.9★ (286 reviews)

Located in the heart of Orlando, The Brass Axe offers a thrilling and unique team building experience. Unleash your inner lumberjack with their state-of-the-art axe throwing range, where you and your colleagues can compete in a friendly, high-energy tournament. For a more tactical challenge, the venue also features an intense laser tag arena, perfect for strategic group missions.

What to Expect

When you arrive at The Brass Axe, you'll receive a brief safety orientation before trying your hand at axe throwing. An experienced instructor will teach you the proper technique, then you'll split into teams and see who can land the most bullseyes. After working up an appetite, head to the laser tag course for an adrenaline-fueled battle across multiple themed arenas.

Who It's For

The Brass Axe is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to bond over a shared physical challenge. The competitive yet lighthearted atmosphere helps break down barriers between departments, making it a fantastic option for company offsites, holiday parties, or any event where you want to energize your crew.
