K1 Speed Orlando

4.4★ (3400 reviews)

K1 Speed Orlando delivers high-octane team building through competitive electric go-kart racing that gets everyone's adrenaline pumping. Teams compete in multiple races on a professionally designed indoor track, with lap times displayed in real-time to fuel friendly rivalry. The electric karts level the playing field since they don't require prior racing experience, making it accessible for colleagues of varying comfort levels.

What to Expect

Your group will start with a safety briefing and helmet fitting before hitting the track for qualifying laps to determine starting positions. The main event consists of multiple races where teams can compete individually or in relay format, with live timing displays showing who's leading. Between races, groups can grab food and drinks while reviewing lap times and trash-talking their colleagues' driving skills.

Who It's For

Ideal for competitive teams of 8-25 people who want an active, engaging experience that brings out everyone's playful side. The combination of individual performance and team dynamics works particularly well for sales teams or groups looking to energize relationships through friendly competition.
