The Mad Potter

4.6★ (168 reviews)

The Mad Potter in Houston, TX provides a unique and hands-on team building experience through pottery classes and craft workshops. Unleash your group's creativity as you design and decorate your own ceramic pieces, fostering collaboration and camaraderie in a laid-back, artistic atmosphere.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be welcomed by the Mad Potter's knowledgeable instructors who will guide you through the process of selecting, molding, and painting your pottery. Whether you're shaping a one-of-a-kind mug or decorating a decorative plate, the interactive nature of the activity encourages teamwork and problem-solving as you bring your creations to life.

Who It's For

The Mad Potter's team building offerings are perfect for groups of 10-30 looking to step away from the desk and tap into their artistic side. The shared experience of crafting pottery cultivates a fun, relaxed environment that's ideal for bonding across departments or celebrating a special occasion.
