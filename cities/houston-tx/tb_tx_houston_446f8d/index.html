Sur La Table

4.7★ (809 reviews)

Located in the heart of Houston, Sur La Table offers teams a unique culinary adventure. Their expert-led cooking and baking classes provide a hands-on, collaborative experience that brings groups together through the shared challenge of mastering new recipes and techniques.

What to Expect

At Sur La Table, your team will don aprons and work together to prepare a multi-course meal or baking project. Guided by experienced chef instructors, you'll learn professional tips and tricks while honing your communication and problem-solving skills. The interactive format encourages friendly competition and camaraderie as you work towards a delicious final product.

Who It's For

These classes are ideal for corporate events, birthday celebrations, and girls' nights out. The intimate, 10-30 person format fosters teamwork in a fun, relaxed setting - perfect for mid-size groups looking to strengthen bonds through a unique shared experience.
