The Groundlings Theatre & School

4.7★ (317 reviews)

The Groundlings Theatre & School in Los Angeles offers a truly unique team building experience like no other. Embracing the art of improv comedy, groups get to flex their creative muscles and learn to think on their feet through a series of fun, high-energy exercises and games.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by a seasoned Groundlings instructor who will guide you through a 2-hour improv workshop. You'll start with some icebreakers to get everyone comfortable, then dive into collaborative improv exercises that encourage listening, risk-taking, and spontaneous problem-solving.

Who It's For

This activity is perfect for teams looking to foster better communication, build trust, and inject more playfulness into their workplace culture. The Groundlings' intimate, low-pressure environment makes it an ideal choice for groups of 10-30 seeking an unforgettable team building adventure.
