Escape Hotel Hollywood

4.6★ (800 reviews)

Escape Hotel Hollywood transforms team building into an immersive adventure across 12 elaborately themed escape rooms in Los Angeles. Your group will navigate everything from carnival mysteries to high-stakes espionage missions, with each room designed to challenge different problem-solving skills and communication styles.

What to Expect

Teams split into smaller groups of 4-8 people and get assigned to different themed rooms simultaneously, then rotate through multiple experiences during your session. Each room presents unique puzzles, props, and storylines that require collaborative thinking and clear communication to solve within the time limit.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 12-50 people who want an engaging challenge that reveals natural leadership styles and communication patterns. The variety of themes means there's something to spark every personality type, making it particularly effective for cross-departmental groups.
