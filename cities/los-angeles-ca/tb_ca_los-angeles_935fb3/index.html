Hospitality Training Academy Kitchen

4.7★ (52 reviews)

The Hospitality Training Academy Kitchen in Los Angeles offers an immersive team building experience that lets groups get hands-on with the culinary arts. Aspiring chefs and bartenders can collaborate to prepare a multi-course meal or custom cocktail menu, developing new skills while bonding over the shared challenge.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, teams are guided through a private lesson with experienced instructors. They'll work together to plan a menu, prep ingredients, and bring their creations to life in the academy's state-of-the-art kitchen. The activities are designed to encourage communication, problem-solving, and creative thinking - all while having fun.

Who It's For

This is an excellent option for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to spice up their next offsite or team outing. The competitive yet cooperative nature of the cooking and mixology challenges helps break down barriers and build camaraderie, making it a popular choice for company parties, department retreats, and other group events.
