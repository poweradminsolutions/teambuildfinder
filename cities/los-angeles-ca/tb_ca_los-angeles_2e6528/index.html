Team Rubicon National Headquarters

4.5★ (10 reviews)

Headquartered in the heart of Los Angeles, Team Rubicon is a one-of-a-kind volunteer organization that empowers teams to make a real difference. With a focus on disaster response and veteran reintegration, their team building activities provide a unique opportunity to contribute to meaningful causes while fostering camaraderie and collaboration.

What to Expect

When you book a team building experience with Team Rubicon, your group will dive into hands-on projects that could include packing emergency supply kits, assembling temporary shelters, or supporting community clean-up efforts. The activities are designed to be engaging, educational, and leave a lasting positive impact.

Who It's For

Team Rubicon's team building programs are perfect for mid-size to large groups (15-100 people) looking to bond over a shared sense of purpose. Whether you're celebrating a company milestone or want to strengthen interdepartmental relationships, the team's well-structured activities and passion for service create an unforgettable experience.
