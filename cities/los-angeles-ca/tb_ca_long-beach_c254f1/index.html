Bad Axe Throwing Long Beach

4.9★ (274 reviews)

Based in the heart of Long Beach, California, Bad Axe Throwing offers a unique and thrilling team building experience. Groups of all sizes can try their hand at the art of axe throwing, honing their skills in a safe and supervised environment. The expert instructors will guide you through the basics, ensuring everyone feels comfortable before letting the competition commence.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive a comprehensive safety orientation and training session. You'll learn proper throwing techniques and have the chance to practice before embarking on a series of fun, competitive challenges. The experience is structured to encourage collaboration, communication, and a healthy dose of friendly rivalry between team members.

Who It's For

Bad Axe Throwing is an excellent option for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a physically engaging, adrenaline-fueled team building activity. The shared experience of mastering a new skill helps to break down barriers and foster stronger bonds between colleagues from different departments.
