Rage Ground

4.7★ (676 reviews) 2 hours

Rage Ground transforms workplace stress into team bonding through destructive therapy sessions where groups safely smash and demolish everyday objects. This Los Angeles venue provides protective gear and breakable items ranging from electronics to furniture, creating an unconventional outlet that builds camaraderie through shared cathartic release. The controlled chaos environment surprisingly fosters communication as teams channel frustration into something constructive together.

What to Expect

Teams gear up in safety equipment before entering demolition rooms stocked with breakable items like printers, dishes, and furniture. Armed with sledgehammers, baseball bats, and crowbars, participants take turns or work together to destroy objects while music pumps in the background. Sessions typically run 30-60 minutes with debriefing time afterward where groups often find themselves laughing and connecting over the shared experience.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams dealing with high-pressure environments or major transitions who need a physical stress release. Works particularly well for groups of 8-20 people who aren't afraid to get their hands dirty and want an experience they'll still be talking about months later.
