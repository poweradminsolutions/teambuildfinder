K1 Speed Torrance

4.5★ (2476 reviews)

K1 Speed Torrance brings high-octane team building to Gardena with electric go-kart racing that gets everyone's adrenaline pumping. Teams compete on a professional indoor track where natural leaders emerge and office hierarchies disappear behind racing helmets. The combination of individual skill and group competition creates memorable bonding moments that translate back to the workplace.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety briefings and helmets before hitting the indoor electric track for multiple racing sessions. Teams typically compete in qualifying rounds followed by head-to-head races, with real-time lap timing adding competitive edge. The facility includes a viewing area where teammates can cheer each other on between races, plus food service to extend the team experience.

Who It's For

Ideal for competitive teams of 8-25 people who want an active, memorable experience that breaks from traditional meeting rooms. The racing format naturally creates talking points and friendly rivalries that energize team dynamics long after the checkered flag.
