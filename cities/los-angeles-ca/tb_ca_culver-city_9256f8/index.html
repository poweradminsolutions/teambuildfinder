Feet First Eventertainment - Los Angeles Team Building

5.0★ (87 reviews)

Feet First Eventertainment in Culver City offers a dynamic lineup of team building activities that bring groups together through laughter, friendly competition, and shared experiences. From scavenger hunts that send teams racing around the city to improv workshops that boost creativity and communication, this team delivers innovative, high-energy events that leave teams feeling energized and connected.

What to Expect

When you book with Feet First, their experienced facilitators will work with you to customize the perfect team building itinerary for your group. Trivia challenges, casino nights, and charitable giveback projects are just a few of the engaging options they provide. Participants can expect a well-organized flow, clear instructions, and plenty of opportunities for teamwork and camaraderie.

Who It's For

Feet First's activities are ideal for mid-size to large teams (15-100 people) looking to strengthen bonds, foster collaboration, and ignite some friendly competition. Their dynamic events work well for corporate retreats, holiday parties, and new hire onboarding - any occasion where you want to energize your crew and create lasting memories.
