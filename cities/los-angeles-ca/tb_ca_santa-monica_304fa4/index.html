LifeRush Adventures

4.9★ (110 reviews)

LifeRush Adventures in Santa Monica offers a thrilling range of outdoor team building activities that push groups to work together and connect in dynamic new ways. From heart-pumping water sports to challenging wilderness hikes, these experiences are designed to get teams out of their comfort zones and into a shared adventure.

What to Expect

When your group arrives at LifeRush, you'll be outfitted with the necessary gear and safety equipment before embarking on your chosen activity. Whether you opt for a whitewater rafting expedition or a coastal nature hike, you can expect plenty of hands-on teamwork, problem-solving, and adrenaline-fueled fun.

Who It's For

LifeRush Adventures is ideal for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking an active, collaborative team building experience. The shared challenges and victories of these outdoor outings tend to foster stronger bonds and deeper understanding between co-workers faster than any hotel conference room exercise.
