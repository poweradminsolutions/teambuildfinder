Taglyan Complex

4.7★ (309 reviews)

Taglyan Complex in Los Angeles is a one-of-a-kind venue that takes team building experiences to the next level. With its elegant event spaces and world-class catering, this complex provides a seamless backdrop for immersive, facilitated workshops and off-site retreats that foster collaboration and morale.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of team-focused activities, from high-energy escape room challenges to guided meditation sessions. The expert event staff works closely with each client to curate a customized itinerary that meets the unique needs and goals of the team.

Who It's For

Taglyan Complex is an ideal choice for mid-to-large corporate groups (20-100 people) seeking an upscale yet functional setting for their team building events. The versatile spaces and comprehensive services make it easy to plan a memorable experience that brings colleagues together in a fresh, energizing environment.
