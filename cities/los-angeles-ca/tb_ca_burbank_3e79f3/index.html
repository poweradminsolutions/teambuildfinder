LA Connection Comedy Theatre | Improv Shows & Classes

4.7★ (116 reviews)

The LA Connection Comedy Theatre in Burbank, CA offers a uniquely engaging team building experience through their improv comedy shows and classes. Groups can bond over the shared thrill of taking the stage, experimenting with spontaneous scenes, and supporting each other's comedic skills.

What to Expect

Improv workshops challenge teams to think on their feet, collaborate creatively, and tap into their innate sense of play. The experienced instructors guide participants through fun, low-pressure exercises that build trust and communication. Teams then showcase their newfound improv abilities in a live performance for the group.

Who It's For

The LA Connection is an ideal team building venue for groups of 10-40 looking to shake up their routine and try something truly memorable. The interactive nature of improv is a fantastic way for colleagues to break out of their shells, learn about each other's strengths, and strengthen workplace relationships.
