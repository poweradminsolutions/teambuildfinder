Kick Axe Throwing BROOKLYN

4.7★ (1571 reviews)

Kick Axe Throwing BROOKLYN combines the primal satisfaction of hurling sharp objects with the relaxed atmosphere of a well-designed sports bar. Teams gather in Brooklyn's flannel-decorated venue to learn proper throwing techniques before competing in tournaments that reveal surprising talent and create lasting memories. The combination of skilled instruction and full bar service makes this more engaging than typical corporate outings.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety briefings and throwing instruction from experienced coaches before rotating through private axe-throwing lanes. Teams typically spend 90 minutes alternating between competitive rounds and socializing in the lounge area with craft cocktails and bar food. The format encourages natural mixing as colleagues cheer each other's bullseyes and laugh off wild throws.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-25 people who want something more interactive than dinner but less intense than escape rooms. The activity works especially well for groups with mixed athletic abilities since technique matters more than strength.
