Palmetto Place Event Hall

4.7★ (36 reviews)

Located in the heart of downtown Columbia, SC, Palmetto Place Event Hall offers a versatile and well-equipped space for team building activities. With a focus on facilitated workshops, retreats, and volunteer/charity events, this venue provides a dynamic and engaging environment for groups looking to strengthen bonds, foster collaboration, and give back to the community.

What to Expect

When your team visits Palmetto Place, you can expect a seamless and well-organized experience. The in-house event coordinators work closely with you to curate a tailored program, whether it's a half-day workshop, a full-day retreat, or a hands-on volunteer project. The flexible layout and diverse amenities allow for a wide range of team-focused activities, from interactive problem-solving exercises to outdoor team-building challenges.

Who It's For

Palmetto Place is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 participants) looking to ignite their creativity, build trust, and develop essential leadership skills. The variety of activity options and the venue's commitment to fostering a collaborative, inclusive environment make it a standout choice for corporate groups, nonprofit organizations, and academic institutions alike.
