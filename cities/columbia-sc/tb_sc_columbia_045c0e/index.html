Venture X Downtown Columbia SC

5.0★ (27 reviews)

Venture X in downtown Columbia, SC offers an impressive array of team-building activities that tap into the area's thriving entrepreneurial spirit. From facilitated workshops on innovation and design thinking to high-energy hackathons, this flexible event space has the resources and expertise to bring your group together in dynamic, productive ways.

What to Expect

When you book an experience at Venture X, you can expect a seamlessly-executed agenda tailored to your team's goals. Their facilitators are skilled at creating an engaging flow, whether it's guiding your group through a virtual escape room challenge or getting the creative juices flowing with hands-on design exercises. Ample communal spaces and high-tech amenities make the venue a versatile choice for teams of all sizes.

Who It's For

Venture X is an excellent option for medium to large-sized teams (15-50 people) seeking an elevated, innovative approach to team building. The variety of activity types caters to groups with diverse interests, from analytical problem-solvers to free-spirited creatives. The shared sense of achievement cultivated through these experiences is unmatched.
