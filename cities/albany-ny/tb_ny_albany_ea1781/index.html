Albany Ghosts: Ghost Tours and Haunted Pub Crawls

5.0★ (33 reviews)

Albany Ghosts offers a thrilling and immersive team-building experience in the historic city of Albany, New York. Their guided ghost tours and haunted pub crawls provide a spooky yet fun way for groups to bond over shared scares and local lore.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a 90-minute walking tour through Albany's most haunted hotspots, led by experienced guides who bring the city's ghostly past to life. Alternatively, the haunted pub crawl takes teams on a spirited exploration of the city's oldest watering holes, where they'll encounter eerie tales and enjoy themed libations.

Who It's For

These activities work well for teams of 10-40 looking to try something a little different. The spooky yet social nature of the experiences helps break the ice and foster camaraderie, making them ideal for office outings, team retreats, or any group seeking a unique bonding adventure.
