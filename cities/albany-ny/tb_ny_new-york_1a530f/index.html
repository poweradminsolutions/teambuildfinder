Cubico Offices

4.5★ (109 reviews)

Cubico Offices in New York City offers an inspiring coworking space for teams seeking an energetic, collaborative environment. With a range of activity areas and access to virtual office services, this versatile venue is ideal for group workshops, strategic planning sessions, or team-building exercises.

What to Expect

Groups can book private meeting rooms or reserve the open-concept common areas for hands-on activities. The staff are on hand to help facilitate teamwork challenges, icebreakers, or brainstorming exercises tailored to your group's goals.

Who It's For

Cubico Offices is well-suited for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds and get creative juices flowing. The dynamic workspace and array of resources make it a great option for corporate retreats, offsite meetings, or any occasion that calls for a productive, yet fun team experience.
