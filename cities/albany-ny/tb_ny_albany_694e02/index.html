Woman's Club of Albany

4.6★ (32 reviews)

The Woman's Club of Albany in upstate New York offers a unique team building experience with their immersive retreats. Groups can expect a full day of carefully curated activities that challenge them both mentally and physically, all while fostering collaboration and communication in a supportive environment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, teams dive into a series of interactive workshops and group exercises designed to build trust, improve problem-solving, and enhance leadership skills. The day includes a catered lunch and concludes with a fun, competitive team challenge that gets everyone out of their comfort zones.

Who It's For

The Woman's Club retreats work best for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds and break down silos between departments. The shared experience of tackling creative problem-solving tasks together tends to break the ice faster than a typical team happy hour.
