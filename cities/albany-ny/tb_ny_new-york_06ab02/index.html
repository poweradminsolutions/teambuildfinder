LASER SHOW USA Corp

5.0★ (265 reviews)

LASER SHOW USA Corp in New York City offers a truly immersive team building experience with their cutting-edge laser light shows and interactive drumming sessions. As one of the city's premier event production companies, they've perfected the art of creating unforgettable team-bonding activities.

What to Expect

Groups start the experience by learning the basics of drum circle facilitation, working together to create a rhythmic soundscape. The real magic happens when the lights dim and the lasers ignite, inviting participants to get lost in the mesmerizing display while continuing to contribute to the collective sound. It's a uniquely engaging way to foster collaboration and communication.

Who It's For

LASER SHOW USA's team building activities are perfect for medium to large groups (15-50) looking to break out of their routine and try something unexpected. The shared sense of wonder and accomplishment makes this an excellent choice for offsite retreats, holiday parties, or any occasion where you want to energize and unite your team.
