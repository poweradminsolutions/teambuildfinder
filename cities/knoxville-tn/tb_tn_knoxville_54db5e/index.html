World's Fair Park

4.7★ (3808 reviews)

World's Fair Park transforms Knoxville's most iconic landmark into a distinctive backdrop for corporate retreats and outdoor team building experiences. Groups can combine facilitated workshops with nature-based activities throughout the 266-acre grounds, using everything from the historic Sunsphere to secluded walking trails as venues for connection and collaboration.

What to Expect

Teams typically start with structured activities in one of the park's designated areas, then move through different zones for varied experiences—perhaps problem-solving exercises near the amphitheater followed by reflective discussions along the Tennessee River waterfront. The mix of urban park amenities and natural spaces allows facilitators to shift between high-energy group challenges and quieter team development conversations.

Who It's For

Ideal for mid-size to large teams seeking a retreat atmosphere without leaving the city. The combination of indoor and outdoor options makes it particularly valuable for full-day or multi-day programs where weather flexibility matters.
