Emporium Center

4.8★ (314 reviews)

The Emporium Center transforms team building through hands-on art workshops in a beautifully restored historic venue in Knoxville. Groups work alongside local artists in spacious studios, creating collaborative pieces while exploring different mediums from painting to mixed media. The inspiring gallery setting naturally sparks creativity and conversation among colleagues.

What to Expect

Teams rotate through artist-led stations, experimenting with various techniques while working on individual pieces or group installations. Professional facilitators guide the creative process, helping participants step outside comfort zones without the pressure of producing gallery-worthy art. Sessions typically conclude with a group showcase where everyone shares their work and creative insights.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and groups seeking a reflective, collaborative experience rather than high-energy competition. The intimate studio atmosphere works particularly well for smaller teams of 8-25 people who want meaningful interaction time.
