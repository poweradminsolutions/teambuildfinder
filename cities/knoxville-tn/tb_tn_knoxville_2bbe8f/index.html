The Cutting Edge Classroom Cooking School

4.8★ (757 reviews)

The Cutting Edge Classroom transforms team building into a collaborative culinary adventure where coworkers don aprons and work together to create restaurant-quality meals. This Knoxville cooking school specializes in hands-on group experiences that blend professional instruction with the natural bonding that happens when teams tackle recipes together. Groups leave with new skills, shared memories, and usually a fantastic meal they prepared themselves.

What to Expect

Teams are divided into cooking stations where they collaborate on different components of a multi-course menu under guidance from professional chef instructors. The format typically includes knife skills demonstrations, cooking technique instruction, and coordinated timing to bring all dishes together for a group dining experience. Most sessions conclude with everyone sitting down to enjoy the meal they created together.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams seeking a collaborative rather than competitive experience, especially groups of 8-25 people. The shared goal of creating a successful meal naturally encourages communication and cooperation between colleagues who might not typically work together.
