Ancient Lore Village

4.4★ (715 reviews)

Ancient Lore Village transforms corporate retreats into immersive experiences that blend team building with rustic hospitality in the hills surrounding Knoxville. Groups work through facilitated workshops and DEI training sessions while staying overnight at this unique venue that combines hotel amenities with retreat center programming. The setting encourages deeper conversations and relationship-building that's hard to achieve in traditional conference rooms.

What to Expect

Teams typically arrive for multi-day retreats that mix structured workshop sessions with informal bonding time between activities. Professional facilitators guide groups through targeted exercises while the village setting provides natural opportunities for organic team interactions during meals and evening downtime. Most programs include overnight accommodations and shared dining experiences that extend the team building beyond formal session hours.

Who It's For

Ideal for organizations planning multi-day leadership retreats or companies serious about investing in diversity, equity, and inclusion training. The residential format works best for teams comfortable with overnight stays and extended time together away from the office.
