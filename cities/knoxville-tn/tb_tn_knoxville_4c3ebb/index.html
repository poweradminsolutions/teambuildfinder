Knoxville Coliseum

4.5★ (11 reviews)

The Knoxville Coliseum in Knoxville, Tennessee is a versatile venue that offers thrilling team building experiences. From high-energy sports tournaments to engaging trivia nights, this multipurpose facility can bring any group together through friendly competition and shared challenges.

What to Expect

At the Knoxville Coliseum, teams can compete in a variety of sports like basketball, volleyball, or even dodge ball. For a more cerebral activity, the venue also hosts interactive trivia events where coworkers put their knowledge to the test in a lively, collaborative setting.

Who It's For

The Knoxville Coliseum is an excellent choice for medium to large-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking an active, adrenaline-fueled team building outing. The shared experience of working together towards a common goal, whether on the court or solving trivia, is a surefire way to strengthen bonds and communication across your organization.
