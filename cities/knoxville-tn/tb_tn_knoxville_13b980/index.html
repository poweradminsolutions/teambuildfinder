Escapeez Escape Room

5.0★ (9 reviews)

Escape rooms in Knoxville don't get more thrilling than Escapeez. This locally-owned venue challenges teams to put their wits to the test, working together against the clock to crack codes, solve puzzles, and ultimately escape the themed rooms before time runs out. With multiple immersive scenarios to choose from, Escapeez offers a unique brand of team-building that's equal parts fun and strategic.

What to Expect

When you arrive, your group will be briefed on the rules and objective of your chosen escape room. You'll then be locked inside and have 60 minutes to use your collective problem-solving skills to make your grand escape. Along the way, you'll need to communicate, collaborate, and think critically to overcome a series of cleverly-designed challenges.

Who It's For

Escapeez's escape rooms are ideal for mid-sized teams (10-20 players) looking to strengthen bonds and sharpen their teamwork skills. The high-pressure, time-sensitive nature of the activity encourages participants to step out of their comfort zones and rely on one another, making it a uniquely effective team building experience.
