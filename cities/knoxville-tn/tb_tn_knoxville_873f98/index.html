The Regas Building

4.4★ (8 reviews)

The Regas Building in Knoxville, Tennessee offers a dynamic setting for team building workshops and innovation sessions. Housed in a historic structure, this versatile training center provides the ideal blend of modern amenities and creative inspiration to help groups tackle challenges, spark new ideas, and strengthen bonds.

What to Expect

Facilitated workshops at the Regas Building guide teams through interactive exercises that blend design thinking, ideation, and problem-solving. Flexible spaces allow for a variety of formats, from large group discussions to small breakout sessions, ensuring an engaging flow that keeps participants energized and productive.

Who It's For

The Regas Building's team building programs are well-suited for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to push the boundaries of their creative potential. Whether planning a corporate offsite or a strategic planning session, the venue's combination of thoughtful facilitation and innovative space creates an environment primed for breakthrough thinking.
