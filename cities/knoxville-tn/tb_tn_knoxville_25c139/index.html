Clarence Brown Theatre

4.8★ (444 reviews)

Clarence Brown Theatre transforms University of Tennessee's acclaimed performance space into an interactive team building laboratory through professionally-led improv workshops. Corporate groups work directly with theater artists to develop spontaneous collaboration skills and creative thinking through comedy exercises and scene work. The Knoxville venue's intimate setting creates an environment where colleagues can step outside comfort zones together while building genuine connections through shared laughter.

What to Expect

Teams participate in structured improv games that progress from simple warm-ups to collaborative scene creation, with professional facilitators guiding groups through exercises designed to enhance listening, adaptability, and trust. Sessions typically include 'Yes, and...' activities, character development challenges, and group storytelling that requires real-time cooperation and creative problem-solving. The experience concludes with teams performing short scenes they've developed together, celebrating collective creativity rather than individual performance.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 12-25 who want to break down communication barriers and develop creative confidence together. The improv format works especially well for groups dealing with change management or looking to improve spontaneous collaboration skills in a supportive, laughter-filled environment.
