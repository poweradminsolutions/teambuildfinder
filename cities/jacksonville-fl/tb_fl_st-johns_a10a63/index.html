Game Over Escape Rooms Jacksonville

5.0★ (2999 reviews) 60 mins

Game Over Escape Rooms delivers immersive puzzle-solving challenges that force teams to communicate under pressure and pool their diverse skills to beat the clock. Their St Johns location features multiple themed rooms where groups must work together to uncover clues, crack codes, and escape within 60 minutes. The experience reveals natural leaders and hidden talents while creating genuine moments of collaboration that translate back to the workplace.

What to Expect

Teams are briefed on their chosen scenario, then locked into a themed room filled with puzzles, hidden objects, and interconnected clues. A game master monitors progress and provides hints when groups get stuck, ensuring everyone stays engaged rather than frustrated. Most sessions run about 75 minutes total including the briefing and debriefing discussion.

Who It's For

Ideal for small to medium teams of 4-12 people who want an intense shared challenge without physical demands. The time pressure and problem-solving format work especially well for analytical groups and teams that need to improve their communication under stress.
