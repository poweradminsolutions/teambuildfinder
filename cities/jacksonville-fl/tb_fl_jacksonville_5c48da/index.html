Garden Club of Jacksonville

4.7★ (157 reviews)

The Garden Club of Jacksonville offers a unique team building experience with a focus on environmental conservation. Situated in the heart of Florida's largest city, this nonprofit organization invites groups to get their hands dirty while making a real difference in the local community.

What to Expect

Visiting teams will spend several hours working together to clean up public green spaces, plant native flora, and maintain community gardens. It's a hands-on, collaborative activity that gets everyone outdoors and working towards a shared goal.

Who It's For

This team building activity is ideal for eco-conscious companies and organizations looking to give back. Groups of 10-30 will find the work rewarding and the camaraderie-building aspects extremely beneficial.
