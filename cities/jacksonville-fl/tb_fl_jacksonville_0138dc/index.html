The Cove Social Club

4.8★ (23 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Jacksonville, The Cove Social Club offers a unique and engaging team building experience with its lively trivia and game nights. This social club provides a dynamic setting for groups to come together, bond, and unleash their competitive spirits in a fun and laid-back atmosphere.

What to Expect

When you arrive at The Cove, your team will be greeted with a selection of classic board games, trivia cards, and interactive challenges. The hosts will guide you through team-based games and competitions that encourage collaboration, critical thinking, and a healthy dose of friendly rivalry.

Who It's For

The Cove's team building activities are perfect for mid-size groups of 15 to 40 looking to mix work and play. Whether you're planning a corporate outing, a team retreat, or just a fun night out with colleagues, the shared experience of conquering the games and trivia will bring your group closer together.
