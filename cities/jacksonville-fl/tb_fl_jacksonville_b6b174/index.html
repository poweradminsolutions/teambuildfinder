Axe Champs

5.0★ (721 reviews) $$ 1 hour

Axe Champs brings the primal satisfaction of hurling steel blades at wooden targets to Jacksonville's corporate team building scene. Groups learn proper throwing technique from experienced coaches before competing in bracket-style tournaments that reveal surprising hidden talents and create lasting bragging rights.

What to Expect

Teams rotate through multiple throwing lanes where coaches demonstrate stance, grip, and release technique before participants take turns hurling axes at wooden targets. Sessions typically progress from basic accuracy drills to competitive games like tic-tac-toe or knockout tournaments that pit colleagues against each other in friendly rivalry.

Who It's For

Ideal for groups of 8-30 who want something more memorable than another dinner outing. The novelty factor and competitive element work especially well for sales teams, departments celebrating milestones, or any group where friendly competition enhances rather than threatens relationships.
