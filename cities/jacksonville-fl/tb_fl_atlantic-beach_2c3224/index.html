JAX Little Art

5.0★ (21 reviews)

Located in the charming coastal town of Atlantic Beach, FL, JAX Little Art offers a unique team building experience through their vibrant art workshops. Aspiring artists and seasoned creatives alike will find inspiration in the studio's welcoming atmosphere and expert instruction.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a hands-on, collaborative art session tailored to their skill level and interests. Whether it's painting, pottery, or mixed media, the JAX Little Art team will guide participants through the creative process, encouraging teamwork and individual expression along the way.

Who It's For

JAX Little Art's art workshops are ideal for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The relaxed, supportive environment helps break down barriers and fosters a sense of camaraderie that can be difficult to achieve in a traditional team building setting.
