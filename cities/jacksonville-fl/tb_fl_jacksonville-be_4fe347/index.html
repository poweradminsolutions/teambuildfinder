The Rooftop Event Space

5.0★ (6 reviews)

Perched atop a historic building in the heart of Jacksonville Beach, The Rooftop Event Space offers a one-of-a-kind team building experience with stunning ocean views. From facilitated workshops to multi-day retreats, this versatile venue can accommodate groups of all sizes looking to strengthen bonds, boost morale, and foster collaboration.

What to Expect

Equipped with a covered outdoor lounge, indoor meeting spaces, and a state-of-the-art audiovisual setup, The Rooftop Event Space provides the perfect backdrop for immersive team building activities. Whether it's an intensive communication workshop or a fun-filled scavenger hunt, the experienced on-site staff will work closely with your group to customize the perfect agenda.

Who It's For

The Rooftop Event Space is an ideal choice for medium-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a memorable team building experience. The combination of unique event spaces and curated activities makes it a great option for corporate off-sites, leadership development programs, and groups looking to ignite a new sense of camaraderie.
