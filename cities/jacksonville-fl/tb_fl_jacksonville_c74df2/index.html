Class Axe Throwing

4.9★ (1315 reviews)

Class Axe Throwing transforms the ancient art of axe throwing into an engaging team building experience in Jacksonville. Groups learn proper throwing techniques from certified instructors before competing in various games and challenges. The combination of skill-building and friendly competition creates natural bonding opportunities while giving everyone something to talk about long after the session ends.

What to Expect

Your team will receive safety briefings and basic instruction on stance, grip, and throwing motion before practicing on individual targets. Sessions typically include multiple rounds of different games like tic-tac-toe, 21, and knockout tournaments that encourage team interaction. Instructors keep score and facilitate group competitions while ensuring everyone gets plenty of throwing time.

Who It's For

Ideal for groups of 10-30 people who want an active experience that builds camaraderie through shared challenges. The mix of individual skill development and team competition works particularly well for sales teams, project groups, or any workplace looking to boost morale with something completely different from the office environment.
