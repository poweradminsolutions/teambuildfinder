The Glass Factory

4.5★ (260 reviews)

The Glass Factory in Jacksonville, FL offers a truly unique team building experience. Groups can participate in guided workshops and creative retreats that tap into their artistic and problem-solving skills. The historic industrial space provides an inspiring backdrop for teamwork and camaraderie.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be greeted by the Glass Factory's experienced facilitators. They'll provide an introduction to the space and the day's activities, which could include glassblowing demonstrations, hands-on projects like terrariums or mosaics, or collaborative challenges to solve. The emphasis is on active engagement and shared accomplishment.

Who It's For

The Glass Factory is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a memorable and enriching team building event. The creative, hands-on format encourages cross-collaboration and helps break down silos between departments or disciplines.
