Worcester BrickBox Theater

4.9★ (32 reviews)

Located in the heart of Worcester, Massachusetts, the Worcester BrickBox Theater offers an immersive improv and comedy experience perfect for team building. Performers guide groups through interactive exercises and sketch challenges, fostering collaboration, quick-thinking, and plenty of laughs.

What to Expect

When your team visits the Worcester BrickBox, they'll start with a brief warm-up before diving into a 90-minute improv session. Participants work together in small groups to build short scenes and characters, with the performers providing guidance and feedback to help everyone get comfortable on stage.

Who It's For

The Worcester BrickBox's team building activities work well for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to break out of their shells and bond over a shared experience. The playful, low-pressure environment is ideal for bringing colleagues closer together, regardless of their comfort level with public speaking.
