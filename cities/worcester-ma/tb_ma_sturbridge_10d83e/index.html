Escape the Pike (Escape Room Games)

4.9★ (216 reviews)

Escape the Pike in Sturbridge, MA offers an exhilarating escape room experience that challenges teams to put their problem-solving skills to the test. With immersive themed rooms and clever puzzles, this venue provides a dynamic and engaging team-building activity that brings coworkers closer together.

What to Expect

Groups are locked in a themed room and have 60 minutes to work collaboratively, decipher clues, and escape before time runs out. The experience requires clear communication, critical thinking, and creative problem-solving as teams navigate the various challenges presented.

Who It's For

Escape the Pike is perfect for mid-sized teams (10-20 people) looking for a high-energy, interactive team-building activity. The competitive yet supportive environment encourages employees to step outside their comfort zones and learn to rely on one another to achieve a common goal.
