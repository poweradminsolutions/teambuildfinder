Two Tails Pottery Studio

4.8★ (61 reviews)

Northborough's Two Tails Pottery Studio is the perfect spot for team building through the creative medium of pottery. Guided by experienced instructors, groups of up to 20 can explore the art of hand-building, wheel throwing, and glazing to create unique pieces together.

What to Expect

Sessions begin with a brief introduction to pottery techniques, then participants dive in to shape their own creations from scratch. The hands-on, collaborative nature of the class encourages teamwork and communication as co-workers work side-by-side at the wheel or sculpting tables.

Who It's For

Two Tails is ideal for mid-size teams (10-20) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The relaxed, supportive atmosphere and tangible end products make it a great choice for company offsites, holiday parties, or simply getting to know your colleagues better.
