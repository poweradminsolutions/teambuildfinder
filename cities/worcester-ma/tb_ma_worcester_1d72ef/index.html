The Harvest Cup

4.9★ (36 reviews)

The Harvest Cup in Worcester, MA provides a unique team-building experience that combines the thrill of athletic competition with the camaraderie of an old-fashioned harvest festival. Groups can expect a lively outdoor event featuring classic autumnal activities like pumpkin carving, corn hole, and a spirited pie-eating contest.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be split into teams and guided through a rotating series of fun, collaborative challenges. The tasks emphasize problem-solving, communication, and good-natured rivalry, all set against the backdrop of a charming historic farm. Prepare to get a little messy and work up an appetite for the hearty home-cooked meal served at the end.

Who It's For

The Harvest Cup is perfect for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking to bond over shared experiences that are equal parts silly and strategic. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat or a community organization outing, this venue offers a memorable way to bring colleagues closer together.
