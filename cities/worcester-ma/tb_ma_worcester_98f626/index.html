EcoTarium

4.6★ (2511 reviews)

EcoTarium transforms team building into hands-on environmental learning through its interactive science exhibits and guided nature trails. This Worcester institution combines facilitated workshops with outdoor exploration, letting groups tackle conservation challenges while building collaboration skills. Teams can explore everything from wildlife habitats to planetarium presentations that spark meaningful discussions about problem-solving.

What to Expect

Groups typically start with a structured workshop session focused on environmental themes, then move through interactive museum exhibits that encourage collaborative exploration. The experience often includes guided nature trail activities where teams work together on conservation-focused challenges, plus access to the planetarium for shared discovery moments that naturally build connections.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 10-30 who want something educational yet engaging, especially groups in science, education, or sustainability sectors. The mix of indoor and outdoor activities works particularly well for teams looking to step away from traditional corporate environments.
