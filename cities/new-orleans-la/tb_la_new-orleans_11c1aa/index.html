Solari Orleans

4.4★ (25 reviews)

Solari Orleans in New Orleans offers a truly unique team building experience with its blend of music, movement, and facilitated workshops. Housed in a beautifully restored 19th-century building, this venue provides a dynamic and inspiring setting for groups to connect, communicate, and collaborate in creative ways.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of team building activities, including percussion-based drumming sessions, interactive leadership workshops, and guided meditation and mindfulness exercises. The experienced facilitators tailor each experience to the group's needs, ensuring a seamless and engaging flow throughout.

Who It's For

Solari Orleans is an excellent choice for teams of 15 to 40 looking to step out of their comfort zones and explore new ways of working together. The activities are designed to foster communication, build trust, and unleash creativity, making it a great option for corporate retreats, company offsites, and other team-focused events.
