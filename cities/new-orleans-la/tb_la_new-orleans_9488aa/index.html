Saenger Theatre

4.8★ (4165 reviews)

The historic Saenger Theatre transforms corporate groups into performers through interactive drumming circles and improv comedy workshops. This New Orleans landmark combines the grandeur of a 1920s theater with hands-on activities that get teams collaborating in unexpected ways. The acoustic excellence of the venue elevates simple rhythm exercises into memorable shared experiences.

What to Expect

Groups participate in guided drumming sessions where facilitators teach basic rhythms before building complex layered compositions together. Improv workshops use the theater's stage to run comedy exercises focused on listening, spontaneous collaboration, and building on teammates' ideas. Sessions typically blend instruction with performance time in the historic venue.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and groups comfortable stepping outside their comfort zone. The theatrical setting works especially well for milestone celebrations or leadership retreats where you want something more memorable than a conference room activity.
