Posh Paint Pottery

4.8★ (88 reviews) $$ 2 weeks

Posh Paint Pottery in Harahan, Louisiana offers a fun and creative team building experience for groups of all sizes. With a wide range of pottery painting projects to choose from, your team will have the opportunity to unleash their inner artists and bond over a shared creative challenge.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be guided through the pottery painting process by the knowledgeable staff. Everyone will select a ceramic piece to work on, from mugs and plates to decorative items, and then let their creativity flow with a variety of paints and brushes. The relaxed, social atmosphere encourages collaboration and conversation as teammates assist each other and share ideas.

Who It's For

Posh Paint Pottery is an excellent choice for teams looking to step away from the office and engage in a unique, hands-on activity. The pottery painting experience works well for groups of 10-30, making it a great option for corporate outings, birthday celebrations, or any occasion where your team could benefit from some creative bonding time.
