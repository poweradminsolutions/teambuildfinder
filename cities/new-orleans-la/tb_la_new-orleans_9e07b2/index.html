The Rooftop on Basin

4.8★ (56 reviews)

The Rooftop on Basin in New Orleans offers a stunning setting for team retreats and facilitated workshops. With its open-air layout and sweeping city views, this venue creates an invigorating atmosphere that encourages collaboration and creative thinking.

What to Expect

Groups can take advantage of the Rooftop's flexible indoor and outdoor spaces to host anything from strategic planning sessions to team building exercises. The expert on-site staff is skilled at designing customized agendas that blend educational content with fun, hands-on activities.

Who It's For

The Rooftop on Basin works well for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to step away from the office and bond over shared experiences. The mix of thought-provoking discussions and light-hearted competition creates an ideal environment for breaking down barriers and strengthening interpersonal connections.
