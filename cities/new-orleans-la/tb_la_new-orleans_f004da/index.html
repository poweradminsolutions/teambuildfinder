River City Venues

4.6★ (169 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of historic New Orleans, River City Venues offers a range of immersive team-building experiences that tap into the city's vibrant culture. From facilitated workshops to personality assessments and DIY innovation sessions, this versatile space helps groups connect, collaborate, and unlock their creative potential.

What to Expect

When you visit River City Venues, you'll find a modern, loft-style space that can be configured for a variety of interactive team activities. Whether you're solving mysteries in an escape room, engaging in design thinking exercises, or exploring diversity and inclusion through guided workshops, the experienced facilitators ensure a seamless, engaging process from start to finish.

Who It's For

River City Venues is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to break out of the conference room and dive into team-building with a local twist. The variety of program options makes it easy to find the right fit for your group's needs, whether you're planning a corporate retreat, an offsite strategy session, or a fun team-bonding event.
