Clue Carré - New Orleans

4.8★ (962 reviews) 60 minutes

Clue Carré transforms historic New Orleans intrigue into immersive escape room challenges that demand genuine collaboration and creative problem-solving. Teams work together to decode puzzles, uncover hidden clues, and beat the clock in themed rooms that blend the city's mysterious atmosphere with modern team building objectives.

What to Expect

Groups are briefed on their mission, then locked into elaborately designed rooms where they must communicate effectively and pool their skills to solve interconnected puzzles within a set time limit. The experience combines logical thinking, pattern recognition, and team coordination as participants search for clues, manipulate objects, and piece together solutions under gentle time pressure.

Who It's For

Ideal for small to medium teams of 4-12 people who want an engaging mental challenge that requires everyone to contribute. The shared intensity and problem-solving naturally builds trust and reveals team dynamics in ways traditional activities can't match.
