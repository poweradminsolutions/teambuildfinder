Arbor Room at Popp Fountain

4.6★ (99 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of New Orleans' City Park, the Arbor Room at Popp Fountain offers a unique team building experience that combines the beauty of nature with hands-on environmental stewardship. Groups can expect a day of hiking, botanical art workshops, and meaningful volunteer work to restore the local ecosystem.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will set out on a guided nature hike through the park's lush landscaping, taking in the sights and sounds of this urban oasis. Next, you'll participate in a botanical art class, learning techniques to capture the vibrant flora around you. The highlight is the volunteer project, where your group will work together to clean up the surrounding area and plant native species, leaving a lasting positive impact on the community.

Who It's For

This experience is perfect for teams of 15-40 looking to bond over a shared sense of purpose and environmental consciousness. The combination of physical activity, creative expression, and community service makes it a unique team building opportunity that appeals to a wide range of personalities and interests.
