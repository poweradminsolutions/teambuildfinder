Pickle N Pins

3.9★ (156 reviews)

Pickle N Pins in Metairie, LA is a unique destination that combines the classic fun of bowling and the growing craze of pickleball. This lively sports bar offers a dynamic team building experience, where colleagues can bond over friendly competition on the courts or lanes.

What to Expect

Groups can reserve pickleball or bowling packages, with dedicated staff on hand to provide instruction and facilitate team-based tournament play. The high-energy atmosphere and interactive nature of the activities encourage collaboration, problem-solving, and lots of laughter.

Who It's For

Pickle N Pins is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40) seeking a memorable, active team building outing. The combination of sports, games, and socializing creates an experience that's equal parts fun and team-strengthening.
