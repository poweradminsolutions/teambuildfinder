Findlay Kitchen

4.8★ (42 reviews)

Located in the heart of Cincinnati, Findlay Kitchen is a nonprofit community hub that offers a unique and engaging team building experience through its hands-on cooking and baking classes. With a state-of-the-art culinary facility and a team of passionate instructors, this venue provides groups a chance to bond over the shared joy of creating delicious meals together.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Findlay Kitchen, your group will be greeted by a friendly staff member who will guide you through a fun and interactive cooking or baking class. Depending on the package you choose, you might find yourself whipping up gourmet pizzas from scratch, decorating artisanal cupcakes, or learning the secrets to perfectly searing a steak. The engaging format encourages teamwork and communication as you work together to prepare and plate your creations.

Who It's For

Findlay Kitchen's team building activities are perfect for mid-sized groups (15-40 people) looking to foster camaraderie and collaboration through a shared culinary experience. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, a birthday celebration, or simply want to treat your team to a fun and unique outing, this venue offers a delicious way to bring people together and create lasting memories.
