Cincinnati Circus Company

4.7★ (162 reviews)

The Cincinnati Circus Company is a one-stop shop for all your team building and corporate event needs in the heart of Cincinnati, Ohio. From high-flying aerial acts to hilarious clowning routines, this unique venue offers a range of circus-themed experiences that are sure to bring your group closer together through shared laughter and excitement.

What to Expect

When you book an event with the Cincinnati Circus Company, your team will have the chance to try their hand at various circus skills under the guidance of experienced performers. From juggling and tightrope walking to designing and decorating their own balloon creations, each activity encourages collaboration, communication, and a healthy dose of friendly competition.

Who It's For

This is an ideal option for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to inject some playful energy into their next team building outing. The circus theme creates a lighthearted, festive atmosphere that's perfect for breaking down barriers between departments and fostering a sense of shared accomplishment.
