Full Throttle Adrenaline Park - Florence

4.8★ (2829 reviews)

Full Throttle Adrenaline Park in Florence, Kentucky is a high-octane destination perfect for team building. Get ready to channel your inner racecar driver as you and your colleagues compete on an indoor go-kart track designed with heart-pumping turns and straightaways.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive a safety briefing and instructions on operating the professional-grade electric karts. Then it's time to suit up and hit the track, where you'll navigate a thrilling layout for several high-speed races. Between heats, you can refuel with snacks and drinks at the onsite concessions.

Who It's For

Full Throttle's competitive racing experience works well for mid-size teams (15-40) seeking a physical, adrenaline-fueled challenge. The shared thrill of jockeying for position around the track is a surefire way to spark collaboration and camaraderie among colleagues.
