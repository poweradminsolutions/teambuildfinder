Bury The Hatchet Old Bridge - Axe Throwing

5.0★ (11798 reviews)

Teams sharpen their focus and competitive spirit through guided axe throwing sessions at this premier adventure venue in Matawan. Professional coaches teach proper technique before groups compete in tournaments that build camaraderie through shared triumph and good-natured ribbing. The primal satisfaction of hitting your target creates memorable moments that translate into stronger workplace connections.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety briefings and throwing instruction from certified coaches before taking turns at individual throwing lanes. Teams typically rotate through multiple rounds of competitive games, from accuracy challenges to elimination-style tournaments. The venue handles all equipment and scoring while maintaining a safe, controlled environment for beginners and experienced throwers alike.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-25 people seeking an active, memorable experience that levels the playing field regardless of job title. The mix of skill-building and friendly competition works especially well for groups that want to see each other in a completely different context.
