The Farm NYC - Day Offices - Event Venues

4.7★ (38 reviews)

The Farm NYC in Soho offers an inspiring oasis for team-building experiences in the heart of Manhattan. With a range of flexible event spaces and a proven track record for facilitated workshops and offsite retreats, this versatile venue can elevate your next group gathering.

What to Expect

Groups at The Farm can expect a tailored experience, whether it's an interactive team challenge, a guided mindfulness session, or a creative brainstorming workshop. The on-site event staff work closely with organizers to customize the flow and format to match your group's goals and preferred vibe.

Who It's For

The Farm's offerings are well-suited for mid-size teams of 15-40 seeking an offsite experience to boost collaboration, communication, and morale. The variety of space configurations and activity options make it a great choice for everything from new hire onboarding to annual holiday parties.
