Open Jar Studios

4.9★ (98 reviews)

Open Jar Studios in New York City offers hands-on art and craft workshops that are perfect for team building activities. Guided by talented local artists, your group will work together to create unique, collaborative projects that showcase your collective creativity.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, you'll be welcomed into Open Jar's bright, inspirational studio space. Your experienced instructor will provide all the necessary materials and guide your team through a fun, interactive workshop. You might find yourselves painting colorful murals, sculpting clay sculptures, or even designing custom screen-printed t-shirts - the possibilities are endless.

Who It's For

These team building workshops at Open Jar Studios are ideal for mid-sized groups of 15-40 looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. Whether you're planning a corporate offsite or a special event, the collaborative nature of the activities helps break down barriers and foster better communication among your colleagues.
