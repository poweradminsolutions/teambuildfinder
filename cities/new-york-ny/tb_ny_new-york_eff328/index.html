Big Deal Speakeasy Casino Events

4.8★ (47 reviews)

Located in the heart of NYC, Big Deal Speakeasy Casino Events offers a truly unique team building experience. Groups can embrace their competitive spirit with casino-themed games and trivia, all while sipping on expertly crafted cocktails. The stunning 1920s-inspired atmosphere adds an extra layer of excitement to the proceedings.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be immersed in the glamorous world of a prohibition-era speakeasy. After a brief tutorial, you'll dive into a lively mix of casino games like blackjack, roulette, and poker, along with trivia challenges that test your collective knowledge. It's a dynamic, fast-paced event that keeps everyone engaged and on their toes.

Who It's For

This experience works particularly well for medium to large teams (20-50 people) looking to bond over a shared challenge in an elevated, adult-friendly setting. The casino theme brings out the playful side in most participants, making it an ideal choice for corporate outings, client entertainment, or celebrating team milestones.
