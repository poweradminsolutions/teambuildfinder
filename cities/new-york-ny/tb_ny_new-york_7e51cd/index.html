Broadway Workshop

5.0★ (101 reviews)

Located in the heart of New York City, Broadway Workshop offers a one-of-a-kind team building experience through the power of improv comedy. Facilitated by seasoned performers, these high-energy sessions challenge participants to step outside their comfort zones, unleash their creativity, and forge stronger connections with their colleagues.

What to Expect

Broadway Workshop's team building activities are built around dynamic improv exercises that get everyone engaged and laughing. Groups will work together to devise and act out short comedic sketches, playing off each other's ideas and learning to think on their feet. The emphasis is on fostering spontaneity, effective communication, and a willingness to take risks in a supportive environment.

Who It's For

These improv-focused team building sessions are ideal for medium-sized corporate groups (15-40 participants) looking to inject some playfulness and levity into their teamwork. The shared experience of performing in front of peers tends to break down barriers and build camaraderie across departments, making it a great choice for onboarding, employee retreats, or other team-focused events.
