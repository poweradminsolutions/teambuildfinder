Great Big Game Show New York City

5.0★ (710 reviews)

Located in the heart of New York City, Great Big Game Show offers a one-of-a-kind team building experience that's equal parts fun and challenging. Groups are immersed in a high-energy game show environment, complete with lights, buzzers, and a lively host who keeps the energy electric throughout.

What to Expect

Teams of 4-8 participants compete head-to-head in a series of fast-paced trivia and game show-style challenges. From identifying strange products to reenacting iconic scenes, each round tests a different skill set, ensuring everyone contributes to the group's success. The experience culminates in an epic final showdown that has coworkers cheering each other on.

Who It's For

Great Big Game Show is a fantastic option for mid-size teams (10-40 people) looking to foster camaraderie and healthy competition. The shared experience of working together under pressure helps break down barriers between departments and build stronger bonds. It's an ideal activity for company offsites, holiday parties, or any occasion when you want your team to leave energized and connected.
