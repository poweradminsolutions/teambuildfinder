OASIS - Conference Center & Corporate Events, Flex Office, Meeting Space Venue & Rooftop

5.0★ (190 reviews)

OASIS is a premier conference center and corporate events venue in the heart of New York City, offering a versatile space for team building activities and more. With a modern, flexible design and top-notch amenities, this dynamic space can easily transform to accommodate a wide range of team-focused experiences.

What to Expect

Groups at OASIS can expect expertly facilitated workshops, immersive retreats, and lively hackathons. The open floor plan and technology-enabled rooms make it easy to foster collaboration, while the rooftop terrace provides a scenic backdrop for team building exercises and social gatherings.

Who It's For

OASIS is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking an elevated, professional setting for team building. The flexible event spaces and diverse activity options make it well-suited for corporate offsites, departmental retreats, and other group experiences aimed at boosting morale, enhancing communication, and strengthening workplace bonds.
