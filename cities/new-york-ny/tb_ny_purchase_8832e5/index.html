Boundless Adventures Summer Camp - Westchester's Outdoor Adventure Day Camp

4.7★ (14 reviews)

Boundless Adventures Summer Camp in Purchase, NY offers outdoor enthusiasts of all ages a chance to reconnect with nature and challenge themselves through a variety of adventure-filled activities. From hiking through the stunning Westchester countryside to friendly sports tournaments, this vibrant summer camp provides a refreshing reprieve from the everyday grind.

What to Expect

When your group arrives at Boundless Adventures, you'll be greeted by an experienced team of camp counselors ready to lead you through a custom itinerary of exciting outdoor pursuits. Depending on your group's interests, you might start the day with a vigorous hike along scenic nature trails before breaking for team-building games and sports competitions on the sprawling campgrounds.

Who It's For

Boundless Adventures is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to strengthen bonds and foster camaraderie through shared outdoor experiences. The collaborative nature of the activities encourages cross-departmental cooperation and helps break down barriers in a fun, relaxed setting.
