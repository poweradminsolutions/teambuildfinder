Hapik Industry City

4.8★ (159 reviews)

Brooklyn's Hapik Industry City offers a dynamic team building experience like no other. This rock climbing gym and indoor playground provides a thrilling vertical challenge that brings groups together through shared physical accomplishment and problem-solving.

What to Expect

At Hapik, teams will harness up and take on themed climbing walls, ropes courses, and other gravity-defying obstacles. The hands-on, interactive format gets everyone engaged and working collaboratively to conquer each challenge.

Who It's For

Hapik is an excellent choice for team outings, from birthday parties to corporate retreats. The high-energy, competitive atmosphere is perfect for mid-size groups (15-40 people) looking to build camaraderie through a unique shared experience.
