Jay Suites Wall Street - Flexible Office Space New York and Shared Office Space NYC

4.6★ (36 reviews)

Jay Suites Wall Street is a flexible office and co-working space in the heart of New York City's financial district. They offer a variety of team building activities and retreats that allow groups to step away from the daily grind and bond over shared challenges and experiences.

What to Expect

From facilitated workshops that build communication and problem-solving skills, to immersive escape room adventures that get the adrenaline flowing, Jay Suites provides a dynamic backdrop for teams to collaborate, compete, and strengthen their working relationships.

Who It's For

Jay Suites' team building offerings are well-suited for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to step out of the office and try something new together. Whether celebrating a milestone, aligning on strategic priorities, or simply breaking the routine, these activities are designed to bring people together in a fun, high-energy environment.
