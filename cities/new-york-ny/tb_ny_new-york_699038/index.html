Cipriani Wall Street | Event Venue

4.6★ (317 reviews)

Housed in a landmarked beaux-arts building on the historic Wall Street corridor, Cipriani Wall Street offers an unparalleled team building experience in the heart of New York City. From full-day retreats to virtual escape rooms, this upscale event venue has the flexibility and resources to elevate your next team-bonding activity.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of structured, facilitated teambuilding workshops that encourage collaboration, problem-solving, and friendly competition. Or dive into a high-tech virtual escape room challenge where colleagues work together remotely to crack codes and complete missions against the clock. No matter the format, Cipriani's experienced event staff will ensure your team leaves feeling energized, connected, and ready to take on new challenges.

Who It's For

Cipriani Wall Street's versatile spaces and programming make it a fantastic option for mid-size to large teams (20-100 participants) seeking an impactful team building day in New York. The venue's blend of classic elegance and modern amenities creates the perfect backdrop for corporate retreats, executive workshops, and after-work social activities that foster camaraderie and boost morale.
