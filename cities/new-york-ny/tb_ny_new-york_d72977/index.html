Emerge212 - Flexible NYC Office & Meeting Spaces - 1185 6th Ave

Emerge212 in the heart of New York City offers a versatile canvas for team building activities. With flexible event spaces and a creative staff, they can accommodate everything from facilitated workshops to high-energy hackathons, helping groups unlock their collaborative potential.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Emerge212, you'll find a modern, open-concept setting primed for hands-on teamwork. The experienced team will work with you to design a custom agenda, whether that's guiding your group through a design thinking session or setting the stage for a competitive innovation challenge.

Who It's For

Emerge212 is a great fit for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking a dynamic, purpose-built environment to tackle a shared challenge. The flexible spaces and facilitation services make it easy to bring departments together and forge new connections through creative problem-solving.
