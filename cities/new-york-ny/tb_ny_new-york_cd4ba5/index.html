Eventique: Event Production for Corporate & Global Brands

4.6★ (14 reviews)

Eventique is a premier event production company in New York City, specializing in creating memorable team building experiences for corporate and global brands. With their expert facilitation and innovative approach, they transform ordinary group activities into immersive, bond-building adventures.

What to Expect

Eventique's team building offerings range from fast-paced, collaborative escape rooms to intricate scavenger hunts that have participants solving clues and competing against the clock. Their experienced event coordinators seamlessly guide groups through the activities, ensuring a smooth and engaging flow from start to finish.

Who It's For

Eventique's team building programs are perfect for mid-to-large size corporate groups (15-100 people) looking to foster camaraderie, improve communication, and encourage creative problem-solving. Whether celebrating a milestone or cultivating cross-departmental collaboration, their customizable experiences are sure to leave a lasting impression.
