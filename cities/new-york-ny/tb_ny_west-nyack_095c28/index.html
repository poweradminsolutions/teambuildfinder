K1 Speed - Indoor Go Karts, Corporate Event Venue, Team Building Activities

4.6★ (3386 reviews)

K1 Speed delivers high-octane team building through professional indoor kart racing at their West Nyack facility. Teams compete in electric go-karts on a challenging track, creating an adrenaline-fueled environment that naturally brings out both competitive spirit and camaraderie. The combination of individual performance and team dynamics makes for memorable corporate events that get people talking long after the checkered flag.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety briefings and helmet fitting before hitting the track in electric karts that can reach impressive speeds. Most corporate packages include multiple racing sessions with timing systems that track individual and team performance, followed by awards ceremonies in their dedicated event space. The facility handles everything from race coordination to refreshment areas where teams can celebrate victories and analyze their lap times.

Who It's For

Ideal for competitive teams of 10-50 people who want high-energy bonding without requiring special skills or fitness levels. The racing format works particularly well for sales teams, engineering groups, and departments looking to channel workplace competition into something fun and harmless.
