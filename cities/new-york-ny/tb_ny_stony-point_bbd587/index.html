The Patriot Lounge | Bar & Grille - Golf Simulators, Axe Throwing & Events

4.7★ (86 reviews)

The Patriot Lounge in Stony Point, NY is a one-stop shop for team building adventures. This vibrant bar and grille features a lineup of high-energy group activities like axe throwing and go-kart racing that are sure to get the competitive juices flowing.

What to Expect

Groups can book private axe throwing sessions with expert instructors who'll teach proper technique and safety. Or race against the clock on the venue's indoor go-kart track for a heart-pounding team challenge. Between activities, teams can refuel with pub fare and craft cocktails in the lively lounge.

Who It's For

The Patriot Lounge's combination of entertainment and hospitality makes it an ideal choice for mid-size teams (15-40) looking to bond over shared thrills. The mix of physical activities and social experiences helps break down barriers and build camaraderie faster than a standard team dinner.
