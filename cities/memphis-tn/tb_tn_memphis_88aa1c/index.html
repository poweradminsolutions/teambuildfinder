Craft Axe Throwing Memphis

5.0★ (973 reviews) $$ 45 minutes to 1.5 hours

Craft Axe Throwing Memphis combines the primal satisfaction of hurling sharp steel with the social atmosphere of a sports bar, creating an unexpectedly effective team building environment. Groups learn proper throwing techniques from experienced coaches before competing in tournaments that generate plenty of laughs and friendly trash talk. The Memphis location's bar setup means teams can grab drinks between rounds, turning what could be a quick activity into a proper team outing.

What to Expect

Your group receives safety briefings and throwing instruction before being assigned lanes where you'll rotate through various axe throwing games and competitions. Coaches stay nearby to offer technique tips and keep score while your team progresses from basic target practice to head-to-head tournaments. Most sessions run about 90 minutes with time built in for drinks and socializing between rounds.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams seeking something physical yet accessible - no athletic ability required, just willingness to try something new. The competitive format naturally creates memorable moments and inside jokes that teams reference long after leaving.
