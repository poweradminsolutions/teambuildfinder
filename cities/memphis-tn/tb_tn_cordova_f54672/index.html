Funcity Adventure Park(Cordova)

4.4★ (61 reviews)

Funcity Adventure Park in Cordova, TN is a premier destination for team-building activities that will challenge, engage, and thrill your group. With a variety of ropes courses and obstacle challenges, this adventure park offers an immersive experience that fosters collaboration, communication, and camaraderie among colleagues.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Funcity, your team will be guided through a safety orientation before taking on the park's signature aerial ropes course. Working together, you'll navigate a series of platforms, bridges, and ziplines suspended high above the ground, putting your problem-solving and trust-building skills to the test.

Who It's For

Funcity Adventure Park is an excellent choice for medium-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a dynamic, hands-on team-building experience. The shared sense of accomplishment from conquering the ropes course tends to break down barriers and cultivate stronger bonds between employees from different departments.
