Crosstown Concourse

4.7★ (777 reviews)

Crosstown Concourse transforms team experiences through its unique setting as a repurposed Sears distribution center turned creative hub in Memphis. This historic landmark combines art installations, community spaces, and versatile event areas that encourage organic team interaction and collaboration. The building's industrial architecture and cultural programming create an inspiring backdrop for meaningful group connections.

What to Expect

Teams explore the building's multiple levels, discovering art galleries, maker spaces, and community areas that spark natural conversations and shared experiences. Groups can participate in guided tours highlighting the building's transformation story, engage with resident artists and entrepreneurs, or utilize flexible spaces for structured activities. The experience blends cultural discovery with relationship building in an authentic Memphis setting.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and leadership groups who appreciate unique environments over traditional corporate settings. The cultural atmosphere works particularly well for organizations wanting to inspire innovation and celebrate their community connections.
