K1 Speed - Indoor Go Karts, Corporate Event Venue, Team Building Activities

4.7★ (2629 reviews)

K1 Speed transforms corporate team building into high-octane competition with professional-grade indoor electric go-karts that reach up to 45 mph. Teams in Memphis can experience wheel-to-wheel racing on a challenging track designed to test driving skills and nerves alike. The controlled indoor environment means weather never cancels the action, while the competitive format naturally brings out team dynamics and friendly rivalry.

What to Expect

Groups start with a safety briefing and helmet fitting, then take practice laps to learn the track layout and kart handling. The main event features qualifying rounds followed by head-to-head races, with real-time lap timing displayed on monitors so everyone can track their performance. Corporate packages typically include private track time, team standings, and access to the facility's dining area for post-race discussions.

Who It's For

Ideal for competitive teams of 8-30 people who want an adrenaline-fueled bonding experience. The racing format works especially well for sales teams, engineering groups, or any department that thrives on measurable performance and friendly competition.
