Roundtable Meetings & Events

5.0★ (38 reviews) 1-2 hours

Nestled in the heart of Memphis, Roundtable Meetings & Events provides a unique and engaging team building experience. With their expertly facilitated retreats and workshops, groups can dive into collaborative problem-solving, communication exercises, and creative ideation sessions that bring colleagues closer together.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be greeted by the Roundtable team and guided through a tailored agenda designed to meet your specific needs. Activities range from interactive workshops to immersive simulations, all facilitated by experienced professionals who know how to foster teamwork, boost morale, and unlock your group's full potential.

Who It's For

Roundtable's flexible formats and customizable programs make them an excellent choice for teams of all sizes, from intimate groups to larger corporate gatherings. Whether you're looking to strengthen interpersonal bonds, spark innovation, or simply enjoy a fun, memorable day out of the office, Roundtable has the perfect team building experience to suit your needs.
