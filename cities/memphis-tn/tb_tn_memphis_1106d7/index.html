Crosstown Arts

4.8★ (119 reviews)

Crosstown Arts in Memphis, Tennessee is a dynamic arts hub that offers team-building experiences that are equal parts creative and collaborative. From immersive art workshops to imaginative scavenger hunts, the expert team at Crosstown Arts can craft customized activities that bring groups together through shared inspiration and problem-solving.

What to Expect

Crosstown Arts' team-building programs are highly interactive and tailored to each group's unique needs. Participants might find themselves working in small teams to complete a large-scale mural, competing in a fast-paced trivia challenge, or using art supplies to build innovative prototypes – all while fostering communication, critical thinking, and camaraderie.

Who It's For

Crosstown Arts' team-building offerings are ideal for mid-size groups (15-40 participants) seeking an artistic and engaging way to strengthen bonds, ignite creativity, and encourage out-of-the-box thinking. The hands-on activities are perfect for companies, nonprofits, and other organizations looking to cultivate a more cohesive and innovative workplace culture.
