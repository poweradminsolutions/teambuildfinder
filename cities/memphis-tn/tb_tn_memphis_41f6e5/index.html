The Art Project

4.8★ (68 reviews)

The Art Project in Memphis, Tennessee offers an immersive team building experience unlike any other. Groups collaborate to create custom artworks, fostering creativity, communication, and camaraderie in a welcoming studio setting.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through a hands-on art workshop tailored to your group's needs and interests. You'll have the opportunity to experiment with a variety of mediums, from acrylics and pastels to mosaic tiles and pottery. The end result is a unique, collective piece that your team can proudly display back at the office.

Who It's For

The Art Project's team building activities work well for groups of 10-30 looking to break out of the traditional office setting. Whether planning a company offsite or wanting to infuse some fun into a quarterly meeting, this creative experience is sure to bring your team closer together.
