BRIDGES USA

4.7★ (43 reviews)

BRIDGES USA in Memphis, Tennessee offers a unique blend of volunteer work and diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) workshops that can transform team dynamics. Their hands-on activities challenge groups to collaborate, communicate, and build trust in a supportive environment.

What to Expect

BRIDGES facilitates customized team building experiences, from sorting and distributing donated goods to participating in guided discussions on unconscious bias and inclusive leadership. Participants work closely together to complete tasks and engage in meaningful dialogue, fostering a stronger sense of unity within the group.

Who It's For

BRIDGES' team building activities are ideal for mid-sized groups (15-40 people) looking to strengthen interpersonal connections, develop cross-cultural competencies, and give back to the local community. The shared experience of volunteering or exploring DEI topics creates a powerful bonding opportunity that can't be replicated in a traditional office setting.
