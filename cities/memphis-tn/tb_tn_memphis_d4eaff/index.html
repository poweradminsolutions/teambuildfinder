Battlefront Lasertag and Paintball

4.6★ (333 reviews)

Located in the heart of Memphis, Battlefront Lasertag and Paintball provides an immersive, adrenaline-fueled experience perfect for team building. Compete against colleagues in high-intensity laser tag battles or navigate challenging paintball courses, all while bonding over shared victories and defeats.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive a safety briefing and be equipped with state-of-the-art gear. Then, it's time to hit the arena, where you'll strategize, communicate, and put your teamwork skills to the test. Skilled referees ensure fair play and a safe environment throughout the event.

Who It's For

Battlefront is ideal for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking to engage in some friendly competition and physical activity. The shared challenge of navigating the arena and working together to accomplish objectives can quickly break down barriers between departments and foster stronger workplace relationships.
