Skyway Theatre

4.2★ (1869 reviews)

This former Minneapolis cinema transforms team building with its massive event space hosting everything from improv comedy workshops to group drumming circles. The cavernous venue's theatrical atmosphere creates an immersive backdrop where teams can explore creative expression together, whether they're learning comedic timing or finding rhythm as a collective.

What to Expect

Groups typically participate in instructor-led sessions that might involve improv exercises building on each other's ideas, collaborative drumming where everyone contributes to layered rhythms, or trivia competitions that get increasingly animated. The venue's full bar and concert hall setup means your team can seamlessly transition from structured activities to informal networking in the same dramatic space.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and groups comfortable with performance-based activities who want something more dynamic than traditional workshops. The venue's entertainment focus works particularly well for marketing departments, creative agencies, or any team looking to break out of conventional corporate settings.
