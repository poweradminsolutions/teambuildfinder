ModernWell

5.0★ (69 reviews)

ModernWell in Minneapolis is a versatile coworking space and event venue that specializes in facilitated team-building workshops and immersive corporate retreats. With an emphasis on holistic wellbeing, their experienced facilitators guide groups through a range of interactive exercises and activities designed to foster collaboration, communication, and creative problem-solving.

What to Expect

At ModernWell, team building sessions might involve outdoor adventures like scavenger hunts or ropes courses, indoor team challenges like escape rooms or design thinking exercises, or more introspective workshops focused on mindfulness, emotional intelligence, and leadership development. The flexible event spaces and thoughtful programming create an engaging, productive experience for participants.

Who It's For

ModernWell's team building offerings are well-suited for mid-sized companies and departments (15-40 people) looking to strengthen bonds, ignite innovation, and rejuvenate their workplace culture. The activities cater to a range of group sizes, skill levels, and desired outcomes, making it a great choice for onboarding, quarterly offsites, or annual company retreats.
