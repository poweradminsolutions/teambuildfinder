Glass House

4.6★ (285 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Minneapolis, Glass House offers a unique and immersive team building experience. This versatile event venue provides the perfect backdrop for facilitated workshops, strategic planning retreats, and creative problem-solving sessions that challenge groups to work together and think outside the box.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a mix of engaging activities and structured discussions led by experienced facilitators. From escape room-style challenges that require teamwork and lateral thinking to guided meditation and reflection exercises, the program is designed to foster communication, build trust, and boost morale among participants.

Who It's For

Glass House's team building offerings are well-suited for mid-sized groups of 15 to 40 looking to strengthen interpersonal bonds and cultivate a collaborative spirit. Whether you're planning a corporate offsite, a department-wide retreat, or a leadership development program, the versatile event space and customizable activities make it an ideal choice.
