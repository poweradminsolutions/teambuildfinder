Norway House

4.6★ (333 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Minneapolis, Norway House is a vibrant cultural hub that offers team-building experiences like no other. With its diverse array of event spaces, from the airy Gallery to the cozy Cafe, groups can immerse themselves in a dynamic and collaborative atmosphere, fostering connections and cultivating creativity.

What to Expect

At Norway House, team-building activities range from interactive cultural workshops to art-inspired challenges. Groups might engage in traditional Norwegian crafts, such as rosemaling painting or woodcarving, or tackle brain-teasing scavenger hunts that showcase the venue's rich history and global connections.

Who It's For

Norway House is an ideal choice for teams seeking a unique and enriching team-building experience. Whether you're a mid-size group looking to break down silos or a close-knit team aiming to strengthen bonds, the diverse programming and welcoming atmosphere at Norway House make it a standout option in the Minneapolis area.
