Sassy Lassy Interactive Events I Virtual & In-Person Entertainment for Corporate and Social Events

Sassy Lassy Interactive Events in Minneapolis, Minnesota offers a range of unique, high-energy entertainment experiences perfect for team building. What to Expect

With Sassy Lassy, groups can expect a carefully curated, turnkey experience from start to finish. Their facilitators guide participants through dynamic activities that foster communication, problem-solving, and camaraderie. Whether it's a virtual game night or an in-person hybrid challenge, the Sassy Lassy team ensures the flow is seamless and the fun is maximized.

Who It's For

Sassy Lassy's events work well for medium-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to infuse some playfulness and healthy competition into their team building. The shared challenge of cracking an escape room or nailing an improv routine tends to break down barriers between departments faster than a standard happy hour ever could.
