Northern Clay Center

4.6★ (53 reviews)

The Northern Clay Center in Minneapolis is a hub for artistic expression, offering an array of hands-on workshops that make for engaging team-building experiences. From guided sculpture classes to custom pottery-making sessions, these creative activities foster collaboration, communication, and a shared sense of accomplishment.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a guided, immersive studio session where they'll learn pottery-making techniques and have the chance to create their own unique ceramic pieces. Experienced instructors provide step-by-step instruction, allowing participants to work together to bring their designs to life.

Who It's For

These workshops are ideal for mid-sized teams (10-25 people) seeking a fun, creative team-building activity. The shared creative process encourages bonding and problem-solving, making it a great option for corporate retreats, student groups, or any team looking to foster camaraderie.
