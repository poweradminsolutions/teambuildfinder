Machine Shop

4.7★ (314 reviews)

Located in the heart of Minneapolis, Machine Shop offers a truly unique team building experience. With its industrial-chic aesthetic and hands-on workshops, this venue allows groups to tap into their creative and collaborative sides in a one-of-a-kind setting.

What to Expect

At Machine Shop, teams roll up their sleeves and get to work on custom metalworking projects. Under the guidance of skilled instructors, participants learn techniques like welding, fabrication, and design before putting their newfound skills to the test in friendly competitions.

Who It's For

Machine Shop's team building activities are ideal for mid-sized groups (15-40 people) looking to strengthen bonds and boost morale through a shared sense of accomplishment. The hands-on nature of the workshops encourages colleagues to problem-solve together, fostering better communication and camaraderie.
