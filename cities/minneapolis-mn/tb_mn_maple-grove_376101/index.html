Enigma Adventures: Escape Room Games

4.8★ (366 reviews) $$ 60 minutes

Enigma Adventures in Maple Grove offers a thrilling and collaborative escape room experience that challenges teams to work together and solve intricate puzzles under pressure. With a variety of themed rooms to choose from, this locally-owned attraction provides an immersive team-building activity that's perfect for building communication, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Enigma Adventures, you'll be ushered into one of their meticulously designed escape rooms, where your team will have 60 minutes to work together and find the clues, solve the mysteries, and ultimately make your grand escape. The experience requires creativity, quick thinking, and a willingness to think outside the box.

Who It's For

Enigma Adventures' escape rooms are ideal for medium-sized teams (8-12 participants) looking to foster camaraderie and improve group dynamics through a shared challenge. The competitive yet lighthearted atmosphere encourages friendly collaboration and can help break down barriers between colleagues.
