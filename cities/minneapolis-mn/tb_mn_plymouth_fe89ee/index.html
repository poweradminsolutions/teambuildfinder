Bobby's Gourmet & Culinary Studio

5.0★ (41 reviews) $$$$

Located in the heart of Plymouth, Minnesota, Bobby's Gourmet & Culinary Studio offers an engaging team building experience that's equal parts fun and educational. Groups can roll up their sleeves and master the art of global cuisine, working together to prepare a multi-course meal from scratch under the guidance of Bobby's talented instructors.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be divided into small groups and outfitted with all the necessary cooking tools and ingredients. From there, it's a friendly competition as each group races to chop, sauté, and plate their dishes to perfection. The instructors are there to provide hands-on support and culinary tips, ensuring everyone walks away with new skills and recipes to try at home.

Who It's For

Bobby's Gourmet is a fantastic choice for mid-sized teams (12-30 people) looking to bond over a shared culinary challenge. The interactive format encourages collaboration, communication, and a healthy dose of good-natured rivalry - perfect for bringing coworkers closer together in a relaxed, food-filled setting.
