The Escape Game Minneapolis

5.0★ (14077 reviews)

The Escape Game Minneapolis delivers immersive puzzle-solving adventures right inside the Mall of America, where teams work together to crack codes, find hidden clues, and escape themed rooms before time runs out. Their multiple room options range from classic mystery scenarios to more adventurous themes, each designed to challenge groups through collaborative problem-solving under pressure.

What to Expect

Teams get briefed on their chosen scenario, then spend 60 minutes locked in a themed room working together to solve interconnected puzzles and riddles. A game master monitors progress and can provide hints when teams get stuck, ensuring groups stay engaged without getting completely stumped.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 4-8 people who enjoy mental challenges and don't mind a bit of time pressure. The collaborative puzzle-solving naturally brings out different team members' strengths while creating shared moments of triumph.
