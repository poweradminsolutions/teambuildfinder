B:Hive Bridgeport

4.3★ (15 reviews)

B:Hive Bridgeport is a vibrant coworking space in the heart of Bridgeport, Connecticut that offers a range of engaging team building experiences. From facilitated workshops that foster innovation and design thinking to fast-paced hackathons, this dynamic venue has the expertise and resources to bring out the best in your group.

What to Expect

When you book a team building activity at B:Hive, you can expect a seamless, professionally-facilitated experience. Whether it's a half-day workshop or a multi-hour coding challenge, the dedicated staff will guide your team through interactive exercises designed to cultivate collaboration, problem-solving, and creative thinking.

Who It's For

B:Hive's team building offerings are well-suited for mid-size groups of 15 to 40 looking to break out of their daily routine and tackle exciting new challenges together. The shared experience of working towards a common goal helps to break down silos and build stronger bonds between colleagues from different departments.
