Cardinal Shehan Center

3.9★ (151 reviews)

The Cardinal Shehan Center in Bridgeport, CT is a go-to destination for team building through volunteerism and charitable work. Groups can roll up their sleeves and make a real impact on the local community, whether sorting and packing donated goods, serving meals to those in need, or lending a hand at neighborhood clean-up projects.

What to Expect

When you arrive, the Shehan Center staff will greet your group and provide an orientation to the day's activities. Then it's time to get to work - you'll be divided into teams and assigned specific tasks to complete together. The activities encourage collaboration, communication, and a shared sense of purpose as you work towards a common goal.

Who It's For

This is an excellent team building option for medium to large groups (15-50 people) looking to do more than just a typical office party or happy hour. The hands-on volunteer work creates a meaningful shared experience that brings coworkers closer together.
