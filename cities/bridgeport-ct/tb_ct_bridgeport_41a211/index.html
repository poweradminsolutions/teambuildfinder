Small Game Club of Connecticut

4.0★ (28 reviews)

The Small Game Club of Connecticut in Bridgeport offers a unique team building experience with their monthly sports tournaments and competitions. Whether your group is looking to bond over a spirited game of dodgeball, test their strategy in a round of trivia, or simply let loose and have some fun, this is the perfect spot to bring your team together.

What to Expect

When you book an event at the Small Game Club, your group will have exclusive access to their indoor sports facilities for a 2-hour session. Depending on the package you choose, you'll participate in a tournament-style competition in sports like volleyball, flag football, or laser tag. The staff provides all the necessary equipment and facilitates the games to keep the energy high and the teamwork flowing.

Who It's For

This venue works best for mid-sized teams of 20-40 people looking to get their adrenaline pumping and strengthen their camaraderie through friendly competition. The shared challenge and team spirit fostered here make the Small Game Club an excellent choice for corporate outings, holiday parties, or any occasion when you want to bring your crew closer together.
