The Fig Cooking School

4.8★ (186 reviews) $$ 1-2 hours

The Fig Cooking School in Milford, CT offers a truly unique team building experience. Groups can immerse themselves in the art of gourmet cooking, learning new techniques and recipes while bonding over a shared challenge. The hands-on classes are led by expert instructors who tailor the curriculum to suit your team's needs and skill levels.

What to Expect

When your team visits The Fig, you'll don aprons and gather around state-of-the-art cooking stations. The instructors begin with a fun icebreaker, then guide you through preparing a multi-course meal from scratch. Throughout the session, your group will collaborate on divvying up tasks, sharing tips, and plating the final dishes for a celebratory feast.

Who It's For

The Fig's team building classes are ideal for mid-size groups of 12-30 looking to strengthen bonds and discover new talents. Whether your team is celebrating a milestone or seeking a creative break from the office, the interactive, delicious experience is sure to bring everyone closer together.
