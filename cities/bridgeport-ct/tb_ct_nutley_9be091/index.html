Jersey Axe House

5.0★ (412 reviews)

Jersey Axe House delivers an adrenaline-pumping team building experience where colleagues learn to throw hatchets at wooden targets in a safe, supervised environment. This Nutley venue transforms the ancient skill of axe throwing into a modern competitive activity that gets teams cheering, coaching each other, and celebrating unexpected victories together.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety training and throwing technique instruction from experienced coaches before rotating through lanes for individual and team competitions. Sessions typically include various target games and tournaments that build momentum as participants gain confidence and accuracy with their throws.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams seeking an unconventional bonding experience that levels the playing field regardless of job titles or athletic ability. The shared learning curve and unexpected nature of the activity creates genuine moments of connection and laughter.
