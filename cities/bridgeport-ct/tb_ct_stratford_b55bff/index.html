Sterling Community Center

4.5★ (103 reviews)

The Sterling Community Center in Stratford, CT offers a unique opportunity for teams to come together and make a tangible impact through volunteer and environmental cleanup activities. With a focus on giving back to the local community, these hands-on experiences forge stronger bonds and a shared sense of purpose among participants.

What to Expect

Groups can expect to spend 2-3 hours working side-by-side to beautify public spaces, clear trails, or package supplies for those in need. The center provides all necessary equipment and expert guidance to ensure the work is done efficiently and safely. Teamwork and communication are key as participants tackle the tasks at hand.

Who It's For

These community service projects are well-suited for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to step away from the office and engage in something meaningful. The shared sense of accomplishment tends to break down barriers between departments and build camaraderie in a way that happy hours or off-sites simply can't match.
