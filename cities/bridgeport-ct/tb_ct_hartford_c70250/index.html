Sea Tea Improv Studios

4.9★ (50 reviews)

Situated in the heart of Hartford, Sea Tea Improv Studios offers a one-of-a-kind team building experience that combines laughter, creativity, and collaboration. This dynamic improv troupe has been delighting audiences for over a decade, and now they've opened their doors to help corporate groups unlock their inner comedic talents.

What to Expect

When you book a team building session at Sea Tea, your group will dive into a series of interactive improv games and exercises led by the studio's experienced instructors. Through a mixture of structured activities and free-flowing improvisation, participants learn to think on their feet, embrace spontaneity, and support each other in a fun, low-pressure environment.

Who It's For

Sea Tea's team building workshops are ideal for groups of 10-40 looking to foster stronger interpersonal connections and boost morale. Whether you're planning a company offsite or a department bonding event, the shared experience of improv comedy can break down barriers and get everyone laughing together.
