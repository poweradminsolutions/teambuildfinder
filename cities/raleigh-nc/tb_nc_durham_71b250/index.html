Zacki's Culinary Creations

4.9★ (47 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Durham, Zacki's Culinary Creations offers a delightful team-building experience that combines the joy of cooking with the camaraderie of collaboration. This dynamic culinary studio invites groups to don aprons and work together to craft mouthwatering dishes, fostering a sense of unity and accomplishment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through a hands-on cooking class, where you'll work together to prepare a multi-course meal from scratch. With the expert instruction of Zacki's talented chefs, you'll learn new techniques, share ideas, and enjoy the satisfaction of creating a delectable feast that you can then savor together.

Who It's For

Zacki's Culinary Creations is an excellent choice for teams of 10 to 30 looking to bond over a shared culinary adventure. Whether you're celebrating a company milestone or simply seeking to strengthen interpersonal connections, this interactive cooking class will have your group working in sync, fostering communication and camaraderie.
