Martin Marietta Center for the Performing Arts

4.7★ (2707 reviews)

The Martin Marietta Center transforms Raleigh teams through immersive performing arts experiences that get everyone out of their comfort zone. Groups can dive into guided improv sessions that build spontaneous communication skills, participate in collaborative music workshops, or work together on theatrical exercises designed to strengthen trust and creative problem-solving.

What to Expect

Teams typically start with warm-up activities led by professional performers, then move into hands-on workshops where everyone participates regardless of experience level. Improv sessions focus on quick thinking and supporting teammates' ideas, while music workshops might have groups creating rhythm pieces together or learning basic percussion techniques that require listening and coordination.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and organizations wanting to boost collaboration through artistic expression. The performing arts format works especially well for groups ready to embrace vulnerability and playfulness as pathways to stronger workplace relationships.
