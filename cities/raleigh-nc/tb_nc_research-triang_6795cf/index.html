Game On Escapes & More - Boxyard RTP

5.0★ (810 reviews)

Game On Escapes & More brings immersive puzzle-solving adventures to Research Triangle Park's innovative Boxyard development. Teams tackle elaborate escape rooms with themed scenarios that demand clear communication, creative thinking, and coordinated problem-solving under time pressure. The facility combines high-tech elements with hands-on challenges that reveal natural leaders and hidden talents.

What to Expect

Your group splits into teams and enters themed escape rooms where you have 60 minutes to solve interconnected puzzles, find hidden clues, and complete your mission before time runs out. Each room tells a story through detailed set design and progressive challenges that require different skills - logical reasoning, pattern recognition, and teamwork. A game master monitors your progress and provides hints when teams get genuinely stuck.

Who It's For

Ideal for corporate teams of 8-20 people who want an engaging mental challenge that showcases individual strengths within a collaborative framework. The time pressure and shared problem-solving create natural bonding moments without forced icebreakers.
