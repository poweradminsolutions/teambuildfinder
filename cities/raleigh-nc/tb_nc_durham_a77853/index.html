Escape on Purpose

4.9★ (339 reviews) $$ 1 hour

Located in the vibrant city of Durham, North Carolina, Escape on Purpose offers a thrilling team building experience like no other. Their expertly designed escape rooms challenge groups to work together, think critically, and push their problem-solving skills to the limit in a race against the clock.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be ushered into a themed escape room, each with its own unique puzzles and challenges. You'll have just 60 minutes to collaborate, strategize, and uncover the clues needed to make your daring escape. The experience is immersive, high-energy, and requires seamless teamwork to succeed.

Who It's For

Escape on Purpose's escape rooms are perfect for mid-sized teams of 10-20 looking to strengthen bonds, boost communication, and inject some healthy competition into their team building activities. The shared experience of overcoming challenges together tends to break down barriers and forge stronger connections between coworkers.
