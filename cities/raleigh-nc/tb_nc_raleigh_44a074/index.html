Burning Coal Theatre Company

4.7★ (239 reviews)

The Burning Coal Theatre Company in Raleigh, North Carolina offers uniquely immersive team building experiences through their facilitated workshops and retreats. Groups can expect to dive deep into theatrical exercises that build trust, communication, and creative problem-solving skills in a supportive, judgment-free environment.

What to Expect

A typical Burning Coal team building session starts with interactive icebreakers to get everyone engaged and comfortable. Then the group moves into improvisation exercises and scripted role-playing challenges that simulate real-world workplace scenarios. The activities are carefully facilitated to maximize participation and learning.

Who It's For

Burning Coal's team building programs work best for mid-sized groups of 15-40 looking to push beyond typical trust falls and ropes courses. The shared challenge of bringing a theatrical performance to life tends to break down barriers between departments and reveal new ways of collaborating.
