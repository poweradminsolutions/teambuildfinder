First and Main

4.8★ (85 reviews)

First and Main in Knightdale, NC offers an exceptional team building experience through their facilitated workshops and retreats. This venue's expert facilitators guide groups through interactive exercises designed to strengthen communication, foster collaboration, and build trust among team members.

What to Expect

When your team arrives at First and Main, you'll be greeted by a warm and welcoming staff who will guide you through the day's activities. Expect a combination of thought-provoking discussions, hands-on challenges, and opportunities for creative expression - all within the venue's modern, flexible event spaces.

Who It's For

First and Main's team building programs are well-suited for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to break down silos and bring their group closer together. The mix of intellect and physicality appeals to a wide range of personalities, making it a great choice for corporate offsites, nonprofit retreats, and other group gatherings.
