Times Up! Escape Rooms

4.9★ (566 reviews)

Times Up! Escape Rooms in Cary, NC offers a one-of-a-kind team building experience that challenges groups to work together under pressure. Their expertly-designed escape rooms put your problem-solving skills to the test as you race against the clock to crack the code and make your daring escape.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Times Up!, your group will be ushered into a themed escape room setting and given 60 minutes to work together to find clues, solve puzzles, and escape before time runs out. With heart-pumping excitement and engaging challenges, this activity is sure to bring your team closer together.

Who It's For

Times Up! Escape Rooms is ideal for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to foster collaboration, communication, and creative thinking. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, planning a company retreat, or simply want to inject some friendly competition into your workday, this unique experience is a surefire way to bring your group closer together.
