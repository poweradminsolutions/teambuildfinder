Whole Brain Escape

4.9★ (474 reviews)

Whole Brain Escape in Apex, NC is the ultimate destination for team building through immersive escape room experiences. With a focus on fostering collaboration, communication, and critical thinking, their expertly designed rooms challenge groups to work together and solve puzzles under the pressure of the clock.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be briefed on the objective of the escape room and provided with any necessary equipment. You'll then have 60 minutes to work collectively, leveraging each person's unique skills and perspectives, to decipher clues, unlock doors, and ultimately escape the room before time runs out.

Who It's For

Whole Brain Escape is ideal for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking to strengthen bonds, boost morale, and drive results through a shared, adrenaline-fueled challenge. The competitive yet supportive environment encourages colleagues to step out of their comfort zones and forge new connections.
