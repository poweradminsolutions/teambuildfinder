Frog Hollow Outdoors

4.7★ (56 reviews)

Frog Hollow Outdoors in Durham, NC, is a premier destination for teams seeking a thrilling outdoor adventure. With its diverse offerings, from guided kayak tours to exhilarating ropes courses, this venue provides the perfect backdrop for groups to bond, challenge themselves, and create lasting memories.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by knowledgeable instructors who will provide all the necessary equipment and safety briefings. Whether you choose to paddle through the serene waters of local rivers or navigate a high-flying obstacle course, the activities are designed to encourage teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills.

Who It's For

Frog Hollow Outdoors is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 participants) looking to step outside their comfort zones and engage in a dynamic, nature-based team-building experience. The shared sense of accomplishment and camaraderie that emerges from these challenges can help break down barriers and foster stronger interpersonal connections within your organization.
