Season to Taste

5.0★ (64 reviews)

Located in the heart of Durham, North Carolina, Season to Taste offers a unique team building experience that brings coworkers together through the shared joy of cooking. Participants will collaborate on creating a delicious multi-course meal, learning new culinary skills and techniques under the guidance of the school's expert instructors.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, teams will be assigned to individual cooking stations and provided with all the necessary ingredients and equipment. Working together, they'll learn to prepare an array of dishes, from appetizers to main courses to desserts. The hands-on, interactive format encourages communication, problem-solving, and a healthy sense of competition as groups race to complete their menus.

Who It's For

This activity is ideal for mid-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The convivial, relaxed atmosphere is perfect for breaking down barriers, sparking new conversations, and fostering a stronger sense of camaraderie among colleagues.
