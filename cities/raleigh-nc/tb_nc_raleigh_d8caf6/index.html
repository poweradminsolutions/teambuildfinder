Fieldstream Farm Venue

4.8★ (46 reviews)

Fieldstream Farm Venue in Raleigh, NC offers a truly unique team building experience on their picturesque 30-acre working farm. Get your hands dirty and bond over shared agricultural tasks, from gathering eggs to tending the vegetable garden. This immersive retreat setting helps teams disconnect from the daily grind and connect with nature - and each other.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a hands-on, outdoor-focused itinerary tailored to their needs and interests. Activities might include egg collecting, vegetable harvesting, animal care, or even a friendly farming competition. The team at Fieldstream Farm will guide you through each task, providing all necessary equipment and instruction.

Who It's For

Fieldstream Farm's team building retreats work well for mid-size groups (15-40 participants) looking to get out of the office and try something active and experiential. The farm setting encourages camaraderie and conversation in a refreshing, stress-free environment.
