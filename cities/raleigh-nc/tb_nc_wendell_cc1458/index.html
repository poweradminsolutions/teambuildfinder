The Shatter Space Rage Room

4.9★ (135 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Wendell, North Carolina, The Shatter Space Rage Room offers a unique and cathartic team building experience. This improv-style activity invites groups to let loose and shatter their stress through a guided session of creative destruction.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be outfitted with protective gear and given a selection of everyday items to destroy in a controlled, safe environment. With upbeat music playing, you'll take turns smashing, throwing, and obliterating objects, working together to create a symphony of shattering sounds.

Who It's For

The Shatter Space is ideal for mid-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to bond over a shared experience of pure, unapologetic release. Whether your group needs to blow off steam after a big project or simply wants to try something wildly different, this activity is sure to leave everyone feeling refreshed and connected.
