Bad Axe Throwing Nashville

4.8★ (1811 reviews) $$ 1-2 hours

Bad Axe Throwing Nashville transforms corporate team building into a primal competition where colleagues learn to hurl hatchets at wooden targets under expert coaching. The Music City location combines axe throwing instruction with a full bar and restaurant, creating an atmosphere where adrenaline and camaraderie flow equally. Groups rotate through throwing lanes while coaches teach proper form and safety, building confidence through shared achievement.

What to Expect

Your group receives safety briefings and throwing technique instruction from certified coaches before stepping up to individual lanes with wooden targets. Sessions typically run 90 minutes with rotations between throwing and socializing, allowing everyone multiple chances to hit bullseyes. The venue provides all axes and safety equipment while maintaining a lively bar atmosphere with food and drinks available throughout your session.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-30 people seeking an unconventional bonding experience that levels the playing field regardless of job title. The physical challenge and slight element of danger creates instant conversation starters and memorable moments that translate into stronger workplace relationships.
