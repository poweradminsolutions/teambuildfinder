Skydeck on Broadway

4.4★ (179 reviews)

Perched atop the Assembly Food Hall in downtown Nashville, Skydeck offers music-loving teams a one-of-a-kind experience. Groups can enjoy live performances from up-and-coming local acts, or even try their hand at drumming or other interactive music-making activities led by experienced facilitators.

What to Expect

When you arrive, you'll be greeted by the Skydeck's vibrant atmosphere and sweeping views of the city. Your team will be guided through engaging, rhythm-based exercises that build communication and collaboration, culminating in a chance to showcase your newfound musical skills on stage.

Who It's For

Skydeck is a perfect fit for teams of 20-50 looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The high-energy, music-centric activities create a memorable team experience that's sure to have everyone leaving on a high note.
