Breakout Games

5.0★ (7170 reviews)

Breakout Games delivers immersive escape room experiences that push teams to collaborate under genuine time pressure in Franklin. Groups get locked into elaborately themed rooms and must work together to decode puzzles, uncover clues, and engineer their escape within sixty minutes. The combination of shared adrenaline and collective problem-solving creates natural team dynamics that surface leadership styles and communication patterns.

What to Expect

Your group gets briefed on the scenario, then locked into a themed room filled with hidden clues, puzzles, and props that tell a story. Teams must search thoroughly, think creatively, and communicate constantly as they work through interconnected challenges with the clock ticking down. A game master monitors progress and can provide hints if teams get completely stuck on crucial puzzle elements.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 4-8 people who want an intense, collaborative challenge that reveals how colleagues think and communicate under pressure. The format works especially well for groups where people need to learn to rely on each other's different strengths and perspectives.
