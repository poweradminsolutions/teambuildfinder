SOAR Adventure Tower

4.7★ (693 reviews) 2 hour

Perched atop a scenic hill in Franklin, Tennessee, SOAR Adventure Tower offers an exhilarating team building experience that challenges groups to conquer a high ropes course and navigate a thrilling obstacle course. With towering climbing structures and an 18-hole mini-golf course, this unique recreation facility provides the perfect blend of physical and mental stimulation to bring teams together.

What to Expect

When your group arrives at SOAR, you'll be greeted by a team of experienced guides who will provide a comprehensive safety briefing and equipment fitting. From there, you'll have the opportunity to take on the high ropes course, tackling a series of aerial obstacles and traverses while supporting and encouraging one another. The on-site mini-golf course also provides a fun, competitive element to your team building adventure.

Who It's For

SOAR Adventure Tower is an ideal choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking a dynamic, adrenaline-fueled team building activity. The shared challenge of the ropes course and mini-golf course promotes camaraderie, communication, and problem-solving skills, making it a popular option for corporate groups, sports teams, and outdoor enthusiasts alike.
