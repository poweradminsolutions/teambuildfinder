The Pavilion at Harpeth

4.7★ (20 reviews)

The Pavilion at Harpeth in Nashville, Tennessee offers an inspiring, flexible space for team building and corporate retreats. With a focus on facilitated workshops and interactive activities, this venue can help your group reconnect, problem-solve, and build lasting bonds.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by the Pavilion's experienced event staff, who will guide you through an itinerary tailored to your group's goals. Expect a blend of structured exercises, group discussions, and opportunities for creative collaboration in the Pavilion's light-filled event spaces.

Who It's For

The Pavilion at Harpeth works well for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to step away from the office and engage in meaningful team building. The versatile facilities and customizable programming make it a great choice for departments seeking to strengthen communication, boost morale, or simply have fun together.
