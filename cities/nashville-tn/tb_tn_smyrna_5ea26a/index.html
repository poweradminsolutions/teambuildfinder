Smyrna Outdoor Adventure Center

4.5★ (108 reviews)

Smyrna Outdoor Adventure Center is a hidden gem in the heart of Tennessee, offering team-building experiences that get groups out of the office and into the great outdoors. From scavenger hunts that test problem-solving skills to guided hikes through scenic natural landscapes, this center provides a refreshing change of pace for corporate teams seeking to bond, communicate, and push their boundaries together.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be greeted by the center's knowledgeable staff, who will provide a thorough briefing and set the stage for your chosen activity. Whether you opt for a high-energy scavenger hunt or a more reflective nature hike, the emphasis is on collaboration, critical thinking, and having fun in a beautiful, stress-free setting.

Who It's For

Smyrna Outdoor Adventure Center is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 participants) looking to strengthen interpersonal connections and foster a greater sense of camaraderie. The combination of physical activity and problem-solving challenges encourages employees to step out of their comfort zones and develop new skills together.
