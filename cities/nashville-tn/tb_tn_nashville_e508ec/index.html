The Escape Game Nashville (Opry Mills)

5.0★ (6995 reviews)

The Escape Game Nashville brings movie-quality production values to team building through immersive escape room experiences at Opry Mills. Teams work together to solve intricate puzzles and uncover clues within themed rooms that feel more like film sets than traditional escape games. With an impressive 5-star rating from nearly 7,000 reviews, this Nashville location delivers consistently polished experiences that challenge groups to communicate effectively under time pressure.

What to Expect

Your team enters a carefully crafted themed environment where you have 60 minutes to work together, find hidden clues, and solve a series of interconnected puzzles to 'escape.' Game masters monitor progress and provide hints when teams get stuck, ensuring everyone stays engaged without getting frustrated. Multiple rooms are available, so larger groups can split into competing teams and compare their escape times afterward.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 4-8 people who want an engaging mental challenge that requires genuine collaboration. The time pressure and shared problem-solving create natural opportunities for colleagues to see each other's strengths in a completely different context than the office.
