Faxon's Hatchet House

5.0★ (140 reviews)

Located in the charming town of Spring Hill, Tennessee, Faxon's Hatchet House offers an exhilarating team building experience like no other. This unique sports club and beer garden specializes in the thrilling art of axe throwing, providing groups with a chance to bond over a shared challenge and a few cold brews.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive a comprehensive safety briefing and hands-on instruction from expert coaches. You'll then take turns hurling hatchets at targets, competing individually or as teams to see who can land the most impressive shots. The laid-back, outdoor setting creates a fun, social atmosphere perfect for bringing colleagues together.

Who It's For

Faxon's Hatchet House works well for mid-size teams (15-40) looking for a physical, competitive team building activity. The shared challenge of axe throwing tends to break down barriers between departments faster than a happy hour ever could, making it a great choice for building camaraderie and collaboration.
