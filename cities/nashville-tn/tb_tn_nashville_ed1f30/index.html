Musicians Hall of Fame and Museum

4.6★ (2385 reviews)

The Musicians Hall of Fame and Museum transforms Nashville's rich recording history into an immersive team building experience through hands-on drumming workshops and music appreciation sessions. Groups explore exhibits featuring legendary instruments and recording equipment, then participate in guided rhythm circles that get everyone creating music together regardless of skill level.

What to Expect

Teams start with a curator-led tour through exhibits showcasing the stories behind famous recordings and the session musicians who made them possible. The experience culminates in a collaborative drumming session where participants use various percussion instruments to create group rhythms, fostering communication and synchronization skills in an energizing musical environment.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and groups of 12-30 people who want something different from typical corporate activities. The musical focus works especially well for breaking down inhibitions and encouraging participation from quieter team members.
