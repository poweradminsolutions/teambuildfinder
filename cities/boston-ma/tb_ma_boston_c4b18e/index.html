Boston Tea Party Ships & Museum

4.6★ (9275 reviews)

Step aboard authentic replica ships and immerse your team in the pivotal moments that sparked the American Revolution. This floating museum in Boston combines interactive theater with hands-on history, letting groups participate in a reenactment of the famous tea protest while exploring multimedia exhibits that bring 1773 to life.

What to Expect

Teams board reconstructed Tea Party ships where costumed interpreters guide them through the events of December 16, 1773. Groups actively participate by dumping tea chests overboard, then explore below-deck exhibits featuring artifacts, holographic displays, and documentary films before gathering in the tea room to debrief over refreshments.

Who It's For

Ideal for corporate groups of 20-60 who want an engaging shared experience that sparks conversation. The mix of performance, participation, and historical intrigue works particularly well for teams that appreciate cultural activities over typical trust falls.
