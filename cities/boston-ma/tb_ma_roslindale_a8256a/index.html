The Rozzie Square Theater

4.0★ (144 reviews)

The Rozzie Square Theater in Roslindale, MA is a top-notch comedy club that doubles as a fantastic team building venue. Their improv classes and competitive shows are ideal for bringing groups together through laughter and shared experiences.

What to Expect

When you book a team building session at The Rozzie Square, your group will dive into an interactive improv workshop where everyone gets to showcase their comedic chops. The exercises are designed to build trust, communication, and problem-solving skills in a fun, low-pressure environment.

Who It's For

This venue works well for mid-size teams (15-40) looking to bond over something a little outside the box. The shared challenge of improv performance tends to break down barriers between coworkers faster than a happy hour ever could.
