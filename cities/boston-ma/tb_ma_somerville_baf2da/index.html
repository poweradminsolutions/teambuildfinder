Shiso Kitchen

4.9★ (117 reviews)

Shiso Kitchen in Somerville offers a truly unique team building experience through their hands-on cooking classes. Groups learn to prepare delicious, authentic Japanese dishes together, fostering camaraderie and a shared sense of accomplishment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be guided through a menu of classic Japanese fare, from sushi rolls to tempura. Led by experienced chef-instructors, you'll work together to source ingredients, master culinary techniques, and bring your creations to life. The interactive, high-energy format encourages teamwork and friendly competition.

Who It's For

Shiso Kitchen's team building classes are perfect for mid-sized groups (10-30 people) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. Whether it's a corporate retreat, holiday party, or just an excuse to get the team out of the office, this activity promises plenty of laughs and a new found respect for your colleagues' culinary skills.
