Studio on the Common

5.0★ (47 reviews)

Located in the heart of Winchester, MA, Studio on the Common offers a unique and engaging team building experience through their art and craft workshops. From pottery classes to jewelry making, these hands-on activities encourage collaboration, problem-solving, and creative expression among groups.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be greeted by the studio's knowledgeable instructors who will guide you through the chosen workshop. Participants will have the opportunity to get their hands dirty, explore new mediums, and work together to complete a shared project. The relaxed, creative atmosphere allows colleagues to bond in a fun, low-pressure setting.

Who It's For

Studio on the Common's team building workshops are ideal for mid-sized groups (10-30 people) looking to strengthen interpersonal connections and foster a sense of shared purpose. Whether planning a company off-site or celebrating a milestone event, these activities provide a memorable team experience that goes beyond the typical happy hour.
