The Rockwell

4.6★ (317 reviews)

The Rockwell in Somerville is a beloved local venue that offers teams a chance to unleash their inner comedic talents through interactive improv workshops. With a cozy 150-seat theater and a friendly neighborhood bar vibe, it's the perfect setting for groups to let loose, bond over shared laughter, and hone their quick-thinking skills.

What to Expect

When you book an improv session at The Rockwell, your team will be led through a series of fun, low-pressure exercises and games designed to get everyone comfortable on stage. No prior experience is necessary - the expert instructors will guide you through the basics of improv and help you tap into your natural creativity and sense of humor.

Who It's For

The Rockwell's improv workshops are ideal for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to inject some levity and team spirit into their workday. The shared experience of getting up on stage and supporting one another through the silly, spontaneous exercises creates an instant camaraderie that will serve your group long after the session ends.
