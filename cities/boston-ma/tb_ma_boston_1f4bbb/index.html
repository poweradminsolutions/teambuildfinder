Pottery Studio 1 - Pottery Classes and Events

4.9★ (755 reviews) $$$ 2 hours

Located in the heart of Boston, Pottery Studio 1 provides a unique and engaging team building experience. Guests can unleash their creativity through hands-on pottery classes, working together to craft personalized pieces that capture the spirit of their group.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, the Pottery Studio 1 team will guide your group through a 2-hour pottery workshop. Participants will learn the fundamentals of wheel-throwing and hand-building techniques, then collaborate to design and create their own ceramic works of art. No previous experience is necessary - just a willingness to get your hands dirty and have fun.

Who It's For

This activity is perfect for teams of 10-30 looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The pottery-making process encourages communication, problem-solving, and camaraderie, making it an excellent choice for corporate retreats, holiday parties, or any group seeking a memorable team building experience.
