Improvolution

5.0★ (43 reviews)

Improvolution in New York, NY brings the power of improv comedy to team building. Experienced facilitators guide groups through fun, fast-paced exercises that build collaboration, communication, and creative problem-solving skills in a supportive environment.

What to Expect

Arrive ready to play, experiment, and surprise yourself. Your group will dive into a series of short improv games and scenes, starting with low-stakes warm-ups and building to more involved challenges. The format is flexible, with plenty of opportunities for feedback and reflection on how the principles of improv can apply back at the office.

Who It's For

Improvolution's team building workshops are a perfect fit for mid-sized groups (15-40 people) looking to strengthen bonds, boost morale, and unlock new ways of thinking. The shared experience of stepping out of comfort zones creates a sense of shared vulnerability that breaks down barriers between departments.
