It at Beantown Adventure Ropes Course

4.5★ (460 reviews)

Perched atop a scenic hilltop in Reading, MA, It at Beantown Adventure Ropes Course offers teams a thrilling outdoor challenge that builds camaraderie and problem-solving skills. This state-of-the-art facility features multiple high and low ropes elements, from a daring 50-foot freefall to a web of suspended bridges and platforms that will test your group's mettle.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will receive a comprehensive safety briefing and gear up with harnesses and other essential equipment. Then it's time to take on the course, working together to navigate the obstacles and support one another through the more daring feats. With dedicated facilitators on hand, the experience is equally fun and meaningful, designed to push participants out of their comfort zones in a safe, encouraging environment.

Who It's For

It at Beantown Adventure Ropes Course is perfect for medium-sized teams (12-30 people) looking to bond over a shared physical challenge. Whether you're planning a corporate team building event or a fun outing for your community group, this high-energy experience will leave your participants feeling accomplished, connected, and eager to tackle their next big project together.
