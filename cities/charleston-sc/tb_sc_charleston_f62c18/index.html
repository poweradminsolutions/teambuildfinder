Pluff Mud Pottery

4.8★ (37 reviews)

Pluff Mud Pottery in Charleston, SC is a gem for teams seeking a unique and hands-on creative experience. Their expert-led pottery workshops guide groups through the entire process of crafting their own personalized pottery pieces, from shaping the clay to decorating the final products.

What to Expect

Participants will learn fundamental pottery techniques like throwing on the wheel and hand-building. The class culminates with glazing, where teams work collaboratively to design and apply vibrant, one-of-a-kind finishes to their creations. No prior experience is necessary - just a willingness to get your hands a little muddy!

Who It's For

Pluff Mud Pottery's team building workshops are a great fit for groups of 8 to 20 looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The intimate class size and guided instruction make it an approachable activity for all skill levels, from artsy types to self-proclaimed non-creatives.
