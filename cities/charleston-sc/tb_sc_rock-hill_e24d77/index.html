Canaan Zipline Canopy Tours

4.9★ (242 reviews) $$$ 3 hours

Get your team off the ground at Canaan Zipline Canopy Tours in Rock Hill, South Carolina. This high-flying adventure park offers an adrenaline-fueled team building experience like no other, with a series of ziplines and ropes courses that challenge groups to work together and push their limits.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Canaan Zipline, you'll start with a safety briefing before being outfitted with the necessary gear. Then it's time to take to the trees, navigating a network of suspended bridges, platforms, and thrilling ziplines that range from 50 to over 1,000 feet long. The course is designed to get your heart racing while fostering collaboration and communication among your team.

Who It's For

Canaan Zipline is an excellent choice for medium-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a physically engaging team building activity. The shared sense of accomplishment from conquering the course tends to break down barriers between departments and build lasting bonds.
