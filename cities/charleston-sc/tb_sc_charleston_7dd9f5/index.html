iSmash Charleston

5.0★ (286 reviews)

iSmash Charleston offers a thrilling team building experience in the heart of Charleston, South Carolina. Their state-of-the-art escape rooms challenge groups to work together under pressure, solving intricate puzzles to break free before time runs out.

What to Expect

When your team arrives, you'll be divided into smaller groups and led into a fully immersive, themed escape room. You'll have just 60 minutes to scour the space, uncover clues, and crack the code that will unlock the door to freedom. Teamwork, communication, and critical thinking are key to emerging victorious.

Who It's For

iSmash's escape rooms are perfect for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to build camaraderie through a shared high-intensity challenge. The competitive yet collaborative nature of the activity tends to break down barriers between departments, making it an ideal team building activity for Charleston businesses.
