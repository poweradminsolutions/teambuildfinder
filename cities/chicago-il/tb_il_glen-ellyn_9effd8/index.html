Marcel's Culinary Experience

4.8★ (86 reviews)

Located in the charming Chicago suburb of Glen Ellyn, Marcel's Culinary Experience offers a delightful team building experience that combines the joy of cooking with the camaraderie of friendly competition. Groups of all levels can don their aprons and unleash their inner chefs, learning new skills and creating mouthwatering dishes together.

What to Expect

At Marcel's, teams work collaboratively to complete interactive cooking or baking challenges, putting their communication and problem-solving skills to the test. Under the guidance of skilled instructors, participants learn techniques like knife skills, sauce-making, and presentation, then work against the clock to prepare a full meal or dessert.

Who It's For

Marcel's Culinary Experience is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a unique and engaging team building activity. The shared experience of cooking a meal from scratch fosters bonding, boosts morale, and encourages colleagues to see each other in a new light.
