Orchestra Hall the Club

4.9★ (57 reviews)

Orchestra Hall the Club in Chicago, Illinois offers a truly unique team building experience through the power of music. Groups can unleash their creative rhythms and build a shared sense of teamwork by learning to play percussion instruments together under the guidance of professional musicians.

What to Expect

Participants dive into an immersive 90-minute drumming workshop, working together to master a rousing musical piece. No prior experience is required - the expert instructors will teach everyone the fundamentals and get the whole group playing in harmony.

Who It's For

This hands-on musical activity is perfect for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to bond, communicate better, and tap into their collective creativity. The shared challenge of learning to play as an ensemble tends to break down barriers between departments in a fun, high-energy way.
