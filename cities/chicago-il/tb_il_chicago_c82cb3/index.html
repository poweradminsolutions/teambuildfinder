Expansive 100 S. State Street

5.0★ (70 reviews)

Expansive's flexible event spaces in downtown Chicago are a hub for team building activities that bring groups together through shared experiences. From facilitated workshops to immersive retreats, their expert facilitators guide teams through innovative, design-thinking exercises that spark collaboration and problem-solving.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be welcomed into a modern, light-filled workspace designed to foster creativity and connection. Activities like escape room challenges, scavenger hunts, and 'build-your-own' projects encourage friendly competition and build communication skills in a practical, hands-on setting.

Who It's For

Expansive's team building offerings are ideal for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds, improve cross-functional alignment, or simply have fun together. The versatile spaces and customizable programs make it a great choice for companies, nonprofits, and other organizations seeking an engaging team experience in the heart of Chicago.
