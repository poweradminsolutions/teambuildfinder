The Wooden Spoon

4.4★ (49 reviews)

The Wooden Spoon in Chicago, IL is a cozy culinary hub that offers a unique team building experience through their hands-on cooking classes. Groups can don their aprons and work together to prepare a delicious, multi-course meal from scratch, all while developing important collaboration and communication skills.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be welcomed into the Wooden Spoon's warm, inviting kitchen space. An experienced chef instructor will guide you through the menu, providing demonstrations and tips as you chop, sauté, and plate the dishes. The interactive format encourages friendly competition as you race against the clock to complete each tasty course.

Who It's For

These cooking classes are ideal for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to mix things up from the typical team building activities. The shared challenge of creating a restaurant-quality meal together tends to break down barriers between departments and build camaraderie in a fun, relaxed setting.
