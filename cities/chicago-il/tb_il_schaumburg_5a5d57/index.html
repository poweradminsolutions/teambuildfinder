Zero Latency VR Chicago - Schaumburg

5.0★ (1359 reviews) 2-4 hours

Zero Latency VR Chicago delivers cutting-edge virtual reality team experiences in Schaumburg, where groups navigate immersive digital worlds together using wireless headsets and motion tracking technology. Teams can battle zombies, explore alien planets, or solve intricate puzzles in shared virtual environments that feel remarkably real.

What to Expect

Your group will be fitted with VR headsets and backpack computers, then enter a large open play area where you can move freely within the virtual world. Sessions typically last 30-45 minutes, with teams working together to complete missions while seeing and interacting with each other's avatars in real-time.

Who It's For

Ideal for tech-curious teams of 6-8 people who want something completely different from traditional team building. The collaborative problem-solving in virtual environments creates unique bonding moments that translate into better real-world communication.
