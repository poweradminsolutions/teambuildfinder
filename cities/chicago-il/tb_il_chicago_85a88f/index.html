The iO Theater

4.8★ (1775 reviews)

The iO Theater brings professional improv training to corporate teams through hands-on workshops that build communication skills and creative thinking. Chicago's premier comedy destination transforms groups into performers through guided exercises that emphasize listening, collaboration, and thinking on your feet. The warehouse-style venue creates an relaxed atmosphere where colleagues can step outside their comfort zones together.

What to Expect

Teams participate in structured improv games and exercises led by professional comedians and instructors. Sessions typically begin with warm-up activities to get everyone comfortable, then progress through scene-building exercises that require quick thinking and group cooperation. No performing experience is necessary - the focus stays on teamwork skills rather than entertainment value.

Who It's For

Ideal for groups of 10-25 people who want to improve communication and break down workplace hierarchies. The collaborative nature of improv works particularly well for teams struggling with creative problem-solving or departments that need to work more fluidly together.
