InfiVR - Leading Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality & Mixed Reality Company (VR / AR / MR)

4.7★ (43 reviews)

As one of Chicago's premier virtual reality providers, InfiVR offers cutting-edge immersive experiences that transform team-building activities. Their state-of-the-art VR, AR, and mixed reality technology transports groups to dynamic, engaging environments where they can collaborate, compete, and bond in unique and memorable ways.

What to Expect

When you visit InfiVR, your team will be outfitted with high-quality VR headsets and equipped with intuitive controls. You'll then dive into fully-realized virtual worlds, taking on challenges like navigating treacherous obstacle courses, solving intricate puzzles, or even launching rockets together. The shared sense of discovery and achievement is sure to bring your group closer.

Who It's For

InfiVR's virtual experiences are ideal for mid-size to large teams (10-50 people) looking to break out of the traditional team-building mold. Whether you're planning an off-site retreat, celebrating a milestone, or simply want to shake up your routine, these cutting-edge activities will energize your crew and leave them talking for weeks.
