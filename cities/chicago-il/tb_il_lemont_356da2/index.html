The Forge Adventure Park & Ziplines

4.5★ (1320 reviews)

Built in a former quarry, The Forge Adventure Park transforms Lemont's industrial past into an adrenaline-packed team building destination. Groups tackle high ropes courses, zip lines, and climbing walls while navigating the unique terrain of carved limestone cliffs and wooded trails. The setting creates natural conversation starters as teams push comfort zones together.

What to Expect

Teams rotate through multiple challenge stations including aerial obstacle courses, rock climbing walls, and zip line circuits that span the quarry landscape. Most activities require spotting and encouragement from teammates, creating organic opportunities for collaboration. Groups typically spend 2-3 hours on site with guided instruction at each station.

Who It's For

Ideal for athletic teams and departments comfortable with physical challenges, typically 8-25 people. The outdoor setting and adventure elements work especially well for groups wanting to escape office environments and build trust through shared risk-taking.
