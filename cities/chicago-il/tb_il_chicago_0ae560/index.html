PARK WEST CERAMICS

4.7★ (34 reviews)

Located in the heart of Chicago, Park West Ceramics offers a unique and engaging team building experience through their hands-on art workshops. Participants will dive into the creative process of pottery making, working together to craft one-of-a-kind ceramic pieces under the guidance of skilled instructors.

What to Expect

Groups begin by learning the basics of pottery techniques, from wedging clay to shaping forms on the potter's wheel. The interactive session encourages collaboration as teammates provide feedback and support each other's creative endeavors. By the end, everyone will have a personalized ceramic work of art to take home as a memento of their team-building adventure.

Who It's For

This activity works particularly well for medium-sized teams (10-25 people) looking to bond over a shared creative challenge. The tactile, artisanal nature of the workshop helps to foster a relaxed, playful atmosphere that's ideal for boosting morale and breaking down barriers between colleagues.
