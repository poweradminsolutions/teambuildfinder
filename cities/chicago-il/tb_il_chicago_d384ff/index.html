The Fulton West

5.0★ (38 reviews)

Situated in the heart of Chicago, The Fulton West is a versatile wedding venue that also excels at hosting dynamic team building events. With a range of facilitated workshops and immersive retreats, this spacious and flexible space allows corporate groups to come together, challenge themselves, and forge stronger connections.

What to Expect

Teams can expect an engaging and interactive experience that gets them out of the office and into a vibrant, creative environment. Whether it's a high-energy escape room challenge or a guided problem-solving session, the dedicated staff at The Fulton West will work closely with you to design a customized program that meets your group's unique needs and goals.

Who It's For

The Fulton West is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking to foster camaraderie and communication through shared experiences. The variety of activity options makes it suitable for both active, competitive outings as well as more reflective, collaborative workshops.
