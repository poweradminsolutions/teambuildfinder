PanIQ Escape Room Chicago (Logan Square)

4.9★ (834 reviews)

PanIQ Escape Room Chicago delivers immersive puzzle-solving experiences where teams must work together to escape themed rooms within a 60-minute time limit. Located in the vibrant Logan Square neighborhood, this escape room center challenges groups with intricate storylines, hidden clues, and creative problem-solving scenarios that reveal team dynamics under pressure.

What to Expect

Teams are briefed on their mission and locked into elaborately designed rooms with specific themes and objectives. Working against the clock, groups must communicate effectively, delegate tasks, and combine different perspectives to uncover clues, solve puzzles, and ultimately escape before time runs out.

Who It's For

Ideal for small to medium-sized teams who want an engaging mental challenge that highlights natural problem-solvers and communication styles. The time pressure creates genuine collaboration moments that translate well back to workplace scenarios.
