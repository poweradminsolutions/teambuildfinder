Liven It Up Events

5.0★ (81 reviews)

Liven It Up Events in Chicago offers team-building activities with a twist. From competitive scavenger hunts that send your group racing around the city to lively trivia nights perfect for bonding over a little friendly competition, their event planners know how to inject energy and excitement into any corporate gathering.

What to Expect

Liven It Up's team-building events are designed to get people moving, collaborating, and thinking outside the box. Scavenger hunts send small teams on a high-energy quest to solve puzzles and complete challenges, while trivia nights foster camaraderie as coworkers band together to showcase their knowledge (and have a few laughs in the process).

Who It's For

Liven It Up's team-building activities work well for medium to large corporate groups (15-80 people) looking to strengthen interpersonal connections and blow off some steam. The shared experience of working toward a common goal tends to break down barriers between departments faster than a standard happy hour.
