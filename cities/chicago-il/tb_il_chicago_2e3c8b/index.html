Game Night Out

5.0★ (1125 reviews)

Game Night Out transforms traditional board games and trivia into competitive team experiences that reveal hidden dynamics within your group. This Chicago entertainment venue specializes in structured game tournaments where colleagues face off in everything from strategy games to pop culture challenges. The atmosphere strikes the right balance between competitive and collaborative, making it easy for even reserved team members to engage.

What to Expect

Your group will rotate through multiple game stations, competing in both team-based and individual challenges led by experienced game hosts. Sessions typically include a mix of classic board games, custom trivia rounds, and interactive group challenges that require strategy and communication. The venue handles all setup and scorekeeping, so teams can focus on the competition rather than logistics.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 10-50 who want intellectual competition without physical demands. The format works especially well for mixed groups with varying comfort levels, since the variety of games ensures everyone finds their strength.
