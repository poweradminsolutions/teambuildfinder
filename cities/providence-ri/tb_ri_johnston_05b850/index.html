Ocean State Escape Rooms

5.0★ (267 reviews)

Located in Johnston, Rhode Island, Ocean State Escape Rooms offers an immersive and challenging team building experience. Groups must work together to solve intricate puzzles and escape the themed room before time runs out, fostering camaraderie and problem-solving skills in a high-energy setting.

What to Expect

When you arrive, your group will be ushered into a meticulously designed escape room, such as the Pirate's Cove or the Wizard's Lair. You'll have 60 minutes to search for clues, crack codes, and collaborate to unlock the door before time expires. Along the way, you'll need to think creatively and communicate effectively to overcome the obstacles.

Who It's For

Ocean State Escape Rooms is perfect for medium-sized teams (8-14 people) looking to bond over a shared, high-stakes challenge. The competitive yet cooperative nature of the activity helps break down barriers and build trust between colleagues, making it an ideal choice for corporate team building or social events.
