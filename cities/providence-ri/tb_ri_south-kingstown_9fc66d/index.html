Kingdom Axe

5.0★ (103 reviews)

Kingdom Axe in South Kingstown, RI offers a thrilling team building experience that's perfect for groups looking to bond over a shared adrenaline-fueled challenge. This axe throwing venue provides expert instruction and a safe, supervised environment where teams can master the art of hurtling sharp blades at wooden targets.

What to Expect

When you arrive, the friendly staff will provide a safety briefing and hands-on training to ensure everyone feels confident throwing their first axe. Then it's time to put those newfound skills to the test, as your team competes in a series of fun, skill-based competitions to see who can land the most bullseyes.

Who It's For

Kingdom Axe is best suited for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to step outside their comfort zones and try something new together. The shared sense of accomplishment and healthy competition makes this a fantastic choice for company retreats, bachelor/bachelorette parties, or any group seeking an unforgettable bonding experience.
