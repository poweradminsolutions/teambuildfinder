The RI Riddle Room: Escape Games

4.9★ (618 reviews)

The RI Riddle Room in Warwick, Rhode Island offers an exciting escape room experience that's perfect for team building. Groups of 2-10 players work together to solve a series of themed puzzles and clues, testing their problem-solving skills under the pressure of a ticking clock.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be ushered into a themed room and tasked with escaping within 60 minutes. You'll need to search for hidden items, crack codes, and collaborate to piece together the puzzle. It's an immersive, adrenaline-fueled challenge that encourages communication, critical thinking, and teamwork.

Who It's For

The RI Riddle Room's escape rooms are an excellent choice for mid-size teams (10-40 players) looking to bond over a shared challenge. The high-energy, competitive nature of the activity helps break down barriers and builds trust between coworkers faster than a typical team-building exercise.
