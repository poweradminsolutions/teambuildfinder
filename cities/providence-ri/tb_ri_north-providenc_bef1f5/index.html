The Overlook at Meehan

4.9★ (50 reviews)

Tucked away in the heart of North Providence, The Overlook at Meehan offers a serene and picturesque setting for team retreats. With its stunning views of the surrounding countryside, this historic property provides the perfect backdrop for groups to bond, brainstorm, and challenge each other in a variety of team-building activities.

What to Expect

At The Overlook, groups can expect a personalized experience tailored to their needs. From outdoor scavenger hunts and wilderness survival workshops to indoor team-based problem-solving exercises, the dedicated staff will work with you to design a program that brings out the best in your team. The venue's flexible indoor and outdoor spaces ensure that your retreat is engaging and productive, no matter the weather.

Who It's For

The Overlook at Meehan is an ideal choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a unique and immersive team-building experience. Whether you're looking to foster collaboration, enhance communication, or simply have fun, the variety of activities and the picturesque setting make this venue a standout option for groups in North Providence and the surrounding area.
