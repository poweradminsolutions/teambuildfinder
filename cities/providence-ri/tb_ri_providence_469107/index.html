Sprout CoWorking Providence

4.8★ (59 reviews)

Located in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island, Sprout CoWorking offers a dynamic and versatile space for team building activities. From facilitated workshops that spark creativity to virtual escape rooms that test problem-solving skills, this coworking hub has a range of experiences to bring your group closer together.

What to Expect

When you visit Sprout, your team can expect a tailored itinerary with engaging activities led by skilled facilitators. Whether it's an interactive workshop or a friendly competition like online trivia, the flow is designed to keep your group energized and collaborating. The modern, open-concept venue provides the perfect backdrop for building team camaraderie.

Who It's For

Sprout's team building offerings are ideal for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds and boost morale. The variety of experience types, from hands-on retreats to hybrid virtual games, makes Sprout a great fit for both in-person and remote teams seeking a fun, productive day away from the office.
