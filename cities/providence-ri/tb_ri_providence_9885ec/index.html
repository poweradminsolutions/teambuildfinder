The Pavilion at Grace

4.8★ (10 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island, The Pavilion at Grace offers teams a serene and inspiring setting for immersive retreats. With its stunning architectural design and tranquil outdoor spaces, this venue creates the perfect backdrop for groups to reconnect, collaborate, and push their boundaries through engaging team-building activities.

What to Expect

At The Pavilion, team building experiences are tailored to the unique needs of each group. Facilitators guide participants through a variety of interactive exercises, from problem-solving challenges to creative workshops, all designed to foster communication, leadership, and camaraderie. The flexible indoor and outdoor spaces allow for a dynamic flow between activities, keeping the energy high and the team engaged throughout the day.

Who It's For

The Pavilion at Grace is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams of 15-40 looking to strengthen their bonds and unlock new levels of collective performance. Whether planning a company retreat, a leadership development program, or a departmental team-building day, the versatile nature of the venue and the expertise of the facilitators ensure a truly transformative experience.
