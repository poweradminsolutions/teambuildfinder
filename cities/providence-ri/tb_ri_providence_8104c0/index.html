Level99 Providence

4.9★ (2585 reviews)

Level99 Providence transforms team building into an immersive adventure through meticulously designed challenge rooms that blend escape room elements with interactive puzzles and collaborative problem-solving. Teams navigate through themed environments that require different skill sets, from logic and creativity to communication and leadership. The Providence location combines the thrill of gaming with meaningful team development in a sophisticated recreational setting.

What to Expect

Groups rotate through multiple challenge rooms, each presenting unique puzzles that require 3-5 people working together to progress. Teams face time-sensitive scenarios where success depends on pooling different perspectives and delegating tasks effectively. The experience includes both high-energy competitive elements and strategic thinking challenges, with facilitators tracking progress and highlighting team dynamics throughout.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 12-30 who want something more engaging than traditional trust falls but less physically demanding than outdoor adventures. The varied challenge format works particularly well for mixed groups where different personality types can showcase their strengths.
