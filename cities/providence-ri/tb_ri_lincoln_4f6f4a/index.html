Time Mission - Lincoln, RI / R1 Indoor Karting

4.9★ (455 reviews) $$ 90 min

Nestled in the heart of Lincoln, Rhode Island, Time Mission at R1 Indoor Karting offers a thrilling team building experience that combines the adrenaline of go-kart racing with the challenge of an immersive escape room. This unique hybrid activity is perfect for groups seeking a high-energy, collaborative adventure that will bring your team closer together.

What to Expect

When your group arrives, you'll be divided into teams and assigned to a custom-built escape room. With the clock ticking, you'll need to work together to solve a series of puzzles and challenges in order to escape the room before time runs out. In between sessions, your team can refuel with some friendly competition on the state-of-the-art go-kart track.

Who It's For

Time Mission is an excellent choice for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to inject some healthy competition and camaraderie into their team building activities. The combination of fast-paced racing and high-stakes problem solving creates an unforgettable shared experience that will have your colleagues bonding in no time.
