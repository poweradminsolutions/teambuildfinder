International Banquet & Conference

4.6★ (56 reviews)

Located in the heart of Detroit, International Banquet & Conference offers a versatile space for team building activities that bring groups together. From interactive cooking classes to high-energy scavenger hunts, this expansive banquet hall provides a dynamic setting to foster collaboration and camaraderie.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a tailored experience at International Banquet & Conference. The staff works closely with organizers to curate team building itineraries, whether it's a friendly cooking competition or an elaborate themed event. Participants will enjoy ample space to mingle, strategize, and put their problem-solving skills to the test.

Who It's For

This venue works well for mid-sized teams (20-50) looking to strengthen interpersonal bonds through shared challenges and experiences. The versatile space accommodates a range of team building activities, making it an ideal choice for corporate retreats, department offsites, or any group seeking to build trust and boost morale.
