Outdoor Adventure Center

4.7★ (1706 reviews)

The Outdoor Adventure Center brings authentic wilderness experiences indoors through simulated outdoor recreation and immersive nature displays. Teams explore Detroit's connection to Michigan's natural heritage while participating in guided activities that combine education with hands-on adventure challenges.

What to Expect

Groups rotate through interactive stations featuring simulated biking trails, nature exploration challenges, and collaborative problem-solving activities based on outdoor scenarios. The museum format allows teams to move at their own pace while facilitating guided discussions about local ecosystems and conservation.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams seeking an educational yet active experience that doesn't require actual outdoor conditions. Works particularly well for mixed-ability groups since activities can be adapted for different fitness levels.
