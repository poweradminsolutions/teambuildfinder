Rage Room

4.7★ (191 reviews)

Unleash your team's primal side at Rage Room in Madison Heights, Michigan. This adrenaline-fueled experience invites groups to shatter, smash, and smash their way through a carefully curated collection of everyday objects, providing a cathartic release and an unforgettable team-building adventure.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be outfitted with protective gear and given a selection of items to destroy, from electronics to crockery. Work together to strategize your attack, then let loose in a private rage room, taking turns venting your frustrations while your colleagues cheer you on.

Who It's For

This high-energy activity works best for mid-size teams (15-40) looking to bond over a shared physical challenge. The communal experience of destroying common objects in a controlled environment can help break down barriers and build trust between colleagues from different departments.
