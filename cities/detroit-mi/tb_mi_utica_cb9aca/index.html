Activate Games

5.0★ (593 reviews)

Activate Games in Utica, MI is a unique destination that combines cutting-edge technology with classic team building activities. From high-tech virtual escape rooms to fast-paced laser tag battles, this adventure hub offers a dynamic range of experiences to bring your group closer together.

What to Expect

When you arrive at Activate Games, your team will be immersed in a world of interactive challenges. Start with a virtual escape room, working collaboratively to crack codes, solve puzzles, and beat the clock. Then, test your physical skills in a competitive round of laser tag or paintball. The energy is electric as colleagues become teammates, bonding over shared triumphs and moments of hilarity.

Who It's For

Activate Games is perfect for mid-size teams (15-40 people) looking to inject some adrenaline and healthy competition into their team building agenda. The varied activity options cater to all interests, making it an ideal choice for bringing together departments or celebrating a company milestone.
