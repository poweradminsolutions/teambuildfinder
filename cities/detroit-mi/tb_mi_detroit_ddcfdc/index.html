Brightmoor Artisans Collective

4.6★ (93 reviews)

Brightmoor Artisans Collective in Detroit is a unique community hub that offers hands-on team building opportunities unlike anything else in the area. Get your group of do-gooders together for a rewarding day of volunteering, environmental cleanup, and connecting with the local community.

What to Expect

When you visit Brightmoor, your team will roll up their sleeves and tackle a variety of projects, from sorting donations at the food bank to revitalizing a neighborhood green space. The staff will provide all the necessary tools and guidance to make your volunteer work productive and fulfilling.

Who It's For

This is an excellent option for teams of 10-30 who want to make a positive impact while strengthening bonds. The collaborative nature of the work and shared sense of purpose create an uplifting experience that brings colleagues closer together.
