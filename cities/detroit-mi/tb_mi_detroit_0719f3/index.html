The Elephant Room

4.3★ (542 reviews)

The Elephant Room in Detroit is an ideal spot for team building events that bring groups together through friendly competition and creativity. This buzzy, brick-lined venue with a tin ceiling and elevated pub menu creates a lively, relaxed atmosphere perfect for team trivia challenges and hands-on craft workshops.

What to Expect

At The Elephant Room, teams can put their knowledge to the test with trivia games that foster collaboration and strategic thinking. The venue also hosts interactive craft workshops where coworkers can bond over shared projects like making custom glassware or painting murals. Whatever the activity, the Elephant Room's experienced event staff ensures a smooth, enjoyable experience for the whole group.

Who It's For

The Elephant Room's team building options work well for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to break out of the boardroom. The shared challenges and activities help colleagues from different departments connect in a casual, fun setting - much more effective than a standard happy hour.
