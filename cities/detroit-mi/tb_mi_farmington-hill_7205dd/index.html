Eleven11 Event Venue

4.7★ (173 reviews)

Eleven11 Event Venue in Farmington Hills, MI provides dynamic, facilitated team building workshops and offsite retreats perfect for bringing groups together. Their experienced facilitators tailor each session to your team's unique needs, blending interactive activities with thoughtful discussion to foster stronger bonds and sharper collaboration skills.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a hands-on, engaging experience at Eleven11. Sessions might include competitive escape room challenges, high-energy team games, or immersive problem-solving exercises - all designed to get people working together in new ways. The flow keeps participants energized and invested throughout.

Who It's For

Eleven11's team building offerings work well for mid-size groups (15-40 people) looking to breakthrough silos and strengthen cross-functional relationships. The shared experiences and takeaways make it an excellent choice for company offsites, leadership retreats, or any occasion when a group needs to get out of the office and bond.
