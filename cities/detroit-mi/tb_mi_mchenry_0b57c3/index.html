Monkey Business Adventure Park

4.5★ (205 reviews) $$ 45-90 minutes per Level

Nestled in the heart of McHenry, Maryland, Monkey Business Adventure Park offers a thrilling and team-building experience that's perfect for corporate groups, sports teams, and adventure-seekers alike. With its high-flying ropes courses and challenging obstacle challenges, this outdoor oasis pushes participants to work together, build trust, and push their physical and mental limits in a safe, supportive environment.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be outfitted with safety gear and given a comprehensive safety briefing. Then, you'll embark on a guided tour of the park's impressive aerial obstacle courses, navigating suspended platforms, zip lines, and more as you work together to conquer each challenge. The park's skilled instructors are on hand to provide encouragement and ensure everyone's safety throughout the experience.

Who It's For

Monkey Business Adventure Park is ideal for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to foster camaraderie, improve communication, and boost morale through a shared, adrenaline-fueled challenge. The park's competitive yet supportive atmosphere helps break down barriers and encourages participants to step outside their comfort zones and rely on one another.
