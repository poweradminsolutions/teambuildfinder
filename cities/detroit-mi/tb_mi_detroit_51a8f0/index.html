710 Hall

4.2★ (90 reviews)

710 Hall in Detroit, Michigan is a versatile event space that excels at hosting engaging team building workshops and retreats. From facilitated communication exercises to high-energy competitive challenges, the experienced staff at 710 Hall can customize an experience to bring any group closer together.

What to Expect

When your team arrives at 710 Hall, they'll be greeted by an open, modern event space ready to be transformed. The staff will kick things off with an energizing icebreaker to get everyone comfortable, then transition into the core team building activity. This could involve solving an immersive escape room puzzle, participating in a lively cooking competition, or tapping into their creativity through a guided art project.

Who It's For

710 Hall's team building offerings are a perfect fit for medium-sized groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen interpersonal bonds and improve collaboration. Whether you're planning an off-site for your department or a company-wide retreat, the flexible format and hands-on activities create an atmosphere of shared learning and fun.
