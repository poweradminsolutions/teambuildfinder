The Shadow Gallery Detroit

4.3★ (159 reviews)

The Shadow Gallery in Detroit is a unique event space that offers immersive team-building experiences perfect for groups looking to break out of the ordinary. With its moody, industrial-chic ambiance, The Shadow Gallery creates an energizing environment where teams can collaborate, problem-solve, and bond through a variety of engaging activities.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from the venue's roster of interactive team challenges, such as an adrenaline-pumping escape room or a high-stakes scavenger hunt that puts problem-solving skills to the test. The hands-on nature of these activities encourages teamwork and communication in a fun, fast-paced setting.

Who It's For

The Shadow Gallery is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking a memorable team-building outing. The shared experience of navigating the venue's puzzles and challenges can quickly break down barriers and foster camaraderie among co-workers, making it an ideal option for corporate retreats or social outings.
