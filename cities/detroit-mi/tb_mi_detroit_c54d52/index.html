Belle Isle Casino

4.5★ (363 reviews)

The Belle Isle Casino in Detroit, Michigan offers a unique and historic setting for team building activities. With its elegant 1908 architecture and stunning views of the Detroit River, this versatile event venue provides the perfect backdrop for guided workshops, corporate retreats, and other collaborative experiences.

What to Expect

Groups can expect a seamless, professionally-facilitated team building event at the Belle Isle Casino. Activities range from interactive problem-solving challenges to immersive teambuilding exercises that encourage communication, leadership, and trust-building among participants.

Who It's For

The Belle Isle Casino is an excellent choice for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds and boost morale through shared experiences. The venue's combination of classic charm and modern amenities makes it well-suited for a variety of team building occasions, from annual offsites to department-level workshops.
