Think It Out Escape Rooms

4.9★ (154 reviews) 1 hour

Tucked away in the heart of Fraser, Michigan, Think It Out Escape Rooms offers a thrilling team building experience like no other. With a focus on immersive, story-driven escape rooms, your group will be transported to a different world, working together to solve a series of clever puzzles and challenges before time runs out.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be briefed on the premise of the escape room and given 60-90 minutes to work together and unlock the secrets within. You'll scour the room for hidden clues, crack codes, and put your critical thinking skills to the test, all while bonding over the shared mission to escape before the timer hits zero.

Who It's For

Think It Out Escape Rooms is an excellent choice for teams of 4-10 looking to build camaraderie and improve communication. The high-stakes, collaborative nature of the escape room format encourages problem-solving, leadership, and creative thinking - skills that are essential for any successful team.
