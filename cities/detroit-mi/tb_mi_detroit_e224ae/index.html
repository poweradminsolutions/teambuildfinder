Saint Andrew’s Hall

4.6★ (4197 reviews) 3 hours

Saint Andrew's Hall transforms Detroit's historic venue space into an unconventional backdrop for facilitated team workshops and corporate retreats. The venue's three distinct areas—including the main concert hall with its storied stage—create dynamic environments where facilitators can design immersive experiences that break teams out of typical conference room thinking.

What to Expect

Your group works with professional facilitators in spaces designed for performance and energy rather than boardroom presentations. Teams rotate through interactive workshop stations or participate in large-group activities on the main stage, using the venue's theatrical atmosphere to encourage participation and creative problem-solving.

Who It's For

Ideal for creative teams and companies wanting to shake up their usual retreat format with an edgier, arts-focused venue. The historic concert hall setting works particularly well for groups in media, marketing, or entertainment industries who appreciate the venue's musical heritage.
