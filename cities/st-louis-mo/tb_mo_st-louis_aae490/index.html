Big Muddy Adventures

5.0★ (160 reviews)

Big Muddy Adventures in St. Louis offers groups an exhilarating way to bond through water sports and hiking excursions. As one of the top-rated tour operators in the region, they lead unforgettable outings on the scenic Big Muddy River and nearby nature trails.

What to Expect

Teams can choose from a variety of guided experiences, like paddling tandem kayaks or canoes down the river, or embarking on a naturalist-led hike through the wooded Ozark hills. No prior experience is necessary - the friendly staff provide all the gear and instruction needed to ensure a fun, safe adventure.

Who It's For

These team-building activities work well for medium to large groups (15-50 people) looking to step away from the office, get active, and strengthen connections through shared challenges and discoveries. The physical nature of the experiences tends to break down barriers between co-workers faster than a happy hour ever could.
