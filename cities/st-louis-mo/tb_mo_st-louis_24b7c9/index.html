The Berry’s Banquet Hall

4.5★ (23 reviews)

The Berry's Banquet Hall in St. Louis, MO is a versatile event venue that offers exceptional team building retreats. With its spacious banquet hall, scenic outdoor grounds, and experienced event staff, this local gem is a top choice for groups seeking an immersive team experience.

What to Expect

When you book a team building retreat at The Berry's, your group will enjoy a carefully curated agenda of interactive activities and challenges. From collaborative problem-solving exercises to friendly sports competitions, the hands-on format encourages bonding, communication, and friendly rivalry among coworkers.

Who It's For

The Berry's Banquet Hall is an excellent option for medium-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to strengthen interpersonal skills and company culture. The varied team building programs work well for corporate offsites, end-of-year celebrations, or onboarding new hires.
