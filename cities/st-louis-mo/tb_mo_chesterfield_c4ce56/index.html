The Reverie STL

4.7★ (24 reviews)

The Reverie STL in Chesterfield, Missouri offers a stunning scenic backdrop for team-building retreats. This tranquil venue combines inspiring natural beauty with thoughtfully designed spaces to foster connection and collaboration among groups seeking a restorative getaway.

What to Expect

Groups can immerse themselves in curated wellness experiences, from guided meditation sessions to outdoor adventure activities like hiking and kayaking. The Reverie also features dedicated meeting spaces equipped with the latest tech to support productive work sessions and strategic planning.

Who It's For

The Reverie's serene setting and flexible offerings make it an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to recharge, bond, and return to the office feeling rejuvenated and aligned.
