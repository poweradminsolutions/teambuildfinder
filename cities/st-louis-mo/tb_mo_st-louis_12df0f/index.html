Top Notch Axe Throwing St. Louis

4.9★ (2896 reviews) $$ 1.5-Hour

Top Notch Axe Throwing brings medieval weaponry into the modern team building arena with guided axe throwing sessions in St. Louis. Groups rotate through lanes where instructors teach proper throwing technique before facilitating friendly competitions. The primal satisfaction of landing a bullseye creates instant camaraderie and plenty of celebratory moments.

What to Expect

Your group receives safety briefing and throwing instruction from certified coaches before breaking into smaller groups at individual lanes. Teams take turns hurling hatchets at wooden targets, with coaches offering technique tips and scorekeeping. The session builds from basic throws to tournament-style brackets where departments can compete head-to-head.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams craving something more adventurous than typical corporate activities, especially groups comfortable with mild physical activity. The unique nature of axe throwing generates natural conversation starters and shared stories that last long after the session ends.
