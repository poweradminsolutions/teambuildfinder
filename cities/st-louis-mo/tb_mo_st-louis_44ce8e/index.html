9th Street Abbey

4.4★ (28 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of St. Louis, the 9th Street Abbey offers a unique team building experience that combines history, nature, and friendly competition. This converted historic abbey features lush gardens, a cozy beer garden, and plenty of indoor and outdoor spaces perfect for group activities.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from a variety of team-building options, including guided nature hikes through the on-site trails, interactive music/drumming workshops, and lively trivia nights in the beer garden. No matter the activity, the 9th Street Abbey provides a fun, engaging, and memorable setting for teams to bond and work together.

Who It's For

The 9th Street Abbey is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking a versatile venue that can accommodate a range of team building needs. Whether your group is looking to get active outdoors, tap into their creative side, or simply enjoy some friendly competition, this unique St. Louis destination has something to offer.
