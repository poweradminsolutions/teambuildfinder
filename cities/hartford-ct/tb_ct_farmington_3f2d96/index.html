Complexity: A Puzzling Adventure

4.8★ (310 reviews) 1 hour

Complexity: A Puzzling Adventure in Farmington, CT offers a one-of-a-kind team building experience through their captivating escape rooms. Groups are immersed in meticulously crafted, themed environments and challenged to put their collective wit and problem-solving skills to the test as they race against the clock to find the way out.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, teams are outfitted with a headset and briefed on the objective and rules. They're then locked inside a themed room filled with puzzles, riddles, and hidden clues. Working together, participants must uncover the secrets and strategize their way to a successful escape before time runs out.

Who It's For

Complexity's escape rooms are a perfect fit for mid-sized teams of 15-40 seeking a dynamic, high-energy team building activity. The shared challenge of beating the clock and 'escaping' the room encourages collaboration, communication, and creative thinking - essential skills that translate seamlessly back to the workplace.
