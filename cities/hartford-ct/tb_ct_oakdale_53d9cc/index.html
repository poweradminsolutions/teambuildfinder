Supercharged Indoor Karting and Axe Throwing

4.4★ (2079 reviews)

High-octane team building meets competitive camaraderie at this Oakdale venue that combines electric go-kart racing with axe throwing challenges. Teams can split time between the two activities or focus on one, with both offering natural opportunities for friendly rivalry and group bonding. The sports bar atmosphere keeps the energy flowing between sessions.

What to Expect

Groups typically start with a safety briefing before dividing into smaller teams for racing heats or axe throwing rotations. The go-kart track features electric karts that provide consistent speed and handling, while the axe throwing area includes trained instructors who teach proper technique. Most team events run 2-3 hours with time built in for socializing at the sports bar area.

Who It's For

Ideal for corporate groups of 20-60 people who want active competition without requiring athletic ability. The dual activities work particularly well for mixed groups where some prefer racing while others gravitate toward axe throwing.
