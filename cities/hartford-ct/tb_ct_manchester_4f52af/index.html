Urban Air Trampoline and Adventure Park

4.1★ (913 reviews)

Urban Air brings high-energy team building to Manchester through multi-level obstacle courses, rock climbing walls, and competitive trampoline activities. Teams rotate through various challenge stations that demand communication, trust, and mutual support to navigate successfully. The combination of physical challenges and shared adrenaline creates natural bonding opportunities that carry back to the workplace.

What to Expect

Groups typically spend 90 minutes to two hours moving through different activity zones, with team members spotting each other on climbing walls and cheering teammates through rope course elements. The facilitators can structure friendly competitions between departments or focus on collaborative challenges where success depends on the entire group working together. Teams finish with open jump time on the trampolines for decompression and celebration.

Who It's For

Ideal for active teams of 20-50 people who want to step outside their comfort zones together. The physical nature of the activities naturally reveals leadership qualities and builds trust between colleagues who might rarely interact in the office environment.
