Loft 147

5.0★ (6 reviews) Flexible

Loft 147 in Windsor, CT offers dynamic team building workshops and retreats in a bright, open-concept event space. With a focus on facilitated activities, groups dive into interactive exercises that challenge them to think creatively, communicate effectively, and build trust.

What to Expect

At Loft 147, teams might start the day with an icebreaker game to get everyone engaged and comfortable. They'll then move into structured teamwork activities like scavenger hunts, problem-solving challenges, or themed escape rooms. The experienced facilitators keep the energy high and ensure each exercise has a clear purpose and takeaway.

Who It's For

Loft 147's team building programs are well-suited for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen interpersonal bonds and hone essential collaboration skills. The combination of hands-on activities and guided reflection makes it an ideal choice for corporate offsites, department retreats, or any occasion when a group needs to come together and level up.
