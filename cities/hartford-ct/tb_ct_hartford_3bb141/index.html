Connecticut Convention Center

4.5★ (2215 reviews)

Connecticut Convention Center transforms its expansive Hartford venue into a structured environment for facilitated workshops and corporate retreats. The facility's professional meeting spaces and experienced planning staff create focused sessions where teams tackle strategic challenges, communication exercises, or skill-building activities. Multiple breakout rooms allow for small group rotations followed by large group debriefs in the main auditorium spaces.

What to Expect

Groups work with professional facilitators who guide teams through customized workshops tailored to specific organizational goals. Sessions typically combine interactive exercises, group discussions, and hands-on activities across multiple spaces within the center. The structured format includes planned breaks and meals in the banquet areas, creating natural networking opportunities between formal activities.

Who It's For

Ideal for medium to large corporate groups (30-200 people) planning serious strategic retreats or professional development sessions. The formal convention setting works particularly well for organizations that need to maintain a professional atmosphere while building stronger working relationships.
