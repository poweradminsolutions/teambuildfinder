Bond Ballroom

4.5★ (228 reviews)

The Bond Ballroom in Hartford, CT offers a unique and engaging team building experience with its facilitated workshops and retreats. This historic venue provides a dynamic setting for groups to come together, challenge themselves, and strengthen their bonds through a variety of interactive activities.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be greeted by the Bond Ballroom's experienced facilitators who will guide you through a tailored program designed to meet your team's specific goals. Expect a blend of hands-on workshops, problem-solving exercises, and team challenges that encourage collaboration, communication, and creative thinking.

Who It's For

The Bond Ballroom is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to break out of the traditional meeting format and engage in a memorable team building experience. The versatile space and diverse activity options make it suitable for a wide range of occasions, from corporate retreats to department offsites.
