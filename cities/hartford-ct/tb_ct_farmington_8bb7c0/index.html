Winding Trails

4.7★ (322 reviews)

Winding Trails in Farmington, CT is an oasis of natural beauty where teams can connect and challenge themselves through a variety of outdoor activities. With miles of hiking trails and scenic landscapes, this recreation center provides the perfect setting for groups to step away from the office and bond over shared experiences in nature.

What to Expect

When your team arrives at Winding Trails, they'll be greeted by knowledgeable guides who will lead them on customized adventures. From navigating a high ropes course to scavenger hunts through the woods, the activities encourage teamwork, communication, and a healthy dose of friendly competition.

Who It's For

Winding Trails is ideal for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to strengthen camaraderie and trust through physical, problem-solving challenges. The natural environment and variety of activities make this an excellent choice for corporate retreats, leadership development, and team building events.
