BATL Grounds | Plaza Midwood

4.9★ (1257 reviews)

BATL Grounds transforms the ancient sport of axe throwing into a surprisingly strategic team building experience in Charlotte's trendy Plaza Midwood district. Groups learn proper throwing techniques from trained coaches before competing in tournaments that blend individual skill with team collaboration. The primal satisfaction of hitting bullseyes creates an instant bonding experience that's hard to replicate in traditional corporate settings.

What to Expect

Your team receives safety briefings and throwing instruction before practicing on individual lanes with regulation axes and wooden targets. Sessions typically progress from basic technique drills to competitive games like tic-tac-toe and knockout tournaments. Coaches keep score, offer tips, and facilitate team challenges that get progressively more engaging as everyone's accuracy improves.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-20 who want something memorably different from the usual team outing. The combination of learning a new skill together and friendly competition works especially well for groups that don't interact daily, as the novelty gives everyone common ground regardless of their office hierarchy.
