K1 Speed - Indoor Go Karts, Corporate Event Venue, Team Building Activities

4.4★ (2348 reviews)

K1 Speed transforms corporate team building into a high-octane racing experience at their Concord facility, where groups compete in electric go-karts that level the playing field regardless of driving experience. The indoor track eliminates weather concerns while creating an adrenaline-fueled environment that naturally encourages friendly competition and team bonding.

What to Expect

Teams receive safety briefings before hitting the track in electric karts that reach exciting speeds without requiring racing expertise. Multiple race formats allow groups to compete head-to-head or in relay-style team challenges, with timing systems tracking performance and creating natural scoreboard drama. The venue handles logistics from equipment to race coordination, letting organizers focus on cheering from the sidelines.

Who It's For

Ideal for competitive groups of 10-50 people who want an energizing alternative to traditional team activities. The universal appeal of racing works especially well for mixed departments or client entertainment where you need an instantly engaging icebreaker.
