Corolla Adventure Park

4.9★ (667 reviews) 2 hours

Corolla Adventure Park in Corolla, North Carolina, offers a thrilling team-building experience like no other. With its heart-pumping ropes courses and high-speed go-karts, this adventure hub challenges groups to work together, push their limits, and create lasting memories.

What to Expect

Teams can tackle the park's extensive ropes and obstacle courses, navigating challenging elements high above the ground and relying on communication and trust to get them through. For a more adrenaline-fueled activity, groups can race against the clock and each other on the state-of-the-art go-kart track, putting their driving skills to the test.

Who It's For

Corolla Adventure Park is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a physically engaging, competitive team-building adventure. The shared experience of conquering the park's obstacles and challenges tends to break down barriers between departments and foster camaraderie unlike any happy hour or trust fall.
