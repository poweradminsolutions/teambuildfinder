Alexander Homestead by Wedgewood Weddings

4.8★ (518 reviews)

Located in the heart of Charlotte, North Carolina, the Alexander Homestead by Wedgewood Weddings offers an idyllic setting for team building activities and retreats. With its stunning 19th-century architecture and lush gardens, this historic venue provides a serene and inspiring backdrop for groups to connect, collaborate, and push their boundaries.

What to Expect

Wedgewood Weddings' expert facilitators lead a variety of engaging workshops and team exercises at the Alexander Homestead. Groups can participate in hands-on activities like guided nature hikes, interactive problem-solving challenges, or charitable service projects, all designed to foster communication, trust, and camaraderie within the team.

Who It's For

The Alexander Homestead is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (20-40 people) seeking a unique, memorable team building experience. Whether planning a company retreat, leadership development session, or simply a fun day of bonding, this versatile venue offers a perfect blend of history, nature, and structured team activities.
