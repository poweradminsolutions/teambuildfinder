Queen City Social Space

4.8★ (134 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Charlotte, North Carolina, Queen City Social Space provides a unique and dynamic setting for team building activities. This versatile venue offers a range of facilitated workshops, retreats, and volunteer/charity events that are designed to bring groups together, foster collaboration, and inspire personal growth.

What to Expect

When your team steps into Queen City Social Space, they'll be greeted by a modern, open-concept layout that encourages interaction and experimentation. The experienced facilitators guide participants through a variety of interactive exercises and challenges that promote problem-solving, communication, and camaraderie. Whether it's an escape room challenge or a hands-on service project, the activities are tailored to your group's goals and needs.

Who It's For

Queen City Social Space is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) seeking a transformative team building experience. The diverse range of activities and the collaborative nature of the space make it ideal for companies looking to strengthen cross-departmental bonds, boost morale, and cultivate a stronger sense of community.
