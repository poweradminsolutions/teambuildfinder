Beanstalk Ziplines

4.7★ (164 reviews)

Perched in the Blue Ridge Mountains near Morganton, Beanstalk Ziplines invites teams to soar through the treetops on a thrilling ropes course and zipline adventure. With 14 different ziplines and 45 ropes elements, this outdoor playground challenges groups to work together, build trust, and conquer their fears.

What to Expect

Groups start on the ground, tackling a series of team-based obstacles and challenges that build cooperation and problem-solving skills. Once comfortable, they'll clip into harnesses and ascend 60 feet into the canopy, traversing wobbly bridges, cargo nets, and other high-altitude elements. The grand finale is a half-mile long zipline that sends participants racing through the forest at speeds up to 40 mph.

Who It's For

Beanstalk Ziplines is ideal for teams of 10-50 looking to push their boundaries and foster camaraderie. The physical nature of the activities encourages communication, leadership, and healthy competition - qualities that translate directly back to the workplace.
