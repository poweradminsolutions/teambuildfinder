Ground Level Legacy Center

4.9★ (46 reviews)

Nestled in the heart of Pineville, North Carolina, the Ground Level Legacy Center offers a unique and transformative team building experience. This versatile event venue specializes in immersive retreats and facilitated workshops that challenge groups to work together, build trust, and create lasting memories.

What to Expect

When teams arrive, they're greeted by a modern, flexible event space designed to foster collaboration and creativity. Depending on the package, groups may engage in problem-solving exercises, outdoor team challenges, or guided discussions led by experienced facilitators. The focus is on creating an engaging, high-energy atmosphere that brings people together through shared experiences.

Who It's For

The Ground Level Legacy Center is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 participants) looking to strengthen bonds and improve communication. The combination of thought-provoking activities and a supportive, distraction-free environment makes this venue ideal for corporate offsites, nonprofit retreats, and other group events seeking to build cohesion and inspire lasting change.
