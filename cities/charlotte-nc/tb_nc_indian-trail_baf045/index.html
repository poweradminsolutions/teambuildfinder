Cedar Oak Farm & C.O.F. Pottery

5.0★ (12 reviews)

Nestled in the charming town of Indian Trail, North Carolina, Cedar Oak Farm & C.O.F. Pottery offers a truly unique team building experience. This working farm and art studio provides hands-on workshops in pottery making and other creative pursuits, perfect for groups looking to tap into their artistic sides.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your team will be guided through the process of creating their own pottery piece, from shaping the clay to decorating the final product. The experienced instructors will provide all the tools and materials, ensuring a smooth and engaging creative journey for even first-time potters.

Who It's For

Cedar Oak Farm is an excellent choice for teams of 10 to 30 looking to step away from the office and explore their collective creativity. The pottery workshops foster collaboration, communication, and a sense of shared accomplishment that translates back to the workplace.
