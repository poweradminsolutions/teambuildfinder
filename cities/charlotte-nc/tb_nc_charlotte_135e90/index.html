Comedy Arts Theater of Charlotte

4.9★ (102 reviews) 1 hour to 1.5 hours

The Comedy Arts Theater of Charlotte is a must-visit destination for teams seeking a unique and laughter-filled team building experience. Located in the heart of North Carolina's largest city, this improv theater offers a truly interactive and engaging performance that will have your group bonding over shared giggles and a newfound appreciation for the art of comedy.

What to Expect

When your team visits the Comedy Arts Theater, they'll be immersed in a 90-minute improv workshop led by experienced comedic performers. Together, your group will participate in a series of fun and fast-paced exercises designed to spark creativity, build communication skills, and encourage out-of-the-box thinking - all while laughing the entire time.

Who It's For

This activity is perfect for medium-sized teams (10-30 people) looking to break the ice, boost morale, and have a blast in the process. The inclusive, judgment-free nature of improv makes it an ideal team building activity, as coworkers of all comfort levels can feel empowered to step outside their comfort zones and embrace their inner comedians.
