The Ruth by Beau Monde

4.9★ (103 reviews)

The Ruth by Beau Monde in Charlotte, NC offers a unique and engaging team building experience for groups of all sizes. With a focus on facilitated workshops, retreats, and innovative activities like design thinking challenges and hackathons, this venue provides a dynamic environment for teams to collaborate, develop new skills, and bond over shared experiences.

What to Expect

When you visit The Ruth, your team will be guided through a curated itinerary tailored to your group's goals. Sessions may involve personality assessments, interactive workshops, or hands-on projects that encourage creative problem-solving and effective communication. The flow of the day is designed to keep participants energized and engaged throughout.

Who It's For

The Ruth is an excellent choice for mid-size teams (15-40 people) seeking a team building experience with a bit of an edge. The diverse activity options cater to groups looking to tackle DEI initiatives, boost innovation, or simply strengthen interpersonal connections through shared challenges and triumphs.
