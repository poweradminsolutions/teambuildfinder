Fairmount Park Horticulture Center

4.6★ (456 reviews)

The Fairmount Park Horticulture Center transforms team building into an immersive experience where groups can lead environmental restoration projects, participate in guided botanical workshops, or design collaborative art installations using natural materials. Philadelphia's premier greenhouse venue combines indoor garden spaces with expansive outdoor grounds, creating unique opportunities for teams to work together while making a tangible positive impact on the local ecosystem.

What to Expect

Teams typically start with a brief orientation about sustainable gardening practices, then dive into hands-on activities like planting native species, creating pollinator gardens, or building habitat structures. Art-focused sessions involve crafting sculptures or installations using reclaimed materials and botanical elements, while environmental cleanup activities extend into the surrounding Fairmount Park trails and waterways.

Who It's For

Ideal for environmentally conscious organizations and teams of 20-80 people who want meaningful work beyond typical corporate activities. The combination of physical labor, creative problem-solving, and visible results makes this particularly effective for cross-departmental groups looking to build genuine connections.
