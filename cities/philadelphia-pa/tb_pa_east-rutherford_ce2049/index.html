DreamWorks Water Park

4.3★ (6415 reviews) 2 hours

DreamWorks Water Park transforms team building into a movie-themed aquatic adventure at American Dream in East Rutherford. Your group can bond while conquering waterslides, floating together down the lazy river, or competing in wave pool challenges. The indoor setting means weather never cancels your plans, while the DreamWorks theming adds a playful element that encourages even the most reserved colleagues to let loose.

What to Expect

Teams arrive to a massive indoor water park featuring multiple waterslides, a wave pool, lazy river, and themed play areas. Groups typically split time between high-energy slide competitions and relaxed floating sessions, with plenty of opportunities for organic team bonding in between activities. The movie-themed environment provides natural conversation starters and photo opportunities throughout the experience.

Who It's For

Ideal for mid to large teams (20-60 people) wanting an unconventional team building experience that breaks professional barriers quickly. The casual water park setting naturally levels the playing field between different departments and seniority levels.
