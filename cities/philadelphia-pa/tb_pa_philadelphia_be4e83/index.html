National Society of Colonial Dames

4.8★ (25 reviews)

Located in historic Philadelphia, the National Society of Colonial Dames offers immersive workshops and facilitated retreats that transport teams back in time. Explore the city's rich heritage and bond over shared colonial experiences in this unique, educational setting.

What to Expect

Groups can expect hands-on activities like candle-making, colonial-era games, and guided tours of the Society's elegant 18th-century mansion. The friendly staff leads engaging discussions on topics like early American history and etiquette, fostering camaraderie and a deeper appreciation for our nation's past.

Who It's For

This is an excellent option for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to step away from the office and dive into Philadelphia's storied colonial legacy. The collaborative, interactive nature of the workshops makes them ideal for promoting unity and strengthening interpersonal bonds.
