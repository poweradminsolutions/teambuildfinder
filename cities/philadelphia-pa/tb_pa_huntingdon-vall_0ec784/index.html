Create Me Pottery

4.9★ (148 reviews)

Create Me Pottery in Huntingdon Valley, PA provides a unique team building experience for groups looking to tap into their creative sides. Spend an afternoon immersed in the art of pottery-making, learning professional techniques from skilled instructors while bonding with your colleagues.

What to Expect

Groups start by designing their own custom pottery pieces, then move to the wheel to put their skills to the test. Instructors guide participants through every step, from centering the clay to shaping and smoothing their creations. The shared creative challenge encourages teamwork and problem-solving in a relaxed, supportive environment.

Who It's For

Create Me Pottery is perfect for teams of 10-25 looking to break out of the conference room. The hands-on, artsy atmosphere is a refreshing change of pace from typical team building activities, making it ideal for encouraging cross-department collaboration and boosting morale.
