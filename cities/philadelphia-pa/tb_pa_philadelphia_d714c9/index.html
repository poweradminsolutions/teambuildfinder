BLDG39 at the Arsenal

4.8★ (58 reviews) up to 5 hours

BLDG39 at the Arsenal in Philadelphia offers a unique team-building experience with its facilitated workshops and retreats. Housed in a historic industrial building, this venue provides a creative and inspiring setting for groups to collaborate, problem-solve, and strengthen their bonds.

What to Expect

Depending on the program, groups might take part in interactive exercises, creative challenges, or guided discussions. The experienced facilitators tailor each session to the specific needs and goals of the team, ensuring an engaging and productive experience.

Who It's For

BLDG39 is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to step away from the office and try something a little different. The hands-on, immersive activities encourage open communication, critical thinking, and a healthy dose of friendly competition, making it a perfect fit for team-building events or corporate retreats.
