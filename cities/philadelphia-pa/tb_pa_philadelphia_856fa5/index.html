Vaux Studio | Philadelphia's Premier Micro-Wedding Venue

4.9★ (57 reviews)

Vaux Studio in the heart of Philadelphia offers a unique micro-wedding venue that doubles as a premier team building destination. With a focus on facilitated workshops and off-site retreats, this urban oasis provides a refreshingly intimate setting for groups to step away from the everyday and engage in immersive, collaborative experiences.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, groups are greeted by Vaux Studio's dedicated event staff, who guide them through a range of tailored team building activities. From fast-paced problem-solving challenges to reflective outdoor mindfulness sessions, the flexible event spaces and expert facilitation ensure an engaging, productive time for all participants.

Who It's For

Vaux Studio's team building experiences are well-suited for mid-size groups of 15-40 looking to strengthen bonds, improve communication, and foster a greater sense of camaraderie. The hands-on, interactive nature of the activities makes this an excellent choice for companies, nonprofits, and other organizations seeking a memorable team retreat in the city.
