Events On The Ave

5.0★ (74 reviews) $$ 1-3 hours

Events On The Ave in Philadelphia, PA is a dynamic event space that provides immersive team building experiences perfect for groups seeking to strengthen bonds and spark collaboration. From facilitated workshops to full-day retreats, the versatile venue and experienced staff create customized activities to challenge participants and foster meaningful connections.

What to Expect

When you book an event at Events On The Ave, your group can expect a seamless, professionally-facilitated experience. Whether it's an escape room-style challenge, a cooking competition, or an outdoor adventure, the team building activities are designed to encourage problem-solving, communication, and friendly rivalry in a safe, supportive environment.

Who It's For

Events On The Ave is an excellent choice for mid-sized teams (15-40 people) looking to break out of the office and engage in some friendly competition. The diverse range of programming makes it suitable for a variety of occasions, from new hire orientations to departmental bonding sessions.
