City Food Tours Philadelphia

4.9★ (1194 reviews)

City Food Tours Philadelphia transforms team building into a culinary adventure through the city's historic neighborhoods and legendary food scene. Teams explore together on guided walking tours that combine storytelling, local history, and tastings at authentic eateries from Reading Terminal Market to South Philly cheesesteak joints. The shared discovery of hidden gems and local flavors creates natural conversation and connection among colleagues.

What to Expect

Groups follow an experienced local guide through 3-4 carefully selected food stops over 2-3 hours, sampling signature dishes while learning about Philadelphia's culinary traditions and neighborhood histories. Everyone walks together between locations, with plenty of time for team members to chat and share reactions to each tasting. Tours typically cover 6-8 blocks at a comfortable pace with stops for photos and stories.

Who It's For

Ideal for teams of 8-25 who want something more engaging than a typical dinner out. The walking format and shared food experiences work particularly well for groups looking to build relationships in a relaxed, authentic setting.
