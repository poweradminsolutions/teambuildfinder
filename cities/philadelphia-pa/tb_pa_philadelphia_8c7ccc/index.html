Yay Clay

4.9★ (742 reviews)

Yay Clay transforms team building into hands-on artistry through guided pottery workshops that get everyone's hands dirty in the best possible way. This Philadelphia pottery studio and gallery creates a relaxed creative environment where colleagues can chat naturally while learning wheel throwing or hand-building techniques. The combination of focused concentration and playful experimentation makes for surprisingly meaningful team connections.

What to Expect

Groups gather around pottery wheels or work tables where instructors demonstrate basic techniques before everyone dives into their own clay projects. Teams can choose between wheel throwing sessions where they'll attempt bowls or mugs, or hand-building workshops focused on sculpting and decorative pieces. Finished pieces get fired in the studio's kilns and can be picked up later as lasting mementos of the experience.

Who It's For

Ideal for smaller teams of 8-20 people who want something genuinely different from typical corporate activities. The meditative nature of working with clay naturally encourages authentic conversations while keeping hands busy, making it particularly effective for groups that might feel awkward in more structured icebreaker settings.
