The King and the Flower

5.0★ (10 reviews) 90 minutes

Located in Peoria, Arizona, The King and the Flower offers a unique team building experience through their engaging craft workshops. Participants will have the opportunity to unleash their creative side and bond over the shared experience of designing and molding their own pottery pieces.

What to Expect

Groups are guided through a 2-hour pottery class where they'll learn various techniques, from hand-building to wheel throwing. Under the instruction of skilled artisans, teams will craft their own one-of-a-kind creations, from mugs and bowls to decorative vases and sculptures.

Who It's For

This activity is perfect for teams looking to step away from the office and explore their artistic talents together. Whether planning a company offsite or seeking a fun team bonding experience, The King and the Flower's pottery workshops are an excellent choice for groups of 10-30 people.
