Venture X Chandler Office Space

5.0★ (14 reviews)

Venture X in Chandler, AZ provides a modern, flexible workspace perfect for team building workshops and offsite retreats. With high-tech conference rooms, a fully-stocked café, and a comfortable lounge area, this coworking hub has everything teams need to collaborate, bond, and boost productivity.

What to Expect

Groups can choose from facilitated workshops or use the space for their own offsite retreat. Facilitators lead interactive exercises and team challenges that encourage communication, problem-solving, and friendly competition. Ample breakout rooms allow teams to tackle tasks, then reconvene in the light-filled common areas.

Who It's For

Venture X is an ideal venue for mid-size to large teams (15-50 people) looking to level up their teamwork. The modern, tech-enabled spaces create an energizing backdrop for team building, whether you're planning a half-day workshop or a multi-day offsite.
