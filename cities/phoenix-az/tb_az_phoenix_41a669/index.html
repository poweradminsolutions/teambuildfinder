Play Lab PHX

4.9★ (48 reviews) $$ 30 or 60 minutes

Nestled in the heart of Phoenix, Play Lab PHX is a one-of-a-kind destination for team building. This immersive entertainment hub offers a thrilling mix of virtual reality experiences, laser tag, and video game challenges that are sure to bring your group closer together.

What to Expect

At Play Lab PHX, your team will be transported into dynamic virtual worlds, working together to solve puzzles and navigate high-stakes scenarios. The laser tag arena provides a physical, adrenaline-fueled experience where colleagues can bond over friendly competition. Between adventures, you can refuel with drinks and snacks at the on-site cafe.

Who It's For

Whether you're planning a corporate outing, a birthday celebration, or a unique date night, Play Lab PHX is an excellent choice for teams of 2-6 people. The variety of activities and customizable packages make it easy to find the perfect fit for your group's interests and needs.
