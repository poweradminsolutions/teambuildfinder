Arizona Outdoor Fun Adventures & Tours - ATV / UTV Tours & Rentals

4.8★ (2293 reviews)

Arizona Outdoor Fun Adventures transforms corporate groups into off-road explorers through guided ATV and UTV expeditions across New River's rugged desert landscape. Teams navigate challenging terrain together, from rocky washes to scenic ridgelines, while professional guides ensure safety and provide insights about local wildlife and geology. The combination of adrenaline, natural beauty, and shared adventure creates lasting bonds that translate back to the workplace.

What to Expect

Groups receive safety briefings and basic vehicle operation training before setting out on multi-hour guided tours through desert trails. Teams drive their own ATVs or share UTVs while following experienced guides who adjust routes based on skill level and weather conditions. Tours include photo stops at scenic overlooks and opportunities to spot desert wildlife, with shooting range activities available as add-on experiences.

Who It's For

Ideal for adventure-seeking teams of 8-25 people who want an authentic Arizona experience beyond typical conference rooms. The shared thrill of conquering challenging terrain together builds trust and camaraderie that carries over into collaborative work projects.
