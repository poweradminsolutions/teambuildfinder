Antigua Garden

4.6★ (139 reviews)

Tucked away in the heart of Phoenix, Antigua Garden offers a serene and enchanting setting for team building retreats and workshops. With its lush greenery, tranquil ponds, and Mediterranean-inspired architecture, this unique venue provides an inspiring backdrop for groups to connect, collaborate, and rejuvenate.

What to Expect

At Antigua Garden, team building activities are tailored to the specific needs and goals of each group. Facilitators lead interactive workshops that encourage problem-solving, communication, and leadership development through a blend of indoor and outdoor experiences. Groups may find themselves navigating team challenges, participating in creative exercises, or even engaging in friendly competitions that foster camaraderie.

Who It's For

Antigua Garden is an exceptional choice for mid-size teams (15-40 participants) seeking a memorable team building experience. The natural, retreat-like setting and customizable programming make it an ideal destination for companies looking to strengthen interpersonal bonds, boost morale, and unlock the full potential of their workforce.
