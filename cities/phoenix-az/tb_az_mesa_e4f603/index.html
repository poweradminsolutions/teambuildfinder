Phoenix BBQ and Grilling School

4.2★ (30 reviews)

Located in the heart of Mesa, Arizona, the Phoenix BBQ and Grilling School offers a unique team-building experience that's perfect for groups looking to bond over the sizzle of the grill. This hands-on culinary adventure takes teams through the art of barbecue, from expertly seasoning meat to mastering the perfect smoky char.

What to Expect

Upon arrival, your group will be greeted by the school's passionate instructors who will guide you through a interactive cooking class. Together, you'll tackle the techniques of grilling, smoking, and plating mouthwatering barbecue dishes. It's a fun, collaborative challenge that gets everyone working in sync towards a delicious goal.

Who It's For

The Phoenix BBQ and Grilling School is an excellent choice for medium-sized teams (12-30 people) seeking a memorable, teamwork-focused activity. The shared experience of mastering the grill tends to break down barriers and foster camaraderie in a way that's much more engaging than a typical happy hour.
