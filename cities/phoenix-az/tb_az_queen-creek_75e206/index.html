Pecan Lake Entertainment

4.2★ (854 reviews)

Pecan Lake Entertainment transforms team building into high-energy competition across multiple attractions in Queen Creek. Teams can tackle escape rooms that demand collaboration under pressure, then shift gears to go-kart racing where colleagues become rivals on the track. The variety lets groups mix problem-solving challenges with pure adrenaline rushes.

What to Expect

Your group rotates through different activities based on your package choice. Escape rooms typically run 60 minutes with teams of 4-8 people working together to solve interconnected puzzles before time expires. The go-kart sessions put individuals behind the wheel for timed races, creating natural tournament brackets and plenty of friendly trash talk.

Who It's For

Ideal for energetic teams of 12-30 who want variety in their team building experience. The mix of mental challenges and physical competition appeals to different personality types, making it especially effective for cross-departmental groups.
