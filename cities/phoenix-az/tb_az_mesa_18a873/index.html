Paragon Escape Games

5.0★ (497 reviews) $$ 60-75 minutes

Paragon Escape Games in Mesa, Arizona offers thrilling, immersive escape room adventures that challenge teams to think creatively and work together under pressure. With custom-designed puzzles and engaging storylines, these 60-minute experiences are a unique way to build camaraderie and boost problem-solving skills.

What to Expect

When your group arrives, you'll be ushered into a themed escape room and given a mission to complete before time runs out. You'll need to search for hidden clues, solve mind-bending puzzles, and strategize as a team to make your daring escape. It's an adrenaline-filled test of wits that encourages communication, critical thinking, and creative collaboration.

Who It's For

Paragon Escape Games is an excellent choice for team building exercises, corporate retreats, and holiday parties. The small group size (4-10 players) fosters tight-knit teamwork, while the high-energy challenges are sure to bring your group closer together.
