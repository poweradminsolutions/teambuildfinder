Epic Party Team

4.9★ (247 reviews)

Epic Party Team in Phoenix, AZ is a one-stop shop for truly epic team building experiences. From high-energy scavenger hunts that get everyone on their feet to mind-bending trivia nights and facilitated workshops, their skilled event coordinators will custom-craft an unforgettable outing for your group.

What to Expect

When you book with Epic Party Team, you can expect a seamless, professionally-executed event. They'll work with you to understand your group's needs and tailor the experience accordingly, whether that's a fast-paced, competitive scavenger hunt or a collaborative problem-solving session. Activities are designed to get people interacting, thinking creatively, and building trust.

Who It's For

Epic Party Team's offerings are perfect for mid-size teams of 15-40 looking to break out of their day-to-day routines and bond over shared challenges. The activities are energetic and fun, creating an ideal environment for colleagues to get to know each other better and strengthen workplace relationships.
