Simply Smashing "a Rage Release Room"

4.6★ (708 reviews) $$$ 25-45 minutes

Simply Smashing transforms team stress relief into a memorable group experience through rage rooms where colleagues safely demolish objects together. This Tempe venue provides protective gear and designated destruction zones where small teams can literally break things while bonding over the shared cathartic release.

What to Expect

Your group receives safety equipment and enters a controlled environment stocked with breakable items like dishes, electronics, and furniture. Armed with sledgehammers, baseball bats, and other tools, team members take turns or work together to destroy objects while music plays in the background. Sessions are timed and supervised for safety.

Who It's For

Ideal for tight-knit teams of 2-4 people who want an unconventional stress release activity. The intense shared experience creates strong bonds and works especially well for groups dealing with high-pressure work environments or major project completions.
