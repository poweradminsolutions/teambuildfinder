Boston Tea Party Ships & Museum

Boston, MA 4.6★ (9275 reviews)

Step aboard authentic replica ships and immerse your team in the pivotal moments that sparked the American Revolution. This floating museum in Boston combines interactive theater with hands-on history, letting groups participate in a reenactment of the famous tea protest while exploring multimedia exhibits that bring 1773 to life.

Gore Place

Waltham, MA 4.6★ (310 reviews)

Gore Place in Waltham, MA offers a unique team building experience that transports groups back in time. Explore the grounds of this Federal-period mansion and estate, learning about its history as you navigate challenges and scavenger hunts designed to foster collaboration and problem-solving.

Onondaga Historical Association

Syracuse, NY 4.8★ (44 reviews)

The Onondaga Historical Association in Syracuse, NY offers a one-of-a-kind opportunity to dive into the rich history and culture of this vibrant city. With a vast collection of archival photos, maps, and records, this museum provides a truly immersive experience for teams looking to connect with the past and gain a deeper understanding of their local roots.

Mansion On James

Syracuse, NY 3.8★ (19 reviews)

Housed in a stunning 19th-century mansion, the Mansion on James in Syracuse, NY offers a unique historical experience that's perfect for team building. Groups can explore the meticulously restored interior, learn about the property's rich past, and participate in guided workshops that bring the mansion's legacy to life.
