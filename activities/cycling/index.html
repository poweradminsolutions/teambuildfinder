Pocono Whitewater Rafting

Jim Thorpe, PA 4.6★ (1615 reviews)

Pocono Whitewater Rafting transforms team dynamics through shared adventure on the Lehigh River rapids near Jim Thorpe. Groups navigate Class I-III whitewater together while guides ensure safety and maximize the bonding experience. The outfitter also offers mountain biking trails and paintball scenarios for teams wanting multi-activity packages.

Cliff Creek Outfitters Tours and Rentals

Tempe, AZ 4.9★ (977 reviews)

Cliff Creek Outfitters transforms group cycling into strategic team building adventures through the stunning Sonoran Desert terrain around Tempe. Their guided bike tours combine navigation challenges with collaborative problem-solving as teams work together to complete desert routes and outdoor objectives.
